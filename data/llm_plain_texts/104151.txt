tldr: This article presents two case studies of patients with internal cancers. The first patient, Bartholomew Collins, experienced pain and discomfort before being diagnosed with a cancerous tumor. The second patient, Thomas Trinder, had a history of wrestling injuries and was found to have a tumor in his groin. Both patients underwent dissection after their deaths, revealing the extent of their cancerous growths and the affected organs. The article provides a detailed account of their medical histories, symptoms, and treatments, as well as the findings from the dissections.
