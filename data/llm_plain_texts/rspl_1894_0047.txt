tldr: The Incorporated Kew Committee reports on the operations and findings of Kew Observatory for the year 1893. The committee discusses various observations and measurements, including magnetic disturbances, meteorological data, and instrument maintenance. They also mention the continuous registration of atmospheric pressure, temperature, wind, sunshine, and rain, as well as the analysis of solar activity and time signals.
