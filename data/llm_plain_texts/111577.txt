tldr: This study examines the vertebral characters of the Pterosauria order, specifically the genera Pterodactylus and Dimorphodon. The author, Professor Owen, investigates species from the Lias period to the upper green-sand, discovering the earliest known instance of the "proccelian" type in the reptilian class. The paper includes numerous illustrations of Pterodactyle fossils.
