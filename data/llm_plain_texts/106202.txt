tldr: The author examines the population growth and health conditions in Manchester and its surrounding areas. They found a disparity in the healthiness of urban areas compared to the surrounding countryside. The author discusses factors contributing to poor health in cities, such as luxury, irregularity, and intemperance, as well as the impact of confinement, uncleanliness, and foul air on the duration of life. The author also highlights the importance of addressing these issues to improve the overall health and well-being of urban populations.
