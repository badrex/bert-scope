tldr: The author examines the internal structure of the human brain and compares it to the brains of various animals, such as fish, insects, and worms, using a microscope. They observe differences in brain structure and organization, including the presence of globules, elastic matter, and fluid, as well as the arrangement of nerves and ganglions. This research contributes to the understanding of the nervous system and its functions in different organisms.
