tldr: The Brazilian project aimed to test the use of communication satellites in education, focusing on using satellite technology to improve educational opportunities for students in the country's interior. The project involved a pilot experiment in the Brazilian northeast, where television and radio transmissions, along with printed materials, were used to educate over a thousand teachers and many thousands of primary-level students in 500 schools. The project aimed to test the efficiency of educational programming using various resources, develop techniques for producing educational content, and improve conditions to better meet the needs of the students and teachers involved.
