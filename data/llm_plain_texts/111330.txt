tldr: The authors conducted a detailed chemical analysis and distillation of Burmese naphtha, a natural substance found in the kingdom of Burmah. They discovered that it contains various volatile and solid constituents, including organic bases, hydrocarbons, and nitro-compounds. The authors also found that distillation, using steam and various acids, could separate and purify these components, providing valuable insights into the composition of Burmese naphtha.
