tldr: This article presents research on solar physics, focusing on the heliographical positions and areas of sunspots observed with the Kew Photoheliograph during the years 1862 and 1863. The authors describe their methods for measuring and reducing the data collected, providing a detailed account of the techniques used. The research aims to determine the heliographical coordinates of sunspots and improve our understanding of the sun's astronomical elements.
