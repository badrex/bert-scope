tldr: This article presents a study of the Earth's magnetic field in Southern Africa at the epoch of 1930-50. The author utilized previous magnetic data from various sources and conducted observational work to determine the corrections needed for the secular change that occurred in the region. The study provides valuable information for marine and aerial navigation, as well as for the survey departments of the governments in the region.
