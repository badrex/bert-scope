tldr: The author presents a collection of zoological and petrological specimens gathered from Kerguelen Island during the Transit of Venus Expeditions in 1874-75. The collection includes various sponge species, some of which are common to European and Mediterranean regions, while others are unique to Antarctic waters. The author also discusses the geological context of the island and the various habitats from which the specimens were collected.
