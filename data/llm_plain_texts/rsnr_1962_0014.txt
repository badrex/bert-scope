tldr: This article celebrates the life and work of Sir Frederick Gowland Hopkins, a pioneer in the field of biochemistry who is best known for his discovery of vitamins. Hopkins' work revolutionized the understanding of biochemical processes within living cells, and his vision of the cell as a chemical factory laid the foundation for modern biochemistry. His research on vitamins and their importance in human nutrition has had a lasting impact on the field.
