tldr: Sir John Barrow's letter discusses a series of magnetic observations made on land and on board the ships 'Erebus' and 'Terror' under Captain James Clark Ross's direction. The letter also includes a series of ocean temperature and specific gravity measurements at various depths and the surface. These observations were conducted up to December 31, 1839, and were transmitted to the Royal Society by the Lords Commissioners of the Admiralty.
