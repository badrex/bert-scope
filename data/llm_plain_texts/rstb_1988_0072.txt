tldr: The article describes the discovery and analysis of a new kannemeyeriid dicynodont species, Wadiasaurus indicus, from the Middle Triassic Yerrapalli Formation in India. The study provides a detailed description of the osteology of W. indicus, based on new and previously collected specimens, and discusses its possible relationships with other kannemeyeriid genera. The taphonomy of the bone assemblage and the probable lifestyle of the animal are also investigated.
