tldr: This article discusses the effects of World War I on the organization and development of science and technology in the United Kingdom. It highlights the country's reliance on German industries, particularly in the field of synthetic dyestuffs, and the subsequent shift in focus to develop a strong domestic science and technology sector. The article also examines the national response to the war, including the involvement of scientists and the formation of committees to coordinate war work and address shortages of resources.
