tldr: The author provides a detailed account of the quicksilver mines in Friuli, Italy, including their location, geological features, and extraction methods. He describes the two types of quicksilver produced at the mines, virgin-mercury and plain quicksilver, and explains how they are separated and processed. The author also discusses the challenges faced by miners, such as the presence of mercury in their bodies and the lack of noxious or innocent apparitions, contrary to popular beliefs.
