tldr: The author investigates the function of the sound-post and the proportional thickness of violin strings. They found that the sound-post plays a crucial role in supporting the belly of the violin and transmitting vibrations to the back, influencing the instrument's tone quality. The position of the sound-post and the quality of the wood used also affect the tone of the violin.
