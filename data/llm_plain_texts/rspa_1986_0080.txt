tldr: This article discusses the importance and challenges of prediction in economic theory. It highlights the tension between prediction and prescription in economics and examines the techniques used to cope with difficulties in predicting human behavior and the results of interactions between millions of individuals in various economic settings. The article also critiques the use of concepts like equilibrium, rationality, and maximization in traditional economic analysis.
