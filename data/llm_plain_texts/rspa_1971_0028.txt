tldr: The article discusses the Autokon system, a computer-aided design and numerical control system used in the shipbuilding industry. The system uses a data bank concept, Autokon language, and design rules to streamline the design and engineering process, reducing the amount of specifications needed and increasing efficiency. The second generation Autokon is a semi-automatic solution that maintains human control while utilizing the computer for routine tasks.
