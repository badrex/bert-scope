It discusses magnetic observations, meteorology, seismology, atmospheric electricity, and platinum thermometry. The Magnetographs were in constant operation, and the magnetic elements for the entire year were analyzed. The electrograph functioned well, and the International scheme of balloon ascents was initiated. The report also includes details on inspections, seismograph operations, fog and mist observations, and the performance of various instruments.
