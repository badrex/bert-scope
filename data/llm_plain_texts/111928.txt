tldr: The Earl of Rosse shares his experiences and observations in constructing large telescopes, focusing on the casting and manufacturing processes of 6-foot aperture specula. He also discusses the limitations of telescopic vision, the mounting of large telescopes, and the observations made during six years of use. The paper highlights the discovery of new nebulae with curved or spiral branches, as well as double and multiple nebulae.
