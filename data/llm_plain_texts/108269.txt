tldr: The author conducted a series of experiments to study the effects of crop rotation on soil fertility and nutrient availability. They found that the soil's chemical composition remained consistent throughout the experiments, debunking the theory that soil becomes contaminated by excretions from preceding crops. The author also analyzed the composition of various crops and their impact on soil nutrients, providing valuable insights into the benefits of crop rotation for sustainable agriculture.
