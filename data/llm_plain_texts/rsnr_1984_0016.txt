tldr: 'G. Herbert Fowler was a pioneering oceanographer who made significant contributions to marine science in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Despite facing numerous challenges, Fowler worked to improve collaboration between scientists, standardize equipment, and advocate for better research facilities. His research on midwater fauna and plankton helped shape the field of oceanography and laid the groundwork for future advancements in marine science.'
