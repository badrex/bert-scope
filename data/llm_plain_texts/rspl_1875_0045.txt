tldr: The author describes the structure and function of the Organ of Corti, a crucial part of the mammalian cochlea responsible for hearing. The Organ of Corti consists of rods, hair cells, nuclear cells, supporting cells, and two unique membranes. The author also explains the development and function of these components, as well as the differences in structure between various mammals.
