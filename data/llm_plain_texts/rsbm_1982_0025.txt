tldr: Carrington Bonsor Williams was a renowned entomologist who made significant contributions to the study of insect pests, biological control, and butterfly migration. He worked in various locations, including the John Innes Horticultural Institution, the West Indies, Egypt, and Tanganyika, where he conducted research on insect behavior, control measures, and the impact of climate on insect survival. His work on butterfly migration and the study of insect pests helped improve agricultural practices and protect crops.
