From his early years under the mentorship of Dr James Lind, F.R.S., to his experiments and speculations on electricity and air travel, Shelley's scientific interests greatly influenced his literary works. The article highlights the importance of Dr Lind's guidance and the impact of scientific books like Erasmus Darwin's "The Botanic Garden" on Shelley's writing.
