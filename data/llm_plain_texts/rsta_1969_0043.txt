tldr: This article discusses recent advancements in air pollution measurement techniques, focusing on the development of instruments for monitoring and controlling air pollution. The author highlights the importance of understanding plume behavior from tall chimneys and the use of high-speed recording devices for sulfur dioxide concentration and plume height. The article also emphasizes the need for appropriate averaging times in measurements and the significance of integrating times for different types of pollution complaints.
