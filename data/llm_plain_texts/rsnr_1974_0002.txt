tldr: The inaugural Hartley Lecture discusses the life and achievements of Sir Harold Hartley, a prominent scientist and advocate for applied science and technology. Hartley's contributions spanned various fields, including chemical engineering, control engineering, and the development of railways and air travel. His "hartling" technique, a persuasive method of encouraging others to engage with science, had a significant impact on many individuals throughout his career.
