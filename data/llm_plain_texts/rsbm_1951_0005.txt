tldr: Eustace Tennyson D'Eyncourt was a prominent naval architect and Director of Naval Construction for the British Royal Navy. He made significant contributions to ship design, including the development of the bulge system of underwater protection against torpedoes. D'Eyncourt also played a key role in the design and construction of various warships and merchant vessels throughout his career.
