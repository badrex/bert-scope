tldr: This article discusses the concept of anabiosis, or latent life, where microorganisms can be deprived of visible signs of life but can return to normal life when conditions are restored. The author traces the history of this concept from Leeuwenhoek's observations in 1702 to current research, highlighting the importance of understanding the reversible arrest of life processes in various organisms.
