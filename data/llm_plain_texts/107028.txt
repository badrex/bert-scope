tldr: In this article, William Herschel presents a fourth catalog of the comparative brightness of stars, providing detailed observations and measurements of various stars' positions and magnitudes. He also discusses discrepancies between his findings and those in existing star catalogs, such as the Atlas Caelestis and the British Catalogue, and suggests corrections for some of these discrepancies.
