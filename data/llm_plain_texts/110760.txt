tldr: This article presents a summary of magnetic observations made in various locations in India and Southeast Asia during the period of November 1840 to August 1841. The observations were conducted at magnetic observatories established by the Honourable East India Company and communicated by P. M. Roget. The data includes magnetic dip and intensity measurements, as well as meteorological observations and instrument details.
