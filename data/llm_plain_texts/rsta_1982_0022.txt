tldr: This article discusses the evolution and development of sedimentary basins, focusing on their importance as sources of water, oil, and hydrocarbon gases. It covers various sedimentary basins worldwide, their subsidence history, sedimentary facies, thermal regimes, and relations to crustal structures. The authors provide a review of new information from deep drilling and seismic data, highlighting the significance of understanding these basins for economic and scientific purposes.
