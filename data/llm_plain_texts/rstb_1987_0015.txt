tldr: This article discusses the development and applications of ion-selective field-effect transistors (ISFETs) as biosensors for monitoring ions in biological samples, such as blood. The authors describe the structure and function of ISFETs, their sensitivity to various ions, and their potential for use in medical applications. They also highlight the challenges and future prospects for ISFET technology.
