tldr: The article discusses the results of the Royal Society Expedition to Aldabra in 1967-68, focusing on the Entomostraca fauna, particularly the genus Heterocypris (Crustacea, Ostracoda). The authors describe their sampling methods, ecological parameters, and the significance of permanent freshwater reservoirs on Aldabra. They also discuss the atoll's elevation and tidal influences on freshwater aquifers.
