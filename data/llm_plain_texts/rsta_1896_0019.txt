tldr: This article presents a detailed magnetic survey of the British Isles conducted in 1891. The authors used a series of observations and calculations to determine the magnetic forces and disturbances at various locations, and they identified key regions of magnetic attraction. The study aimed to understand the main magnetic features of the United Kingdom and to detect any changes in the magnetic landscape.
