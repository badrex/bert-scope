tldr: This article presents the results of a detailed study and measurement of spectra photographed during the total solar eclipse of January 22, 1898. The authors used prismatic cameras to capture the spectra and identified various elements present in the Sun's corona. They also discussed the limitations of their investigation and the need for further research.
