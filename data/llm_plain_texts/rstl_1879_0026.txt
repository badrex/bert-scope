tldr: The author examines the Mallophaga, a type of bird parasite, collected from various bird species in Kerguelen's Land and Rodriguez during the Transit of Venus Expeditions in 1874-75. They describe and classify four new species of Mallophaga, providing detailed descriptions of their physical characteristics and distinguishing features. These discoveries contribute to the understanding of the diverse and unique fauna found in these remote locations.
