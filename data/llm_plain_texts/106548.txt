tldr: King George I grants the President, Council, and Fellows of the Royal Society of London a licence to purchase land in perpetuity, not exceeding the yearly value of one thousand pounds, for the improvement of natural knowledge. This licence allows the Royal Society to hold and enjoy lands and hereditaments that have been devised or granted to them, as well as to purchase additional properties for their use.
