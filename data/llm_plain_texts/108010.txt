tldr: The author examines the reflex function in the animal economy, which is a principle of action connected to the medulla oblongata and medulla spinalis. This function is characterized by being excited in its action and reflex in its course, and it is distinct from other functions like voluntary and respiratory motions. The reflex function maintains equilibrium in the muscular system and can be excited into various actions or take on morbid forms.
