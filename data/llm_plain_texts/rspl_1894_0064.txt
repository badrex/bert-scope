tldr: The authors examine over 300 rock and mineral specimens collected from the Karakoram Himalayas by Mr. W.M. Conway. They provide a summary of their work and discuss various types of rocks, including serpentine, diorite, granite, and gneiss, as well as their geological and mineralogical properties. The authors also mention the presence of various minerals such as garnets, staurolite, magnetite, and mica in the collected samples.
