tldr: The article discusses the history and development of the Welcome Research Institution, founded by Silas M. Burroughs and Henry S. Welcome in 1880. The institution played a significant role in the research and production of antitoxins, vaccines, and pharmaceuticals, contributing to the understanding of immunization and the treatment of various diseases. The article highlights the institution's achievements, including the discovery of histamine, the development of methods for standardizing sera, and the production of lifesaving vaccines and antitoxins during both world wars.
