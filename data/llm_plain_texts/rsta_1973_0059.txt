tldr: This article discusses the measurement and interpretation of Earth's strain in Australia, with a focus on the Cooney Observatory. It describes various research programs and instruments used for strain determination, including rod, wire, and laser strain-meters. The Cooney Observatory's research program emphasizes instrument development and verification, aiming to improve established techniques for measuring Earth's crustal movements.
