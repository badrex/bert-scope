tldr: The author presents two innovative surveying instruments, a 5-feet transit-instrument and a zenith sector, designed for the Great Trigonometrical Survey of India to accurately determine longitude and latitude. The transit-instrument features a powerful telescope and adjustable axis, while the zenith sector has a lightweight design and a unique arrangement of sectors for improved accuracy. These instruments aim to provide precise measurements for geodesical and astronomical research in India.
