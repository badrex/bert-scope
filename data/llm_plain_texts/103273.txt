tldr: Alexander Stuart describes a rock-cut temple and its unknown characters at Cannara in Salset Island. The temple features 45 rock-cut pillars and an altar, while the characters are engraved on the rock beside the temple's entrance. Stuart also mentions various caverns with similar rock-cut features and inscriptions on their doors.
