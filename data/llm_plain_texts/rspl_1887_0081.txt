tldr: The authors describe a new type of lichen structure, Gasterolichenes, where the fungus belongs to the order Gasteromycetes. This group consists of two genera, Emericella and other related genera. Gasterolichenes is distinct from the previously known groups of lichens, Ascomycetes and Hymenolichenes, in terms of fungal constituents and their spore production.
