tldr: This article provides personal memories and recollections of Sir Richard Threlfall, a prominent figure in the fields of physics and chemistry, who made significant contributions to science and served in various capacities during his lifetime. Threlfall's career included positions at the University of Sydney, Albright & Wilson Ltd., and various scientific boards and committees. His expertise and accomplishments are highlighted through anecdotes and reflections from his son, R. E. Threlfall.
