tldr: Major Greenwood was a prominent medical researcher and statistician who made significant contributions to the fields of epidemiology and public health. He was a devoted disciple of Karl Pearson and played a crucial role in the development of biostatistics. Greenwood's work on large-scale trials to assess the efficacy of prophylactic and therapeutic measures helped improve the medical profession's understanding of statistical methods.
