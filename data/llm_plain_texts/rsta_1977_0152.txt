tldr: This article discusses the limitations and challenges of current planning systems and operational research in decision making. It highlights the need for a human-centered approach to planning, recognizing the importance of free will and individual choice in decision-making processes. The author argues that a shift in methodology and world view is necessary to address the increasing alienation of people from society and improve the overall quality of life.
