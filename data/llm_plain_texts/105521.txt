tldr: The author examines the origin and formation of the Belemnite fossil, a marine fossil commonly known as the Thunder-bolt. They explore the differences between various species of Belemnites and their unique characteristics, such as the way they break and their internal structures. The author concludes that Belemnites belong to the testaceous part of the animal kingdom and are related to nautili, and that their formation is similar to that of other testaceous bodies.
