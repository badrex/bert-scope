tldr: The authors investigated the crystal structure of di-n-propylmonocyanogold, confirming the planar configuration of auric gold valencies and providing information on the dimensions of the covalent auric gold link. The study also revealed the molecular configuration of the compound, with gold atoms arranged in a square formation and propyl groups attached to the sides of the square.
