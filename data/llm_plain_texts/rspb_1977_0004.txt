tldr: The article discusses the advancements in medical endoscopy, particularly the flexible fibrescope, and its applications in gastroenterology. The fibrescope has revolutionized the diagnosis and treatment of various gastrointestinal conditions, including detecting lesions, performing biopsies, and treating polyps. The author highlights the importance of endoscopy as a complementary tool to barium examinations and its role in diagnosing difficult cases, such as barium-negative dyspepsia and acute upper gastrointestinal bleeding. The article also emphasizes the need for further research and development in endoscopy to improve its clinical applications and contribute to patient management.
