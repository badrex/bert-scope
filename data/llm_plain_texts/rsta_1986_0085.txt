tldr: This article discusses the use of borosilicate glass as a medium for fixing high-level nuclear wastes. It explains the reasons for selecting borosilicate glass, such as its ease of processing and long-term radiation and thermal stability. The article also reviews the important properties of borosilicate glass and its manufacturing process. It highlights the leachability of waste glass and its impact on waste disposal, as well as the effects of waste loading and inhomogeneous distribution of elements in the glass.
