tldr: This article discusses the collaboration between John Locke and Robert Boyle in the fields of medicine and science. Locke contributed to Boyle's research on human blood and air, conducting experiments and keeping weather records. Their work aimed to understand the nature of epidemics and the properties of the air, as well as the analysis of human fluids like blood.
