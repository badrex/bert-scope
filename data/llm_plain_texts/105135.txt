tldr: The article presents an account of the second volume of Flora Sibirica, a valuable work on the plants of Siberia. The author, Professor Gmelin, follows the plant classification system of Van Royen and adopts Linnaeus' genera. The work includes detailed descriptions of plant species, their growth locations, and their uses by the local inhabitants. The author also shares some observations on the medical applications of certain plants in treating diseases.
