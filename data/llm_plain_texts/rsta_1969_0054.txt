tldr: This article discusses the application of air pollution research to power station design, focusing on controlling emissions and reducing pollutant concentrations. The author describes the fundamental principles of air pollution control, the objectives of research on power station emissions, and the methods used to reduce emissions, such as dark smoke, grit and dust, and gaseous pollutants. The article also highlights the importance of chimney design and the use of high-level dispersion to control concentrations of pollutants in the atmosphere.
