tldr: The author examines fossil floras in the Wyre Forest Coalfield and its surrounding areas, dividing the region into four sub-regions and analyzing the flora from three of them. They determine that the Sweet Coal Series belongs to the Middle Coal Measures, while the Sulphur Coal Series belongs to the Transition Coal Measures. The study also discusses the geological relationships between the Wyre Forest and other coalfields in the Welsh Borderland.
