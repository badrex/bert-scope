tldr: This article discusses the discovery of copper springs in Pennsylvania and their properties. The copper water is found to be a strong solution of vitriol, which can be used for various purposes, such as purging, curing ulcers, and treating cutaneous disorders. The author also mentions the potential for setting up a copperas-work or manufacture of vitriol in the area due to the abundant supply of water and fuel.
