tldr: A patient with a severe inflammation in the shoulder had an abscess that led to the removal of the upper head of the humerus bone. The surgeon performed a unique operation to saw off the bone head and, surprisingly, the patient experienced bone regeneration and full recovery, retaining the full motion of the limb. This case study highlights the potential for bone regeneration and the importance of innovative surgical approaches.
