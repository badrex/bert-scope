tldr: This article presents meteorological observations and phenomena recorded in various locations across Europe during the year 1729. The author, William Derham, provides tables of barometric and thermometric readings from different places, discussing the range of mercury in northern and southern climates and the temperature of diverse regions. The article also includes descriptions of meteorological phenomena such as auroras, streamers, and parhelia, as well as an appendix with observations from the Marquess Poleni.
