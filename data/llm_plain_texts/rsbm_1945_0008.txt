tldr: Joseph Arthur Arkwright was a distinguished bacteriologist who made significant contributions to the fields of bacteriology and medical research. Despite starting his scientific career late in life, Arkwright's background in natural history and biology allowed him to become a competent bacteriologist and make valuable observations on variations in bacteria, the role of carriers in infectious diseases, and the aetiology and transmission of various diseases. His work in bacteriology and his dedication to scientific investigation left a lasting impact on the field.
