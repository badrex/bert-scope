tldr: The Tercentenary Conversazione held on 23 July 1960 was a significant event in the history of the Royal Society, attended by 2000 guests, including H.R.H. The Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh. The event showcased various scientific exhibits, covering a wide range of research fields, and aimed to provide a broad survey of scientific endeavors in the UK and the Commonwealth. The exhibition was open to the public and attracted around 7,000 visitors during the week.
