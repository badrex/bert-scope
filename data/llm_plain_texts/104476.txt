tldr: This article presents a case study of a woman who experienced an extra-uterine conception, a rare occurrence where a fertilized egg implants outside the uterus. The author, Starkey Myddelton, details the woman's symptoms, medical examinations, and eventual autopsy, which revealed a fetus encased in a tumor formed by the expansion of the fallopian tube. The case highlights the importance of understanding conception and the role of the uterus in fetal development.
