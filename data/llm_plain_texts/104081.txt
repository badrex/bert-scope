tldr: A patient experienced an extraordinary impostumation of the liver, which led to their death. The doctor, Tho. Short, performed an autopsy and discovered six quarts of purulent, thick, and fetid reddish-brown matter in the lowest and thinnest lobes of the liver. The patient had drained off the thinner part of the impostumation through vomiting and purging. The tumor had compressed the right kidney, causing it to become emaciated.
