tldr: The author investigates the action of bile on fats and its role in digestion. They find that bile chemically decomposes fats into fatty acids, which then form an emulsion. This process is essential for the digestion of fats in the stomach. The author also examines excretine and its presence in feces, finding that it is only present in adult individuals.
