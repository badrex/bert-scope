tldr: Ralph Allen Sampson was a talented mathematician and astronomer who made significant contributions to various fields, including hydrodynamics, astronomical spectroscopy, and the study of Jupiter's satellites. He also worked on improving telescope design and was a prominent member of the Society of Friends. Sampson was elected as a Fellow of the Royal Society and held various academic positions throughout his career, including Professor of Mathematics at Durham University and Professor of Astronomy at Edinburgh University.
