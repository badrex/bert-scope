tldr: This article discusses the osteology of the Solitaire bird, Pezophaps solitaria, from the island of Rodriguez. The authors analyze a large collection of bones to understand the bird's anatomy and evolution. They also address the controversy surrounding the number of species of Solitaire birds and the evidence of human interaction with these birds.
