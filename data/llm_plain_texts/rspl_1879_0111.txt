tldr: The author investigated the fossil plants of Alum Bay and discovered a diverse flora containing at least 116 genera and 274 species. The study found a close connection between the Alum Bay and Sheppey floras, with over 50 species common to both locations. The author also identified several ancestral species and discussed the relationships between Miocene and Eocene species.
