tldr: This biographical memoir recounts the life of Clement Richard Attlee, the first Earl Attlee, who served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom and leader of the Labour Party. Born into a professional family, Attlee initially pursued history and literature but became involved in social welfare and reform after witnessing poverty in East London. His political career spanned decades, including service in World War I, local government, and national leadership. Attlee's dedication to social justice and his role in shaping British politics are highlighted in this memoir.
