tldr: Henry Kater conducted experiments comparing the illumination of Cassegrainian and Gregorian telescopes. He found that the Cassegrainian telescope had a greater illuminating power, with a ratio of approximately 2:1. This research helped to improve understanding of telescope design and construction.
