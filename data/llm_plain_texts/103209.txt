tldr: This article presents a case study of a patient with a scirrhous tumor enclosed in a cyst. The tumor was discovered during treatment for a pleuritic fever. The patient had a history of a breast injury from a horse-riding accident 30 years prior. The tumor was surgically removed, and the author discusses the pathology and formation of such tumors. This case study contributes to the understanding of tumor pathology and surgical treatment options.
