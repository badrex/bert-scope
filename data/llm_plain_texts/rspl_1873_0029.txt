The author traces the origin and causes of these winds based on local physical changes in the atmosphere. The study covers various regions of North India, including the Punjab, Gangetic Plain, Plateau of Pajpootana, Central India, Western Bengal, Gangetic Delta, Assam, and Ardkan Coast. The author also discusses the impact of monsoons and temperature changes on the region's winds.
