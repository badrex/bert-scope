tldr: Erik Helge Osvald Stensiö was a renowned Swedish palaeontologist who specialized in Triassic fishes. He made significant contributions to the field of palaeontology, leading multiple expeditions to Spitsbergen and working in various museums across Europe. Stensiö's research on Triassic fishes and arthrodires helped shape the understanding of ancient marine life and established him as a leading figure in the field.
