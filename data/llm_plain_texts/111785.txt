tldr: This obituary honors Edward Forbes, a prominent geologist and naturalist, who made significant contributions to the fields of marine biology and paleontology. Forbes is best known for his theory of bathymetrical distribution of life, which states that distinct zones of marine species exist at different depths in the ocean. His work in these fields, along with his passion for science and his genial personality, made him a respected figure in the scientific community.
