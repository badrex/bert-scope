The article discusses the unique anatomical features of Postosuchus, such as its erect and facultative bipedal gait, advanced pelvis, and highly serrated teeth. The author also explores the evolutionary significance of Postosuchus, particularly its relation to the ancestry of the Tyrannosauridae family of dinosaurs.
