tldr: This article reports an outbreak of rabies among African wild dogs in Kenya, resulting in the deaths of 21 out of 23 members of one pack. The rabies virus was found to be identical in sequence to that of domestic dogs in Kenya, suggesting transmission between the two species. The outbreak highlights the importance of disease control in protecting endangered species like African wild dogs and the need for continued conservation efforts.
