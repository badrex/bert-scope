tldr: The obituary of Tom Goodey, a renowned nematologist and applied biologist, highlights his significant contributions to the field of protozoa research, particularly in soil. Goodey's work challenged the predator theory of bacteria limitation in soil and paved the way for further research on amoebae. Besides his scientific achievements, Goodey was also a talented musician, performing in various choirs, musical societies, and operatic productions throughout his life.
