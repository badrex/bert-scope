tldr: This article is an obituary for H. F. Newall, an astronomer who made significant contributions to the field. Newall was an accomplished observer and organized the measurement of photographic plates with computing machines, improving the accuracy of astronomical measurements. He also played a crucial role in the establishment of the International Astronomical Union and was a dedicated member of his college community.
