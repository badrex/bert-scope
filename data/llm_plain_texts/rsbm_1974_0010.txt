tldr: 'The article discusses the life and work of Ronald Holroyd, a prominent figure in fuel technology and coal hydrogenation. Holroyd dedicated his career to finding alternative liquid fuels based on coal, aiming to reduce dependence on Middle East oil. His research on coal hydrogenation and the development of more efficient catalysts led to significant advancements in the field, though his work faced challenges due to the availability of cheap Middle East oil. His contributions to fuel technology and the pursuit of alternative energy sources are remembered and celebrated in this biographical memoir.'
