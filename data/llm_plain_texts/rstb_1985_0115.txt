tldr: This article discusses the control of infectious diseases in main food-producing animals, such as cattle, sheep, pigs, and poultry. It explores the factors involved in the epizootiology of diseases, including properties of infectious agents, their natural history, and factors that influence the spread and development of disease. The article also considers the impact of intensive animal husbandry on the occurrence of infectious diseases and their control, and the methods currently available for controlling infectious diseases on a global, national, and local level.
