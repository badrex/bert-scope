tldr: This article discusses the importance of understanding weldability and its implications for materials requirements in engineering. It outlines the potential welding problems in steels, nickel alloys, aluminum alloys, and titanium alloys and the influence on material specifications. The author emphasizes the need for a rational approach to material selection, design of welded structures, and assurance of reliability, which has been provided by the physical metallurgy approach.
