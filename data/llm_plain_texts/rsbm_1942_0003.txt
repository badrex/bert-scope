tldr: Frederick Grant Banting was a Canadian medical researcher who co-discovered insulin, a life-changing treatment for diabetes. Born in Ontario, Banting showed great curiosity and determination throughout his life, which led him to make significant contributions to medical science. He worked closely with Charles Best and other colleagues to extract insulin from the pancreas, saving countless lives of diabetic patients. Banting's work earned him numerous awards, including the Nobel Prize in Medicine, and he is remembered as a pioneer in the field of medical research.
