tldr: Captain Henry Kater invented an astronomical clock with a unique escapement mechanism that aimed to provide equal impulses to the pendulum, regardless of execution quality. This innovation was described by his son Edward Kater and communicated by Sir John F. W. Herschel to the Royal Society.
