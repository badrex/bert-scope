tldr: This article highlights the early work and contributions of Sebastian Ziani De Ferranti, a pioneer in electric power transmission. De Ferranti envisioned central electricity generation and efficient transmission at high voltage to consumption centers. His innovative ideas and designs led to the development of transformers, switchgear, and cables, and he played a significant role in the transformation of the electric power industry.
