tldr: The article discusses the life and scientific achievements of Frank Twyman, who made significant contributions to the field of spectroscopy and interferometry. Twyman worked with Otto Hilger, designing and manufacturing spectroscopes and spectrometers for various applications, including the development of the wavelength spectrometer and the Twyman-Green interferometer. His innovations in instrument design helped improve the quality of optical components and enabled routine spectrochemical analysis in industry.
