tldr: The author, Tiberius Cavallo, explores the temperament of musical instruments with fixed tones, such as the harpsichord, organ, and guitar. He explains how the division of strings can express various notes and the necessity of temperament to adapt the strings to different key-notes. The author also discusses the imperfection inherent in fixed instruments and the importance of the proportions of notes in creating harmonious music.
