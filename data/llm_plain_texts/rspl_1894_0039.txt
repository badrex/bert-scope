tldr: This article presents experimental investigations on the effective temperature of the Sun conducted at Daramona, Streete, Co. Westmeath. The authors discuss and compare their results with previous studies by Rossetti and Le Chatelier. They used a radiation balance method and a modified "meldo-meter" as an artificial source of heat.
