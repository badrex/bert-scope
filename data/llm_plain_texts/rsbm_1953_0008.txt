tldr: William Ewart Gye was a prominent figure in cancer research, known for his support of the virus theory of cancer. He also conducted significant research on silicosis, showing that silica had a toxic effect on tissues and contributed to the development of the disease. Gye's work on filterable fowl tumors helped establish their connection to cancer and provided important insights into the nature of cancerous growths.
