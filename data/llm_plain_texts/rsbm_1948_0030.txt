tldr: William Whitehead Watts was a prominent geologist and educator who made significant contributions to the field of geology. He worked for the Geological Survey, conducted research on rocks, and played a crucial role in the development of geology education at the Royal College of Science and Royal School of Mines. His dedication to teaching and research helped shape the future of geology and its applications in various industries.
