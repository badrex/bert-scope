tldr: This article emphasizes the need for sublethal studies to understand the impact of pollutants on marine life. It discusses the importance of distinguishing between sublethal and lethal effects of pollutants and proposes a method for categorizing experiments based on their duration and the life span of the organisms involved. The article also highlights the need for clear definitions and better methods for measuring and analyzing sublethal effects, such as using information theory to assess ecological diversity.
