tldr: This article discusses the local and exotic components of primitive meteorites and their origin. It explores the properties and classification of chondrites, the processes that occurred in the early Solar System, and the various astronomical sources that contributed to the formation of these materials. The study of meteorites provides valuable insights into the composition and history of the Solar System, and the presence of exotic components in meteorites offers clues to their interstellar origins.
