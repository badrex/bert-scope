tldr: In this letter, Edward Lhuyd describes various observations of alpine waters, rivers, and meteorological phenomena. He discusses the nature of alpine springs, the height and notable features of mountains, and the customs and diet of the local inhabitants. Lhuyd also mentions the superstitions of the common people and occasional observations on animals and plants. He provides detailed accounts of the Rhine, the River Maira, and other rivers and waterfalls, as well as the damage caused by alpine torrents.
