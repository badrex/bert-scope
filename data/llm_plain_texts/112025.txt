tldr: The author, W.H. Perkin, discovered mauveine, a powerful base derived from aniline, and studied its properties and various salts. Mauveine forms a series of compounds known as coal-tar colors, which have significant importance in arts and manufactures. The author describes the synthesis, purification, and analysis of mauveine and its salts, including hydrochlorate, platinum-salt, gold-salt, hydrobromate, and carbonate.
