tldr: This article is a biography of John Charles Burkill, a prominent mathematician and educator who made significant contributions to the field of mathematical analysis. Burkill's life is detailed, from his early years and academic achievements to his long and dedicated service to Peterhouse College, Cambridge. His research in differentiation and integration, as well as his support for graduate studies and refugee scholars, are highlighted. Burkill's work in mathematics and his impact on the academic community are celebrated in this memoir.
