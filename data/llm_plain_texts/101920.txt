tldr: The author, Nicholas Witsen, shares his extensive experience and methods in mapping the vast and challenging terrain of Great Tartary. He discusses his correspondence with various regions, the collection of journals and registers, and the creation of a detailed map. Witsen also addresses uncertainties in the map, such as sea coast stretching and potential mistakes in latitudes, and shares his thoughts on the North-East Point and the possibility of a passage to America.
