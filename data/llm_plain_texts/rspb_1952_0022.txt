tldr: This article presents the results of gravity measurements conducted in the English Channel by a team of researchers led by Sir Gerald Lenox-Conyngham. The measurements were made using a specialized gravimeter, and the data collected provided valuable insights into the geology and tectonic history of the region. This study marked an important step in the development of geophysics and geodesy as scientific disciplines.
