tldr: The author investigates the biology and host relationships of the silver fir chermes, a type of plant lice, in Britain. They study the life cycle and behavior of two species, Dreyfusia nusslini and Dreyfusia piceee, and their interactions with different host trees. The author also examines the impact of these insects on the silver fir and the potential role they play in the ecosystem.
