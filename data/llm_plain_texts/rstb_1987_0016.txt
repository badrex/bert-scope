These sensors can be used for various biosensing purposes, such as detecting gases produced by microbes, monitoring enzymatic reactions, and measuring gaseous species in biochemical reactions. The article also explores the use of gas sensors in plant physiology to detect ethylene production in fruits and vegetables.'
