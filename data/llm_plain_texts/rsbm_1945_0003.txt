tldr: This obituary honors Thomas Ranken Lyle, a distinguished natural philosopher and educator who made significant contributions to the fields of electrical technology and lighthouse illuminants. Lyle held the Chair of Natural Philosophy at Melbourne University and was known for his kindness, dedication to teaching, and active involvement in public service. His legacy lives on through his research, students, and the portrait that his former students had painted in his honor.
