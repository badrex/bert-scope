tldr: This article is an obituary for Peyton Blakiston, a physician and physicist, and William Froude, a civil engineer and scientist. Blakiston was known for his work in medicine, particularly in the study of sound and the use of the stethoscope. Froude made significant contributions to the fields of fluid dynamics, ship design, and the study of bird flight. Both individuals were Fellows of the Royal Society and made valuable contributions to science and medicine throughout their lives.
