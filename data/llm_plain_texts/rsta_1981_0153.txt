The author predicts that future gamma-ray observatories and telescopes will provide a wealth of information on solar activity, the chemical composition of planets, the structure of our galaxy, and high-energy processes in other galaxies. This will lead to a better understanding of the universe's matter-antimatter symmetry and the nature of high-energy particle acceleration.
