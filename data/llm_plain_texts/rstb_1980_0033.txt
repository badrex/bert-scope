tldr: This article discusses the history of penicillin research, from Fleming's initial discovery in 1929 to the development of semi-synthetic penicillins in the late 1950s. It highlights the contributions of various researchers and the unique position penicillin holds in medicine as the "queen of antibiotics." The article also touches upon the synthesis of penicillin V, 6-aminopenicillanic acid (6APA), and the acylation of 6APA to create new penicillins.
