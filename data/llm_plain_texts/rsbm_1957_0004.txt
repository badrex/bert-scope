tldr: This article is a biography of John Evershed, a British astronomer who made significant contributions to solar research and spectroscopy. Evershed discovered the Evershed effect, which describes the radial motion of gases in sunspots, and conducted numerous observations of solar prominences and eclipses. He was a founder member of the British Astronomical Association and served as the director of several sections within the organization. Evershed's work on solar research and his dedication to the field had a lasting impact on the study of astronomy.
