tldr: This article discusses the challenges and concepts involved in collecting and understanding world energy data. It highlights the differences in reserve concepts and definitions between primary energy resources, the importance of collecting energy data close to the source, and the need for better understanding of energy demand to take full advantage of mathematical models. The article also emphasizes the importance of cooperation between the energy industries and provides examples from the 1974 Survey of Energy Resources by the World Energy Conference.
