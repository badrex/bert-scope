tldr: Frederic Charles Bartlett was a pioneering figure in experimental psychology, who played a crucial role in shaping the field in England. He conducted experiments on perception and memory, emphasizing the selective and constructive nature of both processes. Bartlett introduced the concept of'schema' to explain how experiences are stored in the brain and influence perception and memory. His work had a significant impact on the development of psychology and continues to influence the field today.
