tldr: This article discusses the challenges and potential solutions for energy management in the 1980s. The authors explore the need for energy demand management, the role of major energy suppliers, and the importance of diversifying energy sources, including solar, wind, tidal, geothermal, and energy from waste. They also highlight the potential impact of solar energy on reducing energy demand from conventional sources and the need for further research and implementation of alternative energy sources.
