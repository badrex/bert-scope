tldr: Almroth Edward Wright was a pioneering medical scientist who made significant contributions to the field of immunization and infectious disease control. He developed the first effective vaccine against typhoid fever and played a crucial role in establishing the importance of active immunization. Wright's work laid the foundation for modern medicine's understanding of the role of antibodies and the immune system in combating infectious diseases.
