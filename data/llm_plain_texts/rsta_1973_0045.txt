tldr: This article discusses the development and use of various instruments and techniques for measuring tidal tilt in the Earth. The authors describe their long-base liquid-level tiltmeters and a new pendulum tiltmeter suitable for operation in shallow boreholes. They also discuss the importance of selecting appropriate sites and considering local geology for accurate measurements.
