tldr: The author conducted tidal observations in the Arctic Seas, specifically in Northumberland Sound and Wellington Channel, aboard H.M.S. Assistance from May 24th to July 6th, 1853. They analyzed the data to determine the Diurnal and Semidiurnal Tide Ranges, as well as the Lunitidal Intervals at High Water and Low Water. The study provides valuable insights into the tidal patterns and processes in the Arctic region.
