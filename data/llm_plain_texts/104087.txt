tldr: The author, Richard-Middleton Massey, describes the natural history of Coccus Polonicus, a bug that produces a dye. He explains its life cycle, from the insect's development under the sun to its final stage as a cocci adhering to the roots of the Polygonum Cocciferum plant. The author also discusses the insect's role in dye-making and its potential impact on the plant.
