tldr: The author conducted research on the chemical composition of human excrements and the effects of a vegetable diet on their formation. They discovered that margarate of lime, phosphate of lime, and margarate of magnesia are immediate principles of human evacuations. A vegetable diet was found to induce the presence of margaric acid in excrements, and the author also confirmed the presence of cholesterine in the spleen.
