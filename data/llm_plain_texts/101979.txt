tldr: The author describes and analyzes unique transparent stones found in England, which they call Brontiae Laeves, Pellucidae, Resplendentes, Adamantum Aemulae. These stones have a natural polish and hardness, and their shape resembles that of echinoids or Ombriae. The author discusses their differences from other known stones and fossils, and encourages further research into these intriguing specimens.
