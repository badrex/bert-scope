tldr: The Royal Society and the Royal Astronomical Society celebrated the quincentenary of Nicolaus Copernicus's birth with a symposium on planetary science. The event featured presentations on the Moon, Mars, Venus, Jupiter, Saturn, and outer planets. It also included a lecture on Copernicus's work and various exhibits showcasing modern developments in planetary science and historical material related to Copernicus.
