tldr: In 1821, Richard Phillips and Michael Faraday discovered a new compound of chlorine and carbon. They found that this compound, which they called a chloride of carbon, had unique properties and a chemical composition of one part chlorine and two parts carbon. This discovery expanded the understanding of carbon compounds and their potential applications.
