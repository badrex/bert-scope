tldr: The Royal Society established the Aldabra Research Station on the Aldabra atoll in the Indian Ocean to study the island's ecosystem and protect it from human development. The station provides facilities for field research and ensures the conservation of the atoll for scientific study. Various research areas include the study of giant tortoises, birds, land invertebrates, marine sciences, geology, and land vegetation.
