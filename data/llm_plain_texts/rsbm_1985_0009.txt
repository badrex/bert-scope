tldr: This biographical memoir recounts the life of Thomas Maxwell Harris, a natural historian and palaeobotanist, from his childhood and family background to his academic career at Cambridge University. Harris made significant contributions to the field of palaeobotany, working with A. C. Seward and conducting research on ancient plants. His passion for natural history and botany began at a young age, and he went on to achieve a First in both Part I and Part II of the Science Tripos at Cambridge.
