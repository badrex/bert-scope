tldr: The authors describe a new Permian armoured amphibian species, Peltobatrachus pustulatus, found in East Africa. The species is characterized by its massive dermal armour, pustular ornamentation, and unique skull and vertebrae structure. The authors also discuss the taxonomic position of the plagiosaurs and the order Plagiosauria within the Labyrinthodontia.
