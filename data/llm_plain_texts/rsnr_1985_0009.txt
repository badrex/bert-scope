tldr: This article examines the contributions of American colonial scientists who published in the Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society between 1753 and 1775. It highlights the types of scientific research conducted by these scientists, their backgrounds, and the impact of their work on the scientific community during that time. The article also compares the American colonial scientists' contributions to those of non-American scientists published in the same journal.
