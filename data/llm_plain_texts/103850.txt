tldr: This article provides a historical and philosophical analysis of the Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights. The author, John Eames, discusses the various hypotheses proposed by learned scholars to explain the phenomenon and presents a detailed account of the work by Mr. De Mairan, which offers a comprehensive explanation of the Aurora Borealis. The article also covers the nature of the Zodiacal Light, the Solar Atmosphere, and its connection to the Aurora Borealis.
