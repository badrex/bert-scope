tldr: This article discusses the historical methods used to produce plane surfaces in early Newtonian telescopes. It explores the development of techniques for testing and manufacturing flat surfaces, including the use of surface plates, liquid surfaces, and spherical mirrors. The article also examines the reflectivity of various speculum alloys used in these telescopes.
