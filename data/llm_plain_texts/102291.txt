tldr: The author provides a detailed account of the ancient city of Palmyra, its history, and the inscriptions found there. Palmyra was a significant trading hub and enjoyed a neutral status between the Roman and Parthian empires. The city flourished under the rule of Emperor Hadrian and continued to prosper until its eventual decline and destruction in the 3rd century AD. The author also discusses the importance of coins in understanding the history of Palmyra and its rulers.
