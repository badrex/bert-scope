tldr: The article investigates the susceptibility of various animals to the human strain of trypanosome disease in Nyasaland. The study found that goats and sheep were highly susceptible, while oxen and rabbits showed varying levels of resistance. The researchers also compared the virulence of five different strains of the trypanosome and found little difference in their ability to infect animals.
