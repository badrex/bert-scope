tldr: In this letter, Peter Wargentin, Secretary to the Royal Academy of Sciences in Sweden, shares his observations of the Venus transit made by various astronomers in Sweden. He discusses the telescopes used, the differences in meridians between different locations, and the discrepancies in the observations. Wargentin expresses his desire to learn about the observations made by English astronomers and requests to receive those observations.
