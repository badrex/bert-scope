tldr: This article recounts the story of the solar eclipse predicted by Thales of Miletus, which occurred during a battle between the Medes and Lydians in 585 BCE. The eclipse led to the end of the war and the establishment of peace between the two nations. The author, William Stukeley, highlights the importance of astronomy in understanding historical events and the role of divine providence in such occurrences.
