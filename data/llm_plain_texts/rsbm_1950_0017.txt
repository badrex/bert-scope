tldr: Birbal Sahni was an eminent Indian palaeobotanist born in 1891. He conducted extensive research on recent and fossil plants, making significant contributions to the understanding of the structure and morphology of various plant species. Sahni's work on fossil ferns, conifers, and Indian Gondwana plants helped advance the field of palaeobotany. He was a dedicated teacher and researcher, and his findings continue to influence the study of ancient plant life.
