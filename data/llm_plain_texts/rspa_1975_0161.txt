tldr: This article discusses the potential of communication satellites to address educational deficits in developing nations. It examines the challenges and limitations of satellite delivery systems and applies six criteria to evaluate their effectiveness, adaptability, and feasibility in meeting the diverse educational needs of rural areas. The author concludes that while satellites have the potential to reach a large audience, they face significant obstacles in terms of pedagogical effectiveness, adaptability to audience convenience, and organization and management.
