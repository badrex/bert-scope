tldr: The author proposes a decimal compass card with 36 points of ten degrees each to simplify navigation for mariners. This innovation eliminates the need for converting degrees to points and vice versa, reduces errors, and improves the efficiency of navigation. The author also suggests that the new system can be easily integrated with existing charts and compasses.
