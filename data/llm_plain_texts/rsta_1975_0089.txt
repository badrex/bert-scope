tldr: This article discusses the early history of ionospheric research in the United States, from the first suggestion of the ionosphere's existence by Kennelly to the initial data collected using rockets. It outlines the work of various groups and organizations in the field and highlights the development of the magnetoionic theory and multi-frequency techniques, which have contributed to our understanding of ionization in the Earth's outer atmosphere.
