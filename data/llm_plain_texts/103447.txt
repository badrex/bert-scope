tldr: The author, Henry de Saumarez, conducted experiments using his instrument called the Marine Surveyor to measure the strength of tides in the River Thames. He believed that similar measurements in the Channel and on the British coast would greatly benefit commerce and safety. He also mentioned his plans to publish accurate maps of the area for the public good.
