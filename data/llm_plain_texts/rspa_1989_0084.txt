The authors present a detailed theory of the crystal field and electronic wave functions for these ions, discussing their unusual hyperfine interactions and possible magnetic and acoustic resonance experiments. The study provides insights into the properties of lanthanide ions at low concentrations in metallic gold, which can be useful for understanding their behavior in various applications.
