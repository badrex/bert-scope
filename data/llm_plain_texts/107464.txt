tldr: The author examines the role of heart wood in tree growth and vitality. They discover that heart wood becomes a reservoir of organizable matter during winter, which is used for germination in the spring. The fluid sap passes through the heart wood laterally, contributing to the tree's strength and resilience. The author also discusses the differences in growth and vitality between annual, biennial, and perennial plants, as well as the importance of heart wood in maintaining the health of trees.
