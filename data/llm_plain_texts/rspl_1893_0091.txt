tldr: The author describes two new reptile species discovered in the Elgin Sandstone: Erpetosuchus Granti and Ornithosuchus Woodwardi. Erpetosuchus is a small parasuchian crocodile, while Ornithosuchus is a reptile intermediate between dinosaurs and crocodilians. These discoveries provide valuable insights into the evolution of reptiles during the Mesozoic Era.
