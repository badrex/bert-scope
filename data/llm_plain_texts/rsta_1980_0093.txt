tldr: The authors used Auger electron spectroscopy and scanning electron microscopy to analyze the embrittling residuals in engineering alloys that experienced intergranular failure. They categorized the failures into high-temperature, low-temperature, and environmental reactions and developed a relative fragility parameter to identify the most probable embrittling agents in various metallurgical situations.
