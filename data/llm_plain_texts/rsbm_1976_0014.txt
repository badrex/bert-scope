tldr: 'Joseph Proudman was a renowned oceanographer and mathematician who made significant contributions to the field of physical oceanography. He founded the Tidal Institute at Liverpool and conducted groundbreaking research on oceanic circulation, temperature, and salinity. Proudman also initiated observational work in the Irish Sea, collecting temperature and salinity data for several decades. His work on turbulence and tidal friction in tidal currents was also notable.'
