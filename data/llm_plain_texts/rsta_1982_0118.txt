It highlights the challenges posed by the vast resource potential and the demanding technical tasks required to identify and exploit these deposits. The article also emphasizes the importance of environmental research and control in ocean mining and the need for precise positioning systems, such as the Global Positioning System (GPS), to ensure accurate location and navigation in the ocean.
