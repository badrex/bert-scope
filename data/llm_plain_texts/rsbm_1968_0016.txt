tldr: This article is a biography of J. Robert Oppenheimer, a prominent theoretical physicist responsible for raising American theoretical physics to world leadership. Born into a wealthy family, Oppenheimer showed an early interest in science and studied at Harvard University and several European institutions. He made significant contributions to quantum mechanics, continuous spectra theory, and nuclear physics, and was a mentor to many future physicists. Oppenheimer's life and work were marked by both scientific achievements and personal struggles.
