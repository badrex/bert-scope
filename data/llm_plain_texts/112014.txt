tldr: The author examines the evidence of river-action excavation in the Valley of the Somme, specifically at Drucat near Abbeville. They study the high-level gravel, sand, and loess deposits, as well as the calcareous tufa or travertin formations, to understand the valley's formation and the role of water in shaping the landscape. This research provides insights into the geological and paleontological history of the region.
