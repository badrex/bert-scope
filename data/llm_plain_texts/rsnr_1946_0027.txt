tldr: The Kincardine Papers provide a collection of 116 letters written by Sir Robert Moray to his friend Alexander Bruce between 1657 and 1673. These letters offer insights into Moray's life, his involvement in the Royal Society, and his possible role as the first Freemason initiated in England. The papers are now preserved in the Royal Society library, and they represent an important source for historical and scientific research.
