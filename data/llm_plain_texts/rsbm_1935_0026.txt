tldr: The obituary of Herman Glauert, a prominent figure in aeronautics, highlights his life, work, and contributions to the field. Glauert worked at the Royal Aircraft Factory during World War I and later developed aerodynamic theory, significantly impacting the design of airplanes. His work and publications, including the book "Aerofoil and Airscrew," helped shape the future of aeronautics.
