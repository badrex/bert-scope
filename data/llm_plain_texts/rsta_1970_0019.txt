tldr: This article discusses the structure and evolution of the Red Sea and surrounding areas, focusing on the geological history and tectonic events that have shaped the region. It examines the Red Sea's unique structure, including its axial fracture zone, and explores the uplift of the margins of the African and Arabian Precambrian shields. The article also reviews the geophysical studies and research conducted in the Red Sea area, as well as the neighboring regions of the Gulf of Suez and the Gulf of Aqaba-Dead Sea.
