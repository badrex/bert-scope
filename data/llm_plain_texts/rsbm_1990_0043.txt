tldr: Nikolaas Tinbergen was a renowned Dutch biologist and ethologist known for his groundbreaking studies on animal behavior. He made significant contributions to the understanding of animal behavior, including the study of feeding behavior in owls, reproductive behavior in common terns, and the navigation abilities of wasps. Tinbergen's work laid the foundation for the field of ethology and inspired many students to pursue careers in biology and wildlife research.
