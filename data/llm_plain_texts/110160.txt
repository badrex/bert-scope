tldr: The author studied the effects of excessive pulmonary circulation on the air cells of the hare's lungs. They observed that the air cells filled with coagulable lymph and red blood coagula after strenuous exercise. This phenomenon provided insights into the nature of lung tuberculosis. The author referred to Dr. Baillie's description and plates of tubercles in his Morbid Anatomy.
