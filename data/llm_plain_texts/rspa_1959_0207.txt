tldr: The article discusses the significance of recent rocket and satellite studies on the structure of the thermosphere. It examines the composition and temperature of the upper atmosphere and explores the importance of diffusive separation, hydrogen atoms, and photo-chemical action in the terrestrial atmosphere above 100 km. The author also considers the limitations imposed by the requirements of space vehicle guidance, control, and data recovery.
