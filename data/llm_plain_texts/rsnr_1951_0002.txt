tldr: This article delves into the early life and career of renowned naturalist Antoni van Leeuwenhoek in Amsterdam from 1648 to 1654. It explores his connection with merchant William Davidson, his employment as a bookkeeper and cashier, and his training in the cloth trade. The article also discusses Leeuwenhoek's silence about his Amsterdam period in his later correspondence.
