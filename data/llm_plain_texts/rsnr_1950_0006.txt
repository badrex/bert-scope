tldr: 'Rodolph Valltravers, an 18th-century scientist, made significant contributions to applied science and inventions. He presented various models and useful machines to the Royal Society of Arts and the Royal Society, including a hydraulic machine and improvements in kitchen fires. Valltravers also imported a brewer from Yorkshire to make English beer and built an unsinkable boat based on descriptions from Sir Joseph Banks and Dr. Daniel Solander. His house, Rockhall, was later rented to the infamous Cagliostro.'
