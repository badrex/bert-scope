tldr: This article is a biography of Thomas Smith, a prominent figure in the field of optics. Smith made significant contributions to optical science, including the development of characteristic function theory and the optical cosine theorem. He also played a key role in the creation of the internationally accepted C.I.E. system for colorimetry. Smith's career spanned various roles in the optical and physical societies, and he authored numerous scientific papers on various aspects of optics.
