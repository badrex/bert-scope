The first species, called the soft-shelled turtle, has a unique shell structure and is found in fresh water. The second species, named the tuberculated tortoise, has a distinctive appearance with tubercles covering its back and belly. Both species are analyzed in terms of their anatomy, habitat, and potential culinary value.
