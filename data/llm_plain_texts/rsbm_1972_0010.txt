tldr: This biographical memoir recounts the life and scientific achievements of Geoffrey Wingfield Harris, a prominent endocrinologist. Harris made significant contributions to the understanding of the pituitary gland and hypothalamus, particularly in the study of the interaction between the brain and the pituitary gland. His work on the nervous pathway from the hypothalamus to the anterior lobe of the pituitary gland and the role of hormones in the body helped shape the field of endocrinology.
