tldr: This article is a biography of David Randall Pye, a scientist, engineer, and administrator who made significant contributions to aeronautical research and engineering science, particularly in the field of internal combustion engines. Pye's career spanned various roles, including building up the scientific research organization of the Air Ministry during World War I and II, and serving as Provost of University College, London, where he played a crucial role in its post-war recovery and development.
