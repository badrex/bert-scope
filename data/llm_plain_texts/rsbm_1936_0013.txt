tldr: Friedrich August Ferdinand Christian Went was a Dutch botanist and plant physiologist who made significant contributions to the fields of plant tropisms, plant genetics, and plant pathology. He was a professor of botany at the University of Utrecht and played a crucial role in the development of experimental station systems in Dutch East India. Went's research focused on the effects of external factors on plant growth and movement, and he is best known for his work on plant tropisms and the discovery of the growth substance, auxin.
