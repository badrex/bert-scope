tldr: This article is a biography of Frederic Calland Williams, a pioneering engineer in radar and electronics. Williams made significant contributions to the development of IFF (Identification Friend or Foe) systems during World War II and was instrumental in the creation of the operational amplifier, a foundational component in modern electronics. His work in radar technology and electronics has had a lasting impact on the field.
