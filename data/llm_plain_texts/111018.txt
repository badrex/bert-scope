tldr: The author conducted a detailed dissection of the young heifer's heart, focusing on the distribution of ganglia and nerves. They found ninety ganglia on the anterior surface and observed that these ganglia accompanied the coronary arteries throughout the heart's walls. The author believes that these findings provide a more comprehensive understanding of the heart's nervous system.
