tldr: This article introduces a Discussion Meeting on the magnetosphere, high-latitude ionosphere, and their interactions. It highlights the importance of studying these phenomena and their relevance to the centenary of Sydney Chapman's birth. Chapman made significant contributions to the field, including proposing much of its nomenclature. The meeting aims to discuss recent scientific studies at high latitudes using ground-based and space techniques.
