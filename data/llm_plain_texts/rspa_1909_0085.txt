tldr: The author investigates the change in hue of spectrum colors when mixed with white light. They found that the addition of white light causes a shift in hue towards yellow in the red, orange, and green parts of the spectrum, while the blue-green and violet hues remain relatively unchanged. This study provides insights into color perception and discrepancies observed in color descriptions.
