tldr: This article discusses the recent advancements in heavy electrical plant, specifically ultra-high voltage transmission. The adoption of ultra-high voltage transmission is encouraged by factors such as the energy crisis, the need for nuclear power generation, and the growth of strong transmission ties between regions. The research focuses on addressing design problems and investigating the electrical and environmental aspects of 1000 to 1500 transmission.
