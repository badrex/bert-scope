tldr: This article provides a detailed description and analysis of the manuscripts of Nicolaus Mercator, a 17th-century mathematician and astronomer. The manuscripts, found in Sheffield University Library, offer valuable insights into Mercator's life, work, and correspondence with other notable figures of the time. The article highlights Mercator's contributions to mathematical and astronomical fields, as well as his personal struggles to make a living. The manuscripts also provide potential leads for locating additional Mercator manuscripts.
