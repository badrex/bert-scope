tldr: In 1758, Camillo Paderni, the keeper of the Museum at Herculaneum, reported significant discoveries of ancient artifacts, including statues, paintings, and writings. He also described an earthquake that shook the region, with Mount Vesuvius showing signs of activity. Despite the danger, Paderni chose to stay with his family in Portici.
