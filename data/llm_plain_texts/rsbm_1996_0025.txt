tldr: 'Salimuzzaman Siddiqui was a versatile and accomplished scientist, artist, and philosopher. He made significant contributions to natural product chemistry, isolating pharmacologically active compounds from plants in the Indian subcontinent. Siddiqui was also a skilled painter, recognized as the last representative of the uncorrupted period of the Mughal School of painting. His diverse interests and talents made him a true Renaissance man of science and art.'
