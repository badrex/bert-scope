tldr: This obituary highlights the life and work of George Poulett Thomson, a prominent geologist who contributed significantly to the understanding of volcanology and the geological history of the Earth. Thomson's studies and explorations led him to support the Huttonian doctrine and oppose other geological theories of his time. He was a close friend and collaborator with Charles Lyell, another influential geologist, and their work laid the foundation for modern geological research.
