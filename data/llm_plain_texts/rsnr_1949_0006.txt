tldr: This article discusses an unpublished letter from Dr. Seth Ward, a fellow of the Oxford Philosophical Society, to Sir Justinian Isham. The letter provides insights into the early activities of the Oxford Philosophical Society and its members, including John Wilkins, Robert Boyle, Thomas Willis, Jonathan Goddard, and John Wallis. The society played a significant role in the formation of the Royal Society, and Ward's letter offers a glimpse into the intellectual environment of early modern science.
