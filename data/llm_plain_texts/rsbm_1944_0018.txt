tldr: Hugh Frank Newall was a pioneering astrophysicist who established astrophysics as a university study. Born into a family with a strong scientific background, he was the first Professor of Astrophysics at Cambridge. Newall's work focused on building and establishing astrophysics as a field of study, and he contributed to various scientific projects, including eclipse expeditions and telescope design. His dedication to science and passion for travel shaped his life and career, leaving a lasting impact on the field of astronomy.
