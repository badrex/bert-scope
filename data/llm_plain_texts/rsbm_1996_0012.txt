tldr: 'Donald Olding Hebb was a Canadian psychologist and neuroscientist who made significant contributions to the field of psychology. He is best known for his "Hebbian theory," which posits that neurons that fire together, wire together, leading to the formation of neural pathways and the basis of learning and memory. Hebb's work has had a profound influence on the fields of psychology, neuroscience, and cognitive science.'
