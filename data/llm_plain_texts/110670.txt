tldr: This article presents a meteorological register kept at Port Arthur, Van Diemen's Land, during the year 1838. The data was collected by Captain Beaufort, R.N., F.R.S., and the instrument was placed at a height of 57 feet 7 inches above sea level until August 21st, and then lowered to 3 feet for the rest of the year. The register provides valuable information about the local climate and weather patterns.
