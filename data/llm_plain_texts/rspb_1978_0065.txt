tldr: The author examines the significance of the ligament in the classification of Bivalvia, a group of mollusks. They explain the structure and function of the ligament, which connects the two calcareous valves of Bivalvia, and its role in the evolution and adaptation of these animals. The study of the ligament leads to a better understanding of the classification and the various forms and functions of the ligament in different Bivalvia species.
