tldr: Thomas Lewis was a renowned medical scientist who contributed significantly to the field of physiology and clinical medicine. He established clinical science as a vital branch of medical knowledge and conducted groundbreaking research on the pulse, respiration, and irregular heart actions. His work with James Mackenzie led to the use of the string galvanometer in studying the heart's electric activity, paving the way for further advancements in cardiology.
