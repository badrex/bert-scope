tldr: In this article, Joseph Whidbey and William Clift describe the discovery of fossil bones of hyenas, wolves, and foxes in limestone quarries at Oreston. They discuss the location and state of the bones, as well as the geological context of the caves. The authors also mention the analysis and comparison of these fossils with those of recent animals, and their findings contribute to the understanding of the animals' history and evolution.
