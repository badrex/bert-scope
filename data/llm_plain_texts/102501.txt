tldr: Dr. William Musgrave conducted an experiment on a dog to demonstrate the importance of respiration in maintaining blood circulation. By stopping the dog's trachea and observing the stagnation of blood in the lungs, Musgrave concluded that respiration promotes the passage of blood through the lungs, making it a crucial necessity for life.
