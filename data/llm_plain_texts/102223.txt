tldr: This article discusses the murrain outbreak in Switzerland and its impact on cattle. The author describes the symptoms, transmission, and methods of treatment for the disease. The treatment involves examining the tongue of infected animals, using a silver instrument to cause bleeding, and applying a lotion made of salt and vinegar. The author also mentions the possibility of the contagion being carried by some volatile insects and the need for further research on the subject.
