tldr: This article presents a record of barometer, thermometer, and rain data collected in Lyndon, South Lambeth, Selbourn, and Fyfield from 1784 to 1785. The author, Thomas Barker, documents the weather patterns and severe frost experienced during this period, as well as the impact on crops and the environment. The data provides valuable insights into historical weather conditions and the effects of climate on agriculture and ecosystems.
