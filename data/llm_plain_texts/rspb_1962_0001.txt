Powell, Sir Wilfrid Le Gros Clark, and Professor D. H. R. Barton. Krebs is recognized for his work on the citric acid cycle and the glyoxylate cycle, Powell for his contributions to cosmic rays and fundamental particles, Le Gros Clark for his research on primate evolution and neuroanatomy, and Barton for his work on the chemistry of natural products and organic synthesis.
