tldr: Archibald Barr was a distinguished engineer and educator who made significant contributions to engineering education and the development of optical, mechanical, and electrical instruments. He established well-equipped engineering laboratories at the University of Leeds and Glasgow University, and co-founded the company Barr & Stroud, which manufactured specialized instruments for warfare. Barr's work on rangefinders and other military instruments had a lasting impact on the field of engineering.
