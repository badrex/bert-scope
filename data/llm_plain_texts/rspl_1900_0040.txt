tldr: The authors describe their observations and experiences during the total solar eclipse of 1900. They detail the preparations, instruments, and personnel involved in the expedition from England to Bouzareah, near the Algiers Observatory. The authors also discuss the meteorological conditions, instruments used, and the assistance provided during the eclipse. The observations made during this expedition contributed to the understanding of solar eclipses and the study of the sun's corona.
