tldr: The author, George Gulliver, observed the blood corpuscles of different deer species and discovered various shapes and forms of blood cells. These unique shapes include crescents, segments of a circle, commas, triangles, and lances. The blood corpuscles maintain their shape when placed in saline solutions.
