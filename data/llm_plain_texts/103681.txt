tldr: This article presents a detailed account of the bills of mortality in several European towns, including Breslaw, Vienna, Dresden, and Berlin, for the years 1722 and 1723. The author, Sir Conrad Spengell, provides a comprehensive list of births, deaths, and marriages, as well as demographic data such as age and gender. The data highlights the differences in mortality rates and population growth across various towns and regions in Europe during that time period.
