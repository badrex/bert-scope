tldr: A case study of a person who passed away due to a scirrhous tumor in the breast. The tumor was found to be an atheroma containing a pappy substance, and it obstructed the axillary and mamillary glands, causing induration of the surrounding muscles and breast tissue. The tumor was also found to be connected to the lungs, and there was a sink or pelvis in the axillary gland containing discolored matter. The patient had a history of jaundice, and the gallbladder was full of stones.
