tldr: This article describes the changes an egg undergoes during incubation in the common fowl, as observed through a microscope. The author, Everard Home, provides detailed illustrations of the development process, from the formation of the embryo to the growth of its organs and limbs. The findings highlight the differences between bird and quadruped egg development.
