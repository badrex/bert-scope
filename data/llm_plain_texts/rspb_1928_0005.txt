tldr: 'This study investigates the inheritance and survival rates of triplet children in Great Britain and Ireland. The research examines the Royal Bounty records of triplet births and measures the surviving children. The findings reveal differences in survival rates between boys and girls, as well as between different types of triplet births. The study also discusses the importance of obtaining complete data over a uniform range of kinship for accurate results.'
