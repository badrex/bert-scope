tldr: 'James Mercer, a prominent mathematician, passed away in 1932. Born in Bootle, Liverpool, he graduated as Senior Wrangler from Trinity College, Cambridge, in 1907. Mercer made significant contributions to the theory of summable series and integral equations. He was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society in 1922 and served as a Naval Instructor during World War I. His work and life are remembered fondly by the academic community.'
