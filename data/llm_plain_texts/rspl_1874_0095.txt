tldr: This article investigates the effects of iron masts on compasses in two wooden frigates, H.M.S. Undaunted and Newcastle. The study finds that the iron masts significantly affect the compass readings, causing deviations and magnetic disturbances. The author provides coefficients for the standard and steering compasses, highlighting the impact of the iron masts on navigation.
