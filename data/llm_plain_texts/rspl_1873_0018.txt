tldr: The author investigates the lymphatic system of the lungs, both in normal and pathological conditions. They describe the structure and function of the pleural, perivascular, and peribronchial lymphatics, and their role in the respiratory system. The study provides insights into the mechanisms of inflammation and the body's response to it.
