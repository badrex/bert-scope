tldr: This article discusses the concept of ritualization in humans and its relation to conceptual and social development. It explores the differences between the ethologist and social anthropologist's perspectives on ritual and emphasizes the importance of ritual as a communication system in non-literate societies. The author argues that ritual serves to perpetuate essential knowledge for the survival of the performers and highlights the distinction between cultural and natural processes.
