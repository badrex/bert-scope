tldr: Archibald Edward Garrod was a prominent medical researcher who made significant contributions to the understanding of inborn errors of metabolism. His work on alkaptonuria, albinism, and cystinuria helped establish the concept of genetic disorders and their impact on human health. Garrod's research laid the foundation for modern genetics and the study of metabolic disorders.
