The author, Edward King, provides detailed illustrations and observations of the insect's transparent body, fins, and reproductive organs. The insects are found in abundance in this specific location and seem to feed on minute organisms in the water. The author also mentions the extraordinary background of the insect's discoverer, a poor man with exceptional knowledge of nature.
