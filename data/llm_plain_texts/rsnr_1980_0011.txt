tldr: In 1979, the Royal Society elected five foreign members for their significant contributions to various fields. These members include Dr. Julius Axelrod for his work on pharmacology and biogenic amines, Professor Joshua Lederberg for his discoveries in molecular biology and immunology, Professor Jerzy Neyman for his contributions to statistical theory, Professor Yakov Borisovich Zeldovich for his work in chemical physics, detonation, and astrophysics.
