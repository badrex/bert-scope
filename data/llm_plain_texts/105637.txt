tldr: This article describes the observations made at the Cape of Good Hope by Charles Mason and Mr. Dixon, focusing on the use of reflecting telescopes and other instruments. They recorded data on the positions of celestial bodies, including the eclipse of the Sun, and made adjustments to their instruments to improve accuracy. The authors also provide details on the instruments used, such as the reflecting telescopes, quadrant, and astronomical clock.
