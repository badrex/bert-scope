tldr: The authors discuss the existence of more than one fungus in Madura disease, a chronic affection affecting the extremities, particularly the foot. They examine the melanoid and white varieties of the disease, focusing on the identification and study of the fungi involved. The authors also describe their methods for examining and identifying the fungi, including the use of caustic potash and Eau de Javelle as clearing agents.
