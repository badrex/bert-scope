tldr: Herbert Freundlich was a renowned colloid chemist who made significant contributions to the fields of colloid and interfacial phenomena. His work on colloid optics, electrokinetic potential, and mechanical properties of colloid systems helped advance our understanding of complex structures and activities in living organisms. Freundlich's dedication to research and his passion for music made him a respected and beloved figure in the scientific community.
