tldr: The Kew Committee's report for 1886 discusses the operations and findings of Kew Observatory, focusing on magnetic observations, meteorology, and astronomical research. The committee worked on improving instruments and techniques for measuring magnetic fields, atmospheric pressure, temperature, and other meteorological factors. They also conducted experiments with solar radiation thermometers and electrical anemographs, and collaborated with other institutions on various projects.
