tldr: This biographical memoir of George Philip Wells, a renowned zoologist and biologist, highlights his life, family background, and contributions to the field of biology. Wells, the son of famous author H.G. Wells, had a strong interest in living things and made significant contributions to our understanding of animal behavior and biology. He was a superb communicator and played a crucial role in advancing experimental biology in the United Kingdom.
