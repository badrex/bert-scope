tldr: The author discovered a new species of trypanosoma in the cerebrospinal fluid of sleeping sickness cases. The trypanosoma was found in 70% of the cases examined, and it did not resemble the species found in trypanosoma fever. This discovery could help in understanding the relationship between the trypanosoma and sleeping sickness.
