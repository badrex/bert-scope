tldr: This article describes the organic remains found in two fields near Brentford, Middlesex, and analyzes the strata in which they were discovered. The author, William Kirby Trimmer, provides a detailed account of the different layers of soil and the fossils found within them, including bones, shells, and teeth of various animals. The study contributes to the understanding of the geological history of the area and the preservation of these remains.
