tldr: This article discusses the current status and demands of semiconductor tunnelling devices in terahertz systems, focusing on applications in satellite remote sensing and astronomy. The author highlights the need for advances in device technology, particularly in local oscillator sources and mixer conversion loss, to improve the sensitivity and efficiency of these systems.
