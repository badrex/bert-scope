tldr: The author explores the history of time measurement from sundials and water clocks to atomic clocks, discussing the contributions of art and science in the development of timekeeping. They focus on three major advances: the invention of the mechanical clock, the development of precision timekeeping, and the introduction of high-frequency timekeeping. The author also examines the role of clockmakers and astronomers in the evolution of time measurement and the impact of social and urban development on the demand for accurate timekeeping.
