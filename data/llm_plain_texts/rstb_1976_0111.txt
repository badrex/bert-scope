tldr: The study examines the organization and location of motoneurone somata in the prothoracic ganglion of the cockroach, using the axonal iontophoresis technique. The research identifies and maps the somata of motoneurones in 15 different peripheral nerves, providing insights into the neuronal organization and homology in the cockroach's thoracic ganglia.
