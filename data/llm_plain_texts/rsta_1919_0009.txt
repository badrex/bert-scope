tldr: The author describes the magnetic re-survey of the British Isles conducted for the epoch of January 1, 1915. The survey aimed to determine the main magnetic features of the British Isles, including the locations of 200 points chosen by Rucker and Thorpe. The author used a Kew Unifilar and a Dover Dip-Circle for the measurements and collaborated with the Ordnance Survey Department to mark the stations permanently. The survey covered the mainland of Scotland, England, Wales, Ireland, Orkney, and Sky.
