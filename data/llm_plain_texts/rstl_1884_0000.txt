tldr: The Royal Society clarifies its role in the publication of the Philosophical Transactions, explaining that they do not endorse or approve the content of the papers, but rather select and publish them to showcase the variety of subjects and research being conducted. They emphasize that their role is to promote the improvement of knowledge and benefit of mankind, and they discourage crediting unverified reports and public notices of their meetings and approvals.
