tldr: The author, H. Miles, provides a detailed account of the earthquake that occurred in London and its surrounding areas on March 8, 1749. The earthquake was felt throughout the city and in nearby locations such as Merton, Mitcham, Croydon, Streatham, Clapham, Wandsworth, and Epsom. The author describes the shaking experienced, damage to buildings, and the lack of any reported lightning or noise associated with the event.
