tldr: Frederick William Keeble was a versatile biologist who made significant contributions to botany, plant physiology, zoology, and plant genetics. He conducted research on plant and animal color changes, the bionomics of flatworms, and the distribution of oxidases in plants. Keeble also played a crucial role in the development of the Wisley Gardens, focusing on solving scientific problems in horticulture. His work had a lasting impact on the field of biology.
