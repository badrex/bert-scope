tldr: This article discusses the historical context of Isaac Newton's groundbreaking work and its impact on the scientific community. It highlights the shift from the Aristotelian worldview to the experimental method, the influence of Descartes' philosophical system, and the establishment of the Royal Society. Newton's achievements, including the invalidation of the Cartesian system and the development of the laws of motion, significantly transformed the landscape of science during his time.
