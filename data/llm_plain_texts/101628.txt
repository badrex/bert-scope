tldr: This article provides a review of the first year's observations and experiments conducted by the Academy of Curious Inquirers into Nature, a group of German philosophers. The collection includes various medical, anatomical, botanical, and chemical observations, as well as experiments on snakes, stones, horns, and other natural phenomena. The article aims to share a taste of the curious findings from this group's work.
