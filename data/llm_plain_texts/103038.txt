tldr: In this collection of letters, Cotton Mather shares his observations and experiments on various plants in America, their medicinal properties, and their effects on human health. He also discusses natural phenomena such as birds, antipathies, and the force of imagination, as well as the effects of thunder and lightning. Mather's work highlights the rich natural history of America and the potential uses of its plants for medicinal purposes.
