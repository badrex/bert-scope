tldr: Louis Napoleon George Filon was a French-born mathematician and educator who made significant contributions to the fields of applied mathematics, mathematical physics, and geometry. He was a student of Karl Pearson and Micaiah Hill and later became a lecturer in pure and applied mathematics at the University of London. Filon was known for his passion for research, his dedication to teaching, and his efforts to promote academic freedom and the development of teaching and research in the University of London.
