tldr: William Bernard Robinson King was a distinguished geologist and military geologist who made significant contributions to the fields of stratigraphy, hydrogeology, and military operations. He served in both World War I and World War II, where he provided crucial advice on water supply, construction materials, and site selection for airfields and military operations. King's work in the field of geology and his dedication to military service made him a respected figure in both scientific and military communities.
