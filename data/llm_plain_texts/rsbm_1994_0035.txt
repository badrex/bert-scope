tldr: 'Edwin Herbert Land was a renowned inventor and contributor in the fields of polarized light, photography, and colour vision. He founded the Polaroid Corporation and developed cameras and films that produced instant dry pictures. Land's innovations in polarized light, such as the first sheet polarizers, have been widely used in liquid crystal displays, sunglasses, and scientific research. His work has had a significant impact on everyday life and the advancement of natural science.'
