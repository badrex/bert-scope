tldr: The author observed the spectrum of the star Nova Persei and analyzed its changes over time. They discovered that the bright lines in the spectrum were broad and difficult to measure accurately. The author found a connection between the lines in Nova Persei's spectrum and the enhanced lines of iron in other stars, suggesting a similarity in their spectra. This study provided insights into the nature of Novae and their relationship to other stars.
