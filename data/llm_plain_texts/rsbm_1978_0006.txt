tldr: This biographical memoir highlights the life and work of Howard Everest Hinton, a renowned entomologist known for his contributions to taxonomy, functional morphology, and natural history. Hinton's career included positions at the British Museum (Natural History) and the University of Bristol, where he published numerous papers and books on insects. He also played a significant role in founding the Journal of Insect Physiology and Insect Biochemistry. Hinton's dedication to his work and passion for entomology left a lasting impact on the field.
