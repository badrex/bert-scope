tldr: James Glaisher conducted extensive experiments to measure the amount of heat radiation from the Earth and various objects at night. He used accurate thermometers and recorded data from over ten thousand observations at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich. This research provided valuable insights into the relationship between heat radiation, temperature, and environmental factors.
