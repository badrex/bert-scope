tldr: This article presents various accounts of the Aurora Borealis observed in different parts of Europe on October 19, 1726. The descriptions provide details about the time, location, and appearance of the phenomenon, helping to estimate its extent and height. The observations were made by several individuals, including Mons. Gaudin, Mons. Maraldi, Francesco Quaranbotti, and an anonymous writer from Florence.
