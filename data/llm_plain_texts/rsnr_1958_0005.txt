tldr: This article highlights the life and work of Sir John Lubbock, a prominent figure in the field of zoology during the 19th century. Despite his significant contributions to the understanding of insect behavior, color vision, and the study of insects, his work was largely forgotten after the 1914-1918 war. The article aims to bring attention to Lubbock's achievements and the impact he had on the scientific community.
