tldr: The Annual Meeting of the Royal Society took place on June 9, 1887, to elect new fellows. The meeting was chaired by President G. G. Stokes. Dr. J. H. Gladstone and Mr. G. J. Symons were nominated as scrutators to help examine the lists of candidates. The following candidates were elected as fellows: Buchanan, Cash, Douglass, Ewing, Forbes, Gowers, Kennedy, King, Kirk, Lodge, and Whitaker. The Society thanked the scrutators and admitted new members, including the Right Hon. the Earl of Rosebery and Mr. H. C. Russell.
