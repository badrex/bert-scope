tldr: The author reports on the tidal disturbances caused by the volcanic eruptions at Java in August 27 and 28, 1883, and the propagation of the "super-tidal" waves. The study examines the effects of the eruptions on sea levels and the impact of the waves on various coastal locations. The author provides detailed information on the time and height of each appreciable "super-tidal" wave, as well as the intervals between them.
