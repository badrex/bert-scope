tldr: This article examines the relationship between rainfall and sunspot area using data from the Great Trigonometrical Survey of India. The author finds that the Meldrum theory, which suggests a connection between sunspot area and rainfall, is generally supported by the data. The author also discusses the importance of considering solar conditions in understanding various global circumstances.
