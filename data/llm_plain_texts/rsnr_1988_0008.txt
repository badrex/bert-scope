tldr: This article explores the impact of Newton's Principia on British physics, particularly in the 19th century. It discusses the evolution of mathematical physics from geometrical to analytical methods and how British physicists, such as William Thomson and James Clerk Maxwell, perceived and built upon Newton's ideas. The article highlights the importance of Newton's laws of motion and the concept of energy in shaping the scientific landscape of the time.
