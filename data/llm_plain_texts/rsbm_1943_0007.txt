tldr: This article is an obituary for John Norman Collie, a prominent chemist and educator. Collie was known for his expertise in glass-blowing and analytical chemistry, as well as his contributions to the study of spectroscopy. He worked closely with William Ramsay and made significant advancements in the field of chemistry during his career.
