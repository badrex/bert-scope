tldr: The author, Capt. Newbold, analyzed the chemical composition of India's black cotton soil, known as Regar. The soil consists of silica, lime, alumina, oxide of iron, and small amounts of vegetable and animal debris. The study aimed to understand the properties and potential uses of this unique soil type.
