tldr: The Solomon Islands are mostly covered in tropical rainforest, with a climate influenced by young, unstable landscapes, very wet conditions, and centuries of human disturbance and natural catastrophes. The study describes the main features of the vegetation and its relationship to the climate, as well as the impact of human activities and natural disasters on the islands' ecosystems.
