tldr: This article discusses the opportunities and knowledge required to improve animal breeding technology. It assesses the current rate of genetic improvement and explores the potential for increasing the rate of genetic change through multiple ovulation and embryo transfer schemes, as well as the use of new knowledge to facilitate the recognition of genetic merit. The article also highlights the importance of a social framework to ensure the application of knowledge to technology for long-term welfare and environmental benefits.
