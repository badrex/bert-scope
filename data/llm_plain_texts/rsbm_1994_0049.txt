tldr: Yakov Borissovich Zel'dovich was a renowned Soviet physicist and astrophysicist who made significant contributions to various fields, including catalysis, phase transitions, hydrodynamics, combustion and detonation theory, nuclear chain reactions, nuclear physics, and cosmology. His work in astrophysics, particularly the application of the general theory of relativity, helped shape our understanding of the universe. Zel'dovich's life and career are celebrated in this biographical memoir, which highlights his numerous achievements and the impact he had on the scientific community.
