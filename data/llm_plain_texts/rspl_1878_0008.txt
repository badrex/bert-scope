tldr: This article presents an experimental investigation of the repulsion resulting from radiation on various substances. The author uses a torsion apparatus to measure the amount of repulsion produced by radiation on disks coated with different substances. The results are compared and analyzed, providing insights into the effects of radiation on different materials and their potential applications.
