The book aims to address the underrepresentation of women in scientific biographies and histories of science. It covers a wide range of topics, including the education and contributions of women in fields such as biology, mathematics, and chemistry. The review highlights the book's informative and stimulating nature in shedding light on the often overlooked role of women in the development of scientific culture.
