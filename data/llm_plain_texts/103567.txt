tldr: A detailed account of a case where a patient suffered severe arm injuries, leading to the eventual amputation of the arm. The author, Mr. Peter Derante, describes the treatment process, including the use of cauterization and the application of a solution of silver. The case study highlights the challenges faced by surgeons in the 18th century and the importance of proper wound care.
