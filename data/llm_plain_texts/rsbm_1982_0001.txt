tldr: Charles Herbert Best was a Canadian medical researcher who co-discovered insulin with Frederick Grant Banting. Best's early life and family background influenced his interest in medicine, and he later studied at the University of Toronto. His research focused on diabetes, and his work with Banting led to the development of insulin, which revolutionized diabetes treatment. Best's contributions to medical science have had a significant impact on the lives of millions of people with diabetes worldwide.
