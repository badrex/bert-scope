tldr: This article discusses two portraits of Joseph Priestley, a famous scientist, painted by James Millar in 1789 and 1797. The portraits were recently discovered and are now being considered for placement in the National Portrait Gallery and the Royal Society. The article provides a detailed description of the portraits and the artist, James Millar, who exhibited at the Royal Academy from 1784 to 1790.
