tldr: In 1727, an earthquake struck New England, causing significant damage and shaking in the region. The author, Benjamin Colman, provides a detailed account of the event, including the sudden onset, the shaking experienced, and the aftermath. He also discusses the lack of any noticeable changes in the air or water after the earthquake and the absence of any further ground openings or eruptions in the area.
