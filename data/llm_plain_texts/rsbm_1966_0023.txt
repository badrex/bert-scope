tldr: 'Wilson Smith was a dedicated scientist who made significant contributions to the fields of virology and bacteriology. He discovered the WS strain of the influenza virus, which is still studied in laboratories worldwide. Smith also played a crucial role in establishing international standards for antipneumococcal sera and worked on biological standardization methods. His life and career were marked by a strong commitment to research and the training of young scientists.'
