He made significant contributions to the field of organic chemistry and was an inspiring teacher. Armstrong's life was deeply rooted in his Lewisham home and the natural surroundings of the Garden of England. His dual approach to life, combining intellect and emotion, allowed him to see things others often overlooked and made him a unique and influential figure in the scientific community.'
