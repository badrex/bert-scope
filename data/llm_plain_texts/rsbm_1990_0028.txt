tldr: This article is a biography of Henry Gilman, an outstanding organic chemist and one of the leading figures in the field of organometallic chemistry. Born in Boston in 1893, Gilman studied at Harvard University and later worked with renowned chemists such as E.P. Kohler and Roger Adams. He made significant contributions to the understanding of Grignard reagents and their reactions, and his work had a lasting impact on the field of organic chemistry. Gilman also had a successful career at Iowa State University, where he trained many students who went on to achieve great success in academia and industry.
