tldr: This article discusses the physics and chemistry of hydrogen in materials, focusing on muon implantation studies in organic conductors. The authors explore the interaction of muons with polymer chains, the formation of diamagnetic and paramagnetic states, and the role of muons in charge transfer salts and fullerenes. The research provides insights into the behavior of organic conductors and their potential applications in various materials.
