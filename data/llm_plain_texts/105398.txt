tldr: In 1759, Dr. Patrick Russell reported on a series of earthquakes in Syria that caused widespread panic and damage to buildings. The most severe earthquake struck on October 30th, causing significant damage in Damascus, Antioch, Tripoli, Sidon, Acre, and other cities along the Syrian coast. The author provides detailed accounts of the earthquakes' impact on various cities and discusses the aftermath, including the ongoing fear and uncertainty among the population.
