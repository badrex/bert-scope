tldr: The article reports on the Anniversary Meeting of the Royal Society of London held on November 30, 1901, and the subsequent meeting on December 5, 1901. The meeting featured presentations on various topics, including the spontaneous ionization of gases. The full report of the meeting, along with the President's address and the council's report, can be found in the 1902 Year-book. Mr. Hector Munro Macdonald was admitted into the Society during the meeting.
