It discusses the differences in herbivory and defense mechanisms between marine and terrestrial environments, as well as the role of secondary metabolites in deterring herbivores. The article also explores the impact of toxins on phytoplankton and zooplankton populations, emphasizing the unpredictability of these interactions.
