tldr: This article discusses the advancements in the field of organic chemistry, particularly in natural product chemistry and the study of organic phosphates. It highlights the importance of understanding the structure and function of organic compounds found in living organisms, and how this knowledge has led to significant discoveries in fields such as biochemistry and genetics. The author also explores the role of organic phosphates in energy transfer and various biological processes, and how their study has contributed to our understanding of the chemistry of nucleic acids, terpenoids, steroids, and coenzymes.
