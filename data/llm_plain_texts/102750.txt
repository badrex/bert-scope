tldr: This article presents Reverend Mr. Derham's observations of the weather and climate for the year 1699. He provides detailed records of temperature, cloud patterns, wind direction, and rainfall, using a thermometer graduated in inches and tenths of inches. Derham also discusses the implications of his observations on the fertility, scarcity, healthiness, and diseases of the region.
