tldr: Dr. Walter Needham provides annotations on Monsieur Pecquet's discovery of a connection between the Ductus Thoracicus and the Inferior Vena Cava. Needham discusses the function of the Ductus Thoracicus in the mixing of blood and chyle, and examines the role of the lymphatic system in facilitating this process. He also critiques the methods and conclusions of Pecquet's experiments.
