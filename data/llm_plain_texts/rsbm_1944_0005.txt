tldr: Warrington Yorke was a prominent figure in tropical medicine, working at the Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine for 36 years. He conducted research on various tropical diseases, including blackwater fever, trypanosomiasis, and malaria. Yorke's work contributed to the understanding of these diseases and their impact on human and animal health in tropical regions.
