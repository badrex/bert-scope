tldr: The Incorporated Kew Committee reports on the operations and findings of Kew Observatory in 1894. The committee discusses various magnetic observations, meteorological data, and improvements to instruments. They also mention their involvement with the Jackson-Harmsworth Polar Expedition and the Royal Meteorological Society. The report covers topics such as magnetic disturbances, atmospheric pressure, temperature, wind, sunshine, and sunspots.
