tldr: The 1911 total solar eclipse was observed by an expedition from the Joint Permanent Eclipse Committee in Vavau, Tonga Islands. They used various instruments, including coronagraphs and a quartz spectroscope, to study the solar corona and spectrum. The observations provided valuable data for understanding the behavior of the sun and its corona during a total solar eclipse.
