tldr: 'Thomas Charles Robinson invented a compact mountain barometer that can be divided into two portions for safer and more convenient transport. The device consists of a glass tube connected to a steel cistern with an internal screw. The mercury can be easily transferred between the tube and cistern, allowing for easy carrying in any position.'
