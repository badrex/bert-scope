tldr: This article discusses the life and achievements of Hertha Ayrton, a pioneering woman in science who made significant contributions to the fields of electrical engineering and physics. Ayrton was the first woman to be proposed for Fellowship in the Royal Society and the only woman to receive the Society's Hughes Medal. She conducted groundbreaking research on the electric arc and sand ripples, which is still respected today.
