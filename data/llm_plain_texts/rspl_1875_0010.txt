tldr: The Kew Committee's report for the year ending October 31, 1875, details the progress and findings of various scientific studies conducted at the Royal Society's Kew Observatory. These include magnetic work with magnetographs, meteorological studies with self-recording instruments, and the testing and verification of numerous instruments. The report also discusses the development of new methods for measuring solar radiation and the improvement of existing instruments.
