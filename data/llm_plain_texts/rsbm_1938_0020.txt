tldr: Sir Henry Capel Lofft Holden was a distinguished scientist and engineer who made significant contributions to artillery, explosives testing, and early motor vehicles. He introduced scientific methods to the Royal Gun Factory and Royal Carriage Factory, and his work in developing and implementing new technologies had a lasting impact on English gunnery and military transport. Holden was also a pioneer motorist and a founder member of the Royal Automobile Club.
