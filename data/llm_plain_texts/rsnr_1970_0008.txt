tldr: The article discusses the life and scientific achievements of Martinus van Marum, a prominent Dutch scientist in the 18th century. Van Marum made significant contributions to various fields of science, including electrical science, chemistry, and crystallography. He was a member of the Hollandsche Maatschappij der Wetenschappen and played a crucial role in promoting scientific exchange between the commercial and scientific worlds in Haarlem. His influence and connections helped him become a member of numerous scientific societies across Europe.
