tldr: 'Keith Stewartson was a brilliant applied mathematician and fluid dynamics expert who made significant contributions to the field of theoretical fluid mechanics. He had a successful career at the University of Bristol and later at the University of Durham, where he held the Chair of Applied Mathematics. Stewartson was also instrumental in the formation of the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications, serving as one of its Vice-Presidents.'
