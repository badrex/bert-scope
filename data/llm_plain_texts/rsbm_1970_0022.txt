tldr: 'Ernest Basil Verney was a prominent British physiologist known for his significant contributions to the field of renal physiology. He worked closely with E. H. Starling and conducted groundbreaking research on the kidney, leading to the discovery of the role of the pituitary gland in controlling water and chloride output in the bloodstream. Verney's work laid the foundation for modern nephrology and endocrinology.'
