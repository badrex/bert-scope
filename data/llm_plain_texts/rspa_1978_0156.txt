tldr: The 1975-76 drought in England and Wales significantly affected river systems, with varying severity in different locations. This paper examines the factors contributing to low river flows, including precipitation, evaporation, catchment characteristics, and soil moisture deficit. It also discusses the importance of understanding these factors for managing rivers during droughts and designing water resource schemes.
