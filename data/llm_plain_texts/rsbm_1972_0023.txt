tldr: The article discusses the life and achievements of Otto Heinrich Warburg, a renowned biologist and biochemist who made significant contributions to cell biology, biochemistry, and cancer research. Warburg discovered that cancer cells have an increased oxygen consumption and identified the structures now known as mitochondria as the "power plant" of the cell. His work laid the foundation for understanding the fundamental aspects of cell respiration and energy transformations in living organisms.
