tldr: John Gillies was a Scottish botanist and traveller who explored South America in the early 19th century. Despite being well-known among his contemporaries, Gillies is now largely forgotten. He conducted extensive botanical research in Argentina and Chile, collecting and sending plant specimens back to his friends in England. His work contributed to the understanding of South American flora and the scientific community of his time.
