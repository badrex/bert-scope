tldr: This article discusses the observations and analysis of the first Russian artificial earth satellite by the Post Office Engineering Department. The study focused on frequency and field-strength measurements on 20 and 40 Mc/s to provide information for organizations concerned with orbit determination and to increase knowledge of radio propagation. The authors present typical results and discuss the influence of the ionosphere on radio waves emitted by the satellite.
