tldr: The author presents preliminary reports on oceanic deposits and sea-bottoms collected during the "Challenger" expedition. The reports describe the nature and distribution of various samples, including deep-sea clays, manganese nodules, and remains of surface animals. The author also discusses the methods used for analyzing and categorizing the samples, focusing on the presence of pelagic Foraminifera and other organisms.
