tldr: Victor Christian William Cavendish, the Duke of Devonshire, led a life of service and achievement in various fields. He was a successful politician, serving as Treasurer of the Household, Financial Secretary to the Treasury, Civil Lord of the Admiralty, and Governor-General of Canada. He was also an enthusiastic and successful breeder of Shire horses and a strong supporter of agriculture. In addition, he played a significant role in the University of Leeds, serving as its Chancellor for many years. The Duke was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society in 1914 and valued his family's long connection with Cambridge University.
