tldr: This study investigates the compressibility of aqueous solutions, focusing on the effects of adding various electrolytes and non-electrolytes to water. The research aimed to improve the understanding of solutions' nature and provide accurate calculations of osmotic pressures. The authors used a specialized compressibility apparatus to measure the compressibility of different solutions, including aqueous sucrose solutions, and compared their results with previous studies.
