tldr: 'The author presents a detailed account of the molluscan fauna collected at Rodriguez Island during the Transit of Venus Expeditions in 1874-75. The collection includes 78 marine species, 16 terrestrial forms, and six fluviatile species. The author discusses the various species, their characteristics, and their distribution, highlighting the similarities and differences with other regions. The study contributes to the understanding of the island's molluscan fauna and their ecological context.'
