tldr: Charles Samuel Myers was a prominent figure in psychology and anthropology, known for his contributions to experimental psychology and social anthropology. He was a part of the Cambridge Anthropological Expedition to the Torres Straits, where he conducted experimental observations on sensory reactions of the native population. Myers later became a reader in experimental psychology at Cambridge and played a significant role in establishing the field in the university. He also contributed to the British Journal of Psychology and reorganized the British Psychological Society.
