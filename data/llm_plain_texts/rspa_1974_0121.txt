tldr: This article discusses the geological setting of useful minerals in Britain and the conflict between exploiting these resources for industrial purposes and countryside conservation. It covers the various geological groupings and their mineral deposits, as well as the historical and structural geology that determines the geography of these deposits. The author also addresses the ecological effects of mineral extraction and the need for conservation efforts.
