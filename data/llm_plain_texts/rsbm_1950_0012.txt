tldr: Johan Hjort was a renowned oceanographer and fisheries researcher who made significant contributions to the field. He discovered vast stocks of deep-sea prawns, led the development of the Norwegian fisheries industry, and pioneered the use of statistical methods to study fish stocks. Hjort's influence on oceanography and fisheries research has been lasting and profound, inspiring many governments to invest in marine research and resource management.
