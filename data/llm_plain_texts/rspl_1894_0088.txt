tldr: The Annual Meeting of the Royal Society took place on June 7, 1894, to elect new fellows. The Statutes were read, and Sir Erasmus Ommanney and Mr. Scott were nominated as Scrutators to help the Secretaries examine the lists. The votes of the present fellows were collected, and the following candidates were declared elected: Bateson, William, and Boulenger, George Albert.
