tldr: The study examines the effects of industrial effluent from pulp and paper mills on the marine benthic environment. It discusses the types of effluent discharged, their impact on the benthic environment, and the results of the Lochs Linnhe-Eil survey. The research highlights the importance of understanding the ecological and physiological reasons behind the varied faunal successions that occur under conditions of pollution stress.
