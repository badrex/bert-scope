tldr: The author, Hans Sloane, discusses the use of Nux Vomica seeds for water clarification. These seeds come from the East Indies and are used to purify muddy or foul water. Dr. Brown explains the process of rubbing or grating the seeds and mixing them with water, which is then added to the impure water to clarify it. Nux Vomica seeds are also highly regarded in the Philippine Islands for treating various ailments.
