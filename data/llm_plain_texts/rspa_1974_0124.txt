tldr: This article discusses the historical and current exploitation of British iron ores, their geological characteristics, and the challenges and opportunities for their future use. The author highlights the decline in the use of home iron ores due to changes in steelmaking and transportation practices, and the rise of imported iron ores. The article also examines the major iron ore fields in Britain and their geological features, as well as the potential for ore beneficiation and land restoration associated with ironstone mining.
