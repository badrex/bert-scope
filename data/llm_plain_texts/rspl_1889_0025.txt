tldr: This article reports on the effects of contact metamorphism on Silurian rocks near the town of New Galloway in the Southern Uplands of Scotland. The research examines the changes in mineral composition and structure of the rocks due to the intrusion of granite. The study provides insights into the geological processes and the effects of contact metamorphism on sedimentary rocks.
