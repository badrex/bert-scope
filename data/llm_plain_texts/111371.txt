tldr: The author, John Denis Macdonald, discovered a new species of polyzoon on the Australian coast. This polyzoon is related to Vesicularia and has unique characteristics, such as transparent, plant-like structures and a vascular system. The polyps in this species have eight ciliated tentacula, which is different from Bowerbankia, another related genus.
