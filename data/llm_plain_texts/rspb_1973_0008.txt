These mechanisms include facilitated diffusion, active transport, and group translocation, which is coupled with phosphorylation. The author provides examples of various microorganisms and their specific uptake systems for different types of carbohydrates, highlighting the importance of understanding these processes for studying microbial metabolism and biotechnology applications.
