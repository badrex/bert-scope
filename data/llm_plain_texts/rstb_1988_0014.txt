tldr: This article discusses the current status and future prospects of biological control agents (BCAs) in pest management. It examines the advantages and disadvantages of BCAs, including their reliability, patentability, and cost-effectiveness compared to conventional control techniques. The authors also explore the potential market for BCAs and the challenges they face in commercialization. While BCAs currently account for only 1% of the global crop protection market, industry believes that biotechnology will play a significant role in improving their usefulness and increasing their market share.
