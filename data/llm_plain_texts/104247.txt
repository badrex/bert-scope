tldr: A boy from Cremona, Italy, born with a unique anomaly where the lower parts of another boy's body are connected to his abdomen. The additional body parts have a well-formed anus and penis but lack testicles. The boy is otherwise healthy and has had smallpox. This case study provides a detailed description of the physical examination and the boy's condition.
