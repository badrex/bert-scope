tldr: The article is an obituary for a famous metallurgist who made significant contributions to the iron and steel industry. He was known for his analytical skills and innovative methods, such as using percussion figures in metals to study their crystalline structure. His research on blast-furnace slag, eutectics, and the removal of phosphorus from iron through pressure made a lasting impact on the industry. He was a member of the Iron and Steel Institute and received the Bessemer Medal for his work.
