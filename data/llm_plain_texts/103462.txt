tldr: The author, John Hadley, observed the Northern Lights and described the various shapes, colors, and patterns of the lights in the sky. He noticed the lights' irregular shapes, the direction of the rays, and the central figure's movement. Hadley also provided the coordinates of the observed phenomenon, which occurred on the 8th of October and the 6th of March 1715/1716.
