tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and work of Patrick Playfair Laidlaw, a prominent figure in science and medicine. Laidlaw made significant contributions to the fields of physiology, pharmacology, and hemoglobin research. His work on the derivatives of haemoglobin, the pharmacological action of Apocynum cannahinum, and the study of amines from amino-acids have left a lasting impact on the scientific community. Laidlaw's life and career are remembered with admiration and respect.
