tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Harry Julius Emeléus, a renowned inorganic chemist who made significant contributions to the field of chemistry in Britain. Emeléus is known for his seminal book "Modern Aspects of Inorganic Chemistry" and his work in building an internationally acclaimed school of inorganic chemistry at Cambridge. His research students and collaborators went on to achieve distinguished careers in academia, and he is remembered for his dedication to teaching and mentoring.
