tldr: In March 1749, a violent earthquake shook the town of Bridport and neighboring villages. The author, Nath. Downe, wrote a letter to Mr. Greenville describing the event. The earthquake happened after a bright morning was suddenly obscured by dark clouds, followed by a thunderstorm. The main impact was a great surprise to the people who experienced it, with no reported damage.
