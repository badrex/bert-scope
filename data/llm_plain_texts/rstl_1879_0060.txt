tldr: The author collected and analyzed coral specimens from Rodriguez Island during the Transit of Venus Expeditions in 1874-75. They identified 49 coral species and found that the coral fauna of Rodriguez is closely related to that of Mauritius, Madagascar, and the Seychelles Islands. The author also discussed the differences in coral species and their geographical distribution in the Indo-Pacific region.
