tldr: The article discusses a computer system designed to aid in mechanical engineering design and manufacture. The system allows for the direct entry of design information from the designer's layout into the computer, using a combination of equipment and a graphical coding system. This system simplifies the design process, improves efficiency, and enables the storage of detailed part descriptions on magnetic media for subsequent reproduction of detail drawings.
