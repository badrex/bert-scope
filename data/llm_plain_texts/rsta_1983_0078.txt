It highlights the importance of high-technology-low-volume innovations, such as alkali-resistant glass-fiber reinforced composites and mdf cements, and emphasizes the need for more research and development in basic materials, processing, and concrete performance behavior. The article also stresses the significance of improving R&D planning, management, and education to enhance the performance of cement products.
