tldr: Pierre Young was a brilliant engineer who made significant contributions to the development of gas-turbine engines. He worked on the Anglo-French Concorde supersonic airliner and helped solve the icing problems that plagued the Proteus engine in the Bristol Britannia turboprop airliner. Young's unique blend of practical and scientific expertise made him a valuable asset in the highly competitive field of aviation engineering.
