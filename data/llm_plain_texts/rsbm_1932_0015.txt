tldr: 'William Diller Matthew was a renowned palaeontologist who specialized in the study of fossil vertebrates. He made significant contributions to the understanding of the modes of deposition of rocks containing fossil materials and the correlation of the ages of these materials. Matthew's work on mammalian evolution and classification was extensive, covering various orders of mammals. He was a dedicated teacher and mentor, influencing many young palaeontologists throughout his career.'
