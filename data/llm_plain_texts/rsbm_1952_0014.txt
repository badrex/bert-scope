tldr: Alexander McKenzie was a prominent Scottish chemist who made significant contributions to the fields of stereochemistry and optical activity. He studied at the University of St Andrews and later worked in Landolt's laboratory in Berlin, where he collaborated with W. Marckwald. McKenzie's research focused on the separation of racemic mixtures and the study of optical activity. He also had a passion for education and inspired many students throughout his career.
