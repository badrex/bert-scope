tldr: The Kew Observatory Committee reports on their observations and experiments for the year ending December 31, 1896. They discuss magnetic observations, including the magnetographs' operation and the effects of earthquakes on magnetic elements. They also cover meteorological observations, such as atmospheric pressure, temperature, wind, and sunshine records. Additionally, they mention various experiments and improvements to instruments, including the electrograph, anemograph stations, and platinum thermometers.
