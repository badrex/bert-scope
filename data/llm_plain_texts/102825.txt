tldr: The author, Anthony van Leeuwenhoek, shares his observations and experiments with microscopes and the limits of human vision. He discusses the limitations of seeing through metals and the influence of starlight, as well as the motion of blood vessels in the eye. He also expresses skepticism about the claims of others who say they can see starlight through magnifying glasses.
