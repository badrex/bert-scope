tldr: Captain James Cook shares his methods for maintaining the health of his crew during a three-year voyage around the world. He emphasizes the importance of proper nutrition, cleanliness, and hygiene, including the use of antiscorbutic foods like malt, sour krout, and portable soup, as well as fresh water and regular air circulation. The crew experienced a remarkably low rate of illness, with only four deaths during the entire journey.
