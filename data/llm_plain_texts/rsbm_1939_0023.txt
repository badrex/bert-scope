tldr: Sir Robert Mond was a prominent chemist and industrialist who made significant contributions to the field of chemistry, particularly in the development of the carbonyl process for separating nickel. He was also a generous supporter of scientific research and institutions, donating funds to various projects and organizations throughout his life. In addition to his work in chemistry, Mond was involved in Egyptology and archaeology, supporting the establishment of the British School of Archaeology in Jerusalem. His legacy as a scientist, administrator, and benefactor will be remembered for generations to come.
