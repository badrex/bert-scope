tldr: This article discusses the importance of extractive sites, such as quarries and mines, in geological education and research. It highlights the symbiotic relationship between geology and the extractive industries, and how mineral workings provide valuable scientific information and exposures for geologists. The author also addresses the challenges and conflicts that arise between the conservation of extractive sites and their use for mineral extraction.
