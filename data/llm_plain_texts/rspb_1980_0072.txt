tldr: This article discusses the challenges of using local food resources to address malnutrition and food insecurity in rural communities, specifically in The Gambia. It highlights the low energy and nutrient density of traditional foods, the consequences of inadequate diets during pregnancy and lactation, and the importance of improving agricultural production and post-harvest storage. The article also explores the use of food supplements, such as biscuits, to improve the nutritional intake of mothers and infants.
