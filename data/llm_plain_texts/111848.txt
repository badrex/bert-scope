tldr: Dr. Martin Barry was a renowned physician and embryologist who made significant contributions to the field of embryology. He conducted extensive research on the development of mammalian ova and embryos, including the discovery of spermatozoa within the ovum. His work helped advance the understanding of cell biology and tissue formation, and he was awarded the Royal Medal by the Royal Society of London for his achievements. Dr. Barry also contributed to the improvement of the Royal Maternity Hospital in Edinburgh and was known for his kindness and dedication to helping pregnant women in need.
