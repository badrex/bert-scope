tldr: This article presents a detailed record of barometer, thermometer, and rain measurements taken in Lyndon, Rutland, and nearby locations in 1785. The author, Thomas Barker, documents the weather patterns, including frost, drought, and rainfall, and their effects on crops and the environment. The data collected helps to understand the changing weather patterns and their impact on agriculture and daily life during that time.
