tldr: Edwin Tulley Newton was a prominent paleontologist and geologist who worked for the Geological Survey for forty years. He made significant contributions to our understanding of British fossils, particularly those of mammals, birds, and fish. Newton's innovative methods of preparation and interpretation of fossils led to many discoveries and advancements in the field of vertebrate paleontology.
