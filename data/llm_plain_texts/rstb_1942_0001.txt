tldr: This study examines the fossil plants found in the Senni Beds of the Lower Old Red Sandstone in Monmouthshire and Breconshire. The flora, which includes various plant species such as Drepanophycus, Psilophyton, and Zosterophyllum, is of late Lower Devonian age and provides valuable insights into the evolution of early land plants. The study also discusses the geological context and stratigraphy of the Senni Beds.
