tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and work of William Thomson, Baron Kelvin, a prominent physicist and mathematician. Known for his significant contributions to the field of thermodynamics and the development of the modern general theory of energy, Kelvin's work laid the foundation for understanding the principles of Carnot and Joule. His insatiable thirst for knowledge and unwavering dedication to scientific investigation left a lasting impact on the scientific community.
