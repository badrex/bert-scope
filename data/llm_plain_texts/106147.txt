tldr: This article presents experiments conducted on different types of marle found in Staffordshire. The author investigates the composition and properties of these marles, including their calcareous earth content, and discusses their potential uses in agriculture, particularly in the production of quicklime. The study aims to provide guidance on the most suitable marles for different types of land and soil conditions.
