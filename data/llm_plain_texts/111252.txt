tldr: In 1853, W.B. Edwards observed a comet while on board H.M. brig Penguin off the coast of South Africa. The comet was first seen on May 1st and appeared to be moving in a retrograde motion. The author provided a description of the comet's appearance, position, and movement, as well as the weather conditions during the observation. The comet was also observed by other cruisers later on May 6th.
