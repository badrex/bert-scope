tldr: The author compared the accuracy of barometrical and trigonometrical measurements to determine the height of a hill in Spitzbergen. The barometrical measurement resulted in a height of 1640'07 feet, while the trigonometrical measurement gave a height of 1644 feet. The author also discussed the inaccuracy of heights mentioned in Captain Phipps's voyage.
