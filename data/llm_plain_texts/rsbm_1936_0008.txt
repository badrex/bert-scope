tldr: Richard Dixon Oldham was a geologist and seismologist who made significant contributions to the study of earthquakes. He is best known for his work on the Assam earthquake of 1897, where he conducted a detailed analysis of the earthquake phenomena, including the description of the Chedrang fault, the discovery of fractures without displacement, and the concept of "the unfelt earthquake." His research laid the foundation for modern geophysics and earthquake engineering.
