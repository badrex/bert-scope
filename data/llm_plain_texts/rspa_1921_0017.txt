tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and work of John William Strutt, Lord Rayleigh, a prominent physicist who made significant contributions to the fields of optics and electromagnetism. Rayleigh's work laid the foundation for modern physics, and his ability to identify the essential aspects of each problem he tackled made him a master of simplification and clarity in his research. His life and career are closely intertwined with the development of physics during his time, and his legacy continues to influence the field today.
