tldr: This paper analyzes the composition and properties of lac, a substance produced by insects and used in various industries. The author identifies four main ingredients in lac: resin, extractive colorant, gluten, and a specific type of wax. The study also explores lac's uses, such as in manufacturing ornaments, sealing wax, varnishes, and dyes. Additionally, the author discusses potential applications in ink, pigments, and medicine.
