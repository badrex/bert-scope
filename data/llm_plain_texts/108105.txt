tldr: The author, Thomas Andrew Knight, conducted experiments on various dog breeds to study the hereditary instinctive propensities of animals. He observed that young dogs inherited the hunting and training skills of their parents, suggesting that these instincts are passed down through generations. The author also noted the influence of breeding and training on the development of these instincts.
