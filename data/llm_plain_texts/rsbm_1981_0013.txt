tldr: This article is a biography of Charles Frederick Goodeve, a Canadian scientist and naval officer. It covers his early life in Canada, his education at the University of Manitoba, and his research in electrochemistry. Goodeve's work on electrolysis and its applications to domestic boilers made significant contributions to the field. He also had a passion for sailing and naval service, which led him to join the Royal Canadian Naval Volunteer Reserve and later serve in the Royal Navy in England. Goodeve's life and career demonstrate his dedication to science and his love for the sea.
