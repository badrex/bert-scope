tldr: 'William Arthur Parks was a renowned geologist and palaeontologist who contributed significantly to the understanding of Dinosaurs, Invertebrate Palaeontology, and the mineral potential of northern Ontario. He was a dedicated teacher and director of the Royal Ontario Museum of Palaeontology, where he collected and displayed numerous fossils. Parks was also a respected member of several scientific societies and received numerous honors throughout his career.'
