tldr: 'The article is a biographical memoir of Basil Kassanis, a renowned plant virologist who made significant contributions to the field of plant pathology. Born in Skoplje, Yugoslavia, Kassanis pursued higher education in Greece and eventually became a British Council Scholar at Rothamsted Experimental Station in England. His research focused on plant viruses and virus diseases, and he is known for his work on tobacco mosaic virus and potato virus Y. Kassanis's achievements in plant virology placed him among the foremost plant virologists in the world.'
