tldr: This article provides a comprehensive bibliography of 169 papers and books on the igneous and metamorphic rocks of the deep ocean floor, published between 1876 and 1969. The bibliography aims to serve as a basic reference for ocean-floor rocks, with a focus on basaltic igneous rocks. The article highlights the growth of knowledge in this field and the wide scope of studies conducted on ocean-floor rocks.
