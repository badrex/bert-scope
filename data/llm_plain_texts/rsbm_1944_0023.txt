tldr: 'Henry George Lyons was a talented geologist and Egyptologist who made significant contributions to the fields of geology, archaeology, and surveying. He organized the Geological Survey of Egypt and played a crucial role in the preservation of the Philae temples. Lyons also led the Cadastral and Topographical Survey, which was essential for the Egyptian economy. His work and dedication to science earned him recognition and respect from the scientific community.'
