tldr: Dr. Robert Fielding shares his personal experience of being shot in the head by a musket bullet during a battle in the Civil Wars. The bullet remained in his head for nearly 30 years, causing various health issues and complications. Eventually, the bullet was discovered and removed in 1672. This case study provides insight into the medical practices and treatment options of the time.
