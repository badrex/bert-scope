tldr: The author conducted meteorological observations during five voyages to India, studying the normal circulation and weight of the atmosphere in the North and South Atlantic Oceans. They discovered that the barometer readings were lower near Africa, and the lowest barometer occurred in the doldrums. The author also discussed the influence of heated lands on atmospheric pressure and the existence of upper air currents.
