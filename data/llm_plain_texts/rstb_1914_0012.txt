tldr: This article discusses the importance of free-living protozoa in soil and their impact on soil fertility. The authors describe a method to demonstrate the presence of active protozoa in the soil and discuss the life cycles of these organisms in different soil types. They also highlight the potential role of protozoa in causing soil sickness and the need for further research on their role in agricultural ecosystems.
