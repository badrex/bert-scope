tldr: The author studied the anatomy and feeding habits of marine testaceous mollusks, observing three main modes of feeding: browsing, rasping, and gorging. They also examined the unique structures and feeding mechanisms of specific mollusk species, such as the Chiton, Patella mammillaris, Buccinum Lapillus, and Buccinum undatum. The author plans to continue studying other tribes of Mollusca in the future.
