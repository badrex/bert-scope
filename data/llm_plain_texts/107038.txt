tldr: The author investigates two types of lime used in agriculture and their effects on soil fertility. They discover that one type of lime, containing calcareous earth, is beneficial to the soil, while the other, containing magnesia, is detrimental. The author explains the chemical properties of each type of lime and their impact on plant growth, concluding that magnesia-laden lime can prevent vegetation for years if left in one spot.
