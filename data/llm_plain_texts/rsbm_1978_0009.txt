tldr: 'The article is a biographical memoir of Paul Karrer, a Swiss chemist who made significant contributions to the fields of organic chemistry, natural products, and biochemistry. Karrer is best known for his work on carotenoids, vitamins, and coenzymes. He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1937 for his research on carotenoids and flavins, as well as Vitamin A. Karrer's career spanned over five decades, during which he published over a thousand research papers and authored a widely-used textbook on organic chemistry.'
