tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of William Cudmore McCullagh Lewis, a pioneer in physical chemistry and colloid science. Lewis made significant contributions to the understanding of chemical change, catalysis, and chemical kinetics. He authored a widely influential textbook, "A System of Physical Chemistry," and conducted research on cancer treatment and malignancy. His work in chemical kinetics and the study of biological and physiological problems left a lasting impact on the field of chemistry.
