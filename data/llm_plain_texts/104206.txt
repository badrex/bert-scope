tldr: The author, William Hunter, examines the structure and diseases of articulating cartilages in the human body. He describes the cartilage as an elastic substance with a smooth surface and explains its role in providing flexibility and cushioning between bones in joints. Hunter also discusses the blood vessels, nerves, and membrane surrounding cartilage, as well as the challenges in studying and treating diseases affecting cartilage.
