tldr: This article is a biography of George Neville Watson, a prominent mathematician known for his work in complex analysis, approximation theory, and applied mathematics. Watson made significant contributions to the understanding of wireless communication and worked on asymptotic expansions, finite difference equations, and hypergeometric functions. He collaborated with E.T. Whittaker on the influential book "Whittaker and Watson: A Course of Modern Analysis."
