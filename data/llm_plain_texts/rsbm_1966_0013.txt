tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of Arthur Holmes, a renowned geologist who made significant contributions to the fields of geology, petrology, and geomorphology. Holmes is best known for his work on radioactive age determination, which allowed scientists to better understand the Earth's history and age. His investigations in Portuguese East Africa led him to study petrology and geomorphology, while his work on radioactivity helped shape the field of geochronology. Holmes authored two standard textbooks and numerous original papers, and his influence on the scientific community was immense.
