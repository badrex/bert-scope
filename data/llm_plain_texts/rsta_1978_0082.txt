tldr: This article discusses the impact of human activities on atmospheric chemistry, focusing on the increase in greenhouse gases and the resulting consequences for the environment. The authors analyze chemical reactions and their rates, as well as the effects of solar radiation and other factors on atmospheric processes. They also examine the discrepancies between new and old rate expressions for certain reactions and the potential implications for atmospheric chemistry.
