tldr: The author presents observations and drawings of the Great Nebula in Orion made at Birr Castle using 3-feet and 6-feet telescopes between 1848 and 1867. The observations reveal changes in the nebula over time and provide insights into the extent and details of the nebulosity. The author also discusses the methods used for measuring the positions of stars and the limitations of their observations.
