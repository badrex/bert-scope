tldr: This study describes the Middle Bajocian ammonites found in Western Australia, focusing on their systematics, evolution, and stratigraphy. The researchers discovered at least 23 species, including 11 new ones, belonging to seven genera. They also compared the Australian ammonite fauna with those from other circum-Pacific countries and central Asia, highlighting the significance of these findings for evolutionary and systematic theories.
