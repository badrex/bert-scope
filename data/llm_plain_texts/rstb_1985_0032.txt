tldr: This article explores the peripheral and central aspects of trigeminal nociceptive systems, focusing on pain perception in teeth and the cornea. It discusses the role of the trigeminal brainstem nuclei in pain and the mechanisms of pain from tissues innervated by the trigeminal nerve. The article highlights the sensitivity of dental pulp and dentine, and the different types of receptors and nerve fibers involved in pain sensation.
