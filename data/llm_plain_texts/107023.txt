tldr: This article documents the weather patterns and tree recovery observations in Lyndon, Rutland, from 1797 to 1798. The author, Thomas Barker, notes the impact of weather on fruit cultivation and tree growth, with particular focus on the recovery of injured trees. He also shares his successful method of training a Lucombe oak tree to grow upright.
