tldr: 'This article is a biographical memoir of John Conrad Jaeger, a prominent Australian mathematician and physicist. Born in Sydney in 1907, Jaeger studied at Sydney University and Cambridge University, where he made significant contributions to the fields of mathematics and physics. His research interests included the theory of metals, photoelectric effect, and electromagnetic waves in ionized media. Jaeger was a Fellow of the Royal Society and a member of the Australian Academy of Science. He passed away in Canberra in 1979.'
