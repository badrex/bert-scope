tldr: This article discusses the findings of the Workshop on Alternative Energy Strategies (W.A.E.S.), which studied energy prospects for the World Outside Communist Areas (W.O.C.A.) from 1985 to 2000. The study involved 15 countries and aimed to understand the range of feasible alternative energy strategies for the nations. The article highlights the importance of understanding and managing the transition from oil to other energy sources, such as coal and nuclear fuel, to ensure a smooth global energy transition.
