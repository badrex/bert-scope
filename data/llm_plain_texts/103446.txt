tldr: In March 1726, an extraordinary high tide was observed in the River Thames by Captain Thomas Jones. The tide reached 20 feet, 5 inches and a half, which was four inches higher than the highest tide in the previous 40 years. This event highlights the importance of understanding and monitoring tidal patterns and their potential impact on coastal areas.
