tldr: This article discusses James Watt's role in the development of steam engine technology and his contributions to patent law. Watt's improvements to the steam engine, such as the separate condenser and the use of steam pressure on the piston, were crucial to the industrialization of Britain. The article also highlights Watt's proposals for reforming the patent system, which aimed to ensure the sufficiency of patent specifications and prevent the overthrow of patents through defects in the specification.
