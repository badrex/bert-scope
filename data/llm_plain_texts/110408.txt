tldr: The author investigates the functions of different parts of the brain and the relationship between the brain and nerves of motion and sensation. They discover that the cerebrum is responsible for both sensibility and motion, with two columns in each hemisphere dedicated to voluntary motion and sensation. This understanding helps explain the effects of brain injuries on the body's abilities.
