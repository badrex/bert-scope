tldr: Frederick Robert Miller was a prominent physiologist who made significant contributions to the field of physiology and medical education. He studied under notable professors at the University of Toronto and Cornell University before establishing a successful career at the University of Western Ontario. Miller's research focused on visceral sensibility, the cardio-inhibitory center, and the masticatory center, among other topics. He also played a crucial role in the development of medical education and facilities during his time.
