tldr: The author observed and documented the daily fluctuations of the barometer in tropical regions, specifically between the tropics. They discovered that the mercury in the barometer experiences regular elevations and depressions twice in every 24 hours in settled weather, with the lowest station occurring around 4 AM and 4 PM. The author also noted that these regular fluctuations were not present in unsettled weather or during the monsoon season.
