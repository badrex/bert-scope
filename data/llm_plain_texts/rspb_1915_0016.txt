tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and achievements of Joseph Jackson Lister, a pioneering scientist and ornithologist. Lister made significant contributions to microscopy and optics, including the discovery of the true principle for constructing compound lenses. He was also an enthusiastic ornithologist, recognized for his exceptional ability to identify birds by their songs. Lister was a member of the notable Lister family, which included his brother, Lord Lister, the founder of modern surgery.
