tldr: 'The article is a biographical memoir of Richard Tecwyn Williams, a significant figure in the history of foreign compound metabolism. Williams' work, which began in the early 20th century, focused on understanding the metabolic disposition of drugs and chemicals for use in humans. His research laid the foundation for the fields of toxicology and clinical pharmacology. Born in Wales, Williams' early life and education were influenced by his family's background and his own interests in Welsh culture and language.'
