tldr: This study examines the abdominal muscular, nervous, and respiratory systems of the praying mantid, Hierodula membranacea. The research identifies 15 pairs of abdominal muscles, four abdominal ganglia, and ten pairs of spiracles. The findings provide an anatomical basis for understanding the mantid's behavior and ventilation processes.
