tldr: In a letter from Florence, Oliver St. John, Esq., describes the Arcutio, a child care device used in Florence to prevent smothering during winter. The device is designed to be placed under the bedclothes and is required for all nurses to use. The author suggests that this device could be beneficial in England as well.
