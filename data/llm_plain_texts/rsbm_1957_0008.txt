tldr: Albert Jan Kluyver was a renowned Dutch microbiologist and biochemist who made significant contributions to the fields of microbiology and biochemistry. He was a pioneer in investigating the biochemistry of microorganisms and played a crucial role in expanding the knowledge of microbial metabolism. Kluyver's work led to advancements in both biochemistry and microbiology, and he was recognized with numerous honors, including the Copley Medal of the Royal Society.
