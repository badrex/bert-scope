tldr: This study investigates the action of hydrochloric acid on codeia, an opium base, and its potential to form a new base called apocodeia. The author finds that the reaction is in accordance with the formula Codeia, and that apocodeia has similar properties to apomorphia, a base derived from morphia. The research also suggests that there may be an intermediate reaction involving apocodeia or morphia.
