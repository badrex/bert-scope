tldr: This paper discusses observations made in 1863-64 at Frederiksdal, near Cape Farewell in Greenland, to study the semidiurnal tides. The observations were conducted at the request of Admiral Irminger of the Royal Danish Navy by Missionary Asboe. The results obtained include the mass of earth compared to the mass of the moon, the depth of the Atlantic deduced from heights, and the depth of the Atlantic deduced from times.
