tldr: This article describes the discovery of fossil plants, including Trigonocarpon, within limestone nodules found in coal seams. The authors discuss the structure and composition of these nodules, as well as the possible factors contributing to their formation. The presence of these fossils provides valuable insights into the vegetation and environmental conditions during the coal formation process.
