tldr: This obituary highlights the life and work of mathematician David Hilbert, who made significant contributions to number theory, geometry, and integral equations. He is known for his work on the theory of algebraic number fields, the development of axiomatic geometry, and the creation of Hilbert space, which became essential in quantum physics. Hilbert's research left an indelible mark on various branches of mathematical science and had a profound impact on the development of modern mathematics and physics.
