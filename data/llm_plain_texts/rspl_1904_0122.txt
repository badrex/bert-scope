tldr: The author analyzes the results from Falmouth magnetograms and discusses the influence of sunspots on magnetic elements. The study shows a close agreement between Kew and Falmouth data, with sunspot influence being greater at Falmouth. The author also compares the dates of occurrence of maximum in the annual and semi-annual terms for different magnetic elements and finds a small but decided difference between sunspot maximum and minimum periods.
