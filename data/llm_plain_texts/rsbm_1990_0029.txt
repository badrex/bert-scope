tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of Sydney Goldstein, a prominent figure in applied mathematics and aerodynamics. Goldstein made significant contributions to fluid dynamics and aeronautical engineering, and his work as the editor of "Modern developments in fluid dynamics" had a profound influence on the field. He was also a dedicated Zionist and educator, mentoring many students throughout his career.
