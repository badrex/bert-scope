tldr: This article is a biography of George Barker Jeffery, a mathematician and educator who made significant contributions to applied mathematics, higher education administration, and West African education. Jeffery held various positions in academia and was instrumental in shaping the London Area Scheme for teacher training and the establishment of the West African Examination Council. He also had a passion for craftsmanship and was an expert cabinet maker.
