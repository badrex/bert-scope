tldr: Honor Bridget Fell was a renowned British biologist who made significant contributions to the field of tissue and organ culture. She founded the Strangeways Research Laboratory, where she conducted extensive research on cellular interactions of cartilage and bone using organ culture techniques. Her work helped advance the understanding of differentiated tissues and their response to various agents, such as vitamins, hormones, and drugs.
