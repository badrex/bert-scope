tldr: 'John Edward Harris was a talented biologist who made significant contributions to the fields of zoology, cell biology, and marine biology. He was also an educator who pioneered innovative approaches to teaching and learning. Harris conducted groundbreaking research on the viscosity of the cytoplasm of eggs, the structure of the nucleus of sea-urchin oocytes, and the mechanics of fish propulsion. His work had a lasting impact on the scientific community and inspired future generations of biologists and educators.'
