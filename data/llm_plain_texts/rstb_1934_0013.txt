tldr: The author describes the extinct forms of rhinoceroses found in Lower Miocene deposits of Dera Bugti in Baluchistan. The collection provides insights into the variety and evolution of contemporary rhinoceroses, despite the fragmentary nature of the specimens. The author discusses the challenges in determining the generic position of the rhinoceroses due to the lack of complete material and the differences in classification among various authors. The study contributes to the understanding of the rhinoceros group and their evolution.
