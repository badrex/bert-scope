tldr: Edward Mellanby was a prominent figure in medical research, serving as the Secretary of the Medical Research Council for 16 years and making significant contributions to the field of medicine. His work in pharmacology and physiology led to advancements in medical research, and he continued his research even during his time as Secretary, finding a balance between administrative duties and hands-on research. Mellanby's dedication to his work and his family was a driving force in his success.
