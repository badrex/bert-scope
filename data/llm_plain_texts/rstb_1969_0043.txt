tldr: The author examines the complex of Ficus deltoidea and Ficus oleaefolia, distinguishing thirteen varieties of F. deltoidea and eight of F. oleaefolia. They discuss the evolution, habitat, and distribution of the complex, concluding that it represents a recent development and offers insights into the manner of speciation and geographical distribution in the genus Ficus. The complex is found mainly in the Sunda Shelf region, with extensions to nearby islands.
