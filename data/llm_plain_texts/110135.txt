tldr: A lady born blind received sight at an advanced age due to the formation of an artificial pupil. The author performed three operations to remove a portion of the closed iris, which successfully imparted vision. The lady could distinguish colors, forms, and relative situations in angular positions, and was profoundly affected by the grand features of nature.
