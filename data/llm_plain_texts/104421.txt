tldr: This article discusses a case of poisoning caused by the consumption of boiled hemlock. The author, William Watson, investigates the different plant species involved and their effects. He identifies the Cicuta major as the poisonous plant responsible for the incident and provides a brief overview of other potentially toxic plants in England. Watson emphasizes the importance of detecting poisonous plants and shares his findings with the Royal Society.
