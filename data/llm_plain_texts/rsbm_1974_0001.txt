tldr: "Neil Kensington Adam was a distinguished classical physical chemist and one of the founders of quantitative surface chemistry. He served as the Head of the Department of Chemistry at the University College of Southampton for 20 years and played a significant role in establishing the School of Chemistry there. Adam's work focused on the intersection of chemistry, physics, and mathematics, and he was known for his dedication to teaching and mentoring students."
