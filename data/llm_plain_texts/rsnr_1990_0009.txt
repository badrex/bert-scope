Banks played a significant role in the Royal Society and contributed to the discovery and documentation of numerous plants and animals during his voyages. Despite not having published much as a scientist, Banks left behind a vast herbarium and personal papers that have been crucial for historians and biographers to study his impact on the scientific community.'
