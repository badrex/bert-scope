tldr: In this lecture, Karl T. Compton discusses the organization of American scientists during World War II and their collaboration with British scientists. He highlights the importance of applying scientific knowledge to serve in the struggle for survival and to preserve peace and freedom. Compton also emphasizes the need for scientists to report on the impact of science on society and to use their skills to address the waste and resource depletion caused by war.
