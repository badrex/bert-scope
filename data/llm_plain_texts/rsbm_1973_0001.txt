tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Norman Percy Allen, a prominent metallurgist who made significant contributions to the understanding of porosity in copper and copper alloys. Allen's work on die-casting alloys and his application of physical methods to solve metallurgical problems are highlighted. His career, from his early days in Sheffield to his time at Swansea and Birmingham, is also discussed.
