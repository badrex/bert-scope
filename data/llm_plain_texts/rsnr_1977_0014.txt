tldr: This article explores the complex and often capricious cultural relationship between Britain and America from the early days of Jamestown and Plymouth to the 20th century. It examines the influence of British culture on American society, including sports, architecture, literature, and entertainment, as well as the impact of American culture on Britain. The author emphasizes the unique historical situation of the close ties between the two countries, which are maintained by their shared language and common cultural roots.
