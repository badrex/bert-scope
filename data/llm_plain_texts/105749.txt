tldr: In 1767, Mount Vesuvius erupted, causing significant damage and forcing people to evacuate. The author, William Hamilton, provides a detailed account of the eruption, including the lava flow, the noise and smell of sulfur, and the impact on nearby towns. He also discusses the possible causes of the eruption and the role of rainwater in the volcanic activity.
