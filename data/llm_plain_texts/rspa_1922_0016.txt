tldr: This obituary honors the life and work of William Odling, a prominent chemist who contributed to the development of the Theory of Types, the recognition of Avogadro's Rule, and the Periodic Classification of Elements. Odling was a key figure in the chemical community, serving as a secretary, vice-president, and president of the Chemical Society, and as a Waynflete Professor of Chemistry at Oxford for forty years.
