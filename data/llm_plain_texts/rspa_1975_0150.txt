tldr: The Satellite Instructional Television Experiment (SITE) aimed to use satellite technology to provide educational television programs to rural areas in India. The experiment aimed to test the effectiveness of satellite-based instructional television systems, demonstrate the potential of satellite technology in mass communication, and improve national development in India. The experiment covered various subjects, including family planning, agriculture, national integration, education, and health and hygiene.
