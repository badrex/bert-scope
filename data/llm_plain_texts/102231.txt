tldr: This article describes a case study of a man who was bitten by a mad dog and eventually died from the disease called hydrophobia. The author, Martin Lister, provides a detailed account of the man's symptoms, including his inability to drink water, and the various treatments attempted, such as opening a vein, applying plasters, and giving him various antidotes. Despite these efforts, the man ultimately succumbed to the disease.
