tldr: Mr. Cradock observed a fiery meteor and communicated his findings to the Royal Society through Mr. Henry Baker. The meteor's head and body emitted a bright white flame, while its tail appeared blue, similar to the flame of sulfur. This event took place on May 27 and was witnessed from the Somerset-Gardens terrace.
