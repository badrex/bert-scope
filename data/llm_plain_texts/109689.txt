tldr: This article discusses a large calculus (bladder stone) removed from a patient named Sir James Ogilvie after his death. The stone weighed 44 ounces and was considered the largest on record. The stone's texture was less compact than usual and resembled the Fusible Calculus. It was composed of ammoniacal phosphate of magnesia and phosphate of lime.
