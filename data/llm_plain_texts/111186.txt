tldr: The author describes the process of calibrating and graduating twelve thermometers supplied from the Kew Observatory for the Arctic Searching Expedition. The thermometers were divided into mercurial and spirit types, and the author explains the different processes used for each type. The author also discusses the importance of accurate temperature measurement in Arctic exploration and the potential errors that can occur during the calibration process.
