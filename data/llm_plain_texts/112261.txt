tldr: This paper explores the relationship between aqueous vapor and radiant heat, demonstrating its impact on meteorology and climate. The author conducts experiments with dry and humid air, discussing the formation of cumuli, tropical rains, and the effects of aqueous vapor on climate. The study also addresses the role of mountain masses in condensing atmospheric moisture and the radiation observed at great elevations.
