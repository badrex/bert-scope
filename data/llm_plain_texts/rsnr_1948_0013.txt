tldr: The Royal Society congratulates King George VI on the upcoming marriage of his daughter, Princess Elizabeth, to Lieutenant Philip Mountbatten. The Society highlights its long-standing relationship with the British monarchy and the fact that Princess Elizabeth was recently elected as a Fellow of the Royal Society. The message is presented to the King by a delegation from the Society, including its President, Sir Robert Robinson.
