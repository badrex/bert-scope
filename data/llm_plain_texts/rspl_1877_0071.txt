tldr: The author discusses the methods used to examine air and collect data on atmospheric pollutants. They describe the process of washing air with water and passing it through various solutions to collect soluble and insoluble substances. The author also highlights the challenges faced in this process, such as adherence to tubes and the need for multiple washing bottles. They emphasize the importance of establishing a chemical department in observatories for further research in this field.
