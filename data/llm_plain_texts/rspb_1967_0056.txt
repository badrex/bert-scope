tldr: The author discusses the Planning-Programming-Budgeting System (PPBS) and its two main techniques, programming and systems analysis, which were introduced to improve high-level planning in large organizations. PPBS aims to link fiscal and substantive planning, making it more realistic and providing a criterion for making hard choices. The author explains the rationale behind PPBS and its potential applications in various sectors, such as government departments, education, and industry.
