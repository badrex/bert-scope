tldr: 'Thomas Slater Price was a prominent chemist and educator who made significant contributions to the fields of physical chemistry, education, and research. He held various positions in academia and industry, including head of the chemistry department at Birmingham Central Technical College and director of research at the British Photographic Research Association. Price also served in World War I, where he worked on developing smoke screens and chemical warfare. His dedication to chemistry and education earned him several accolades, including Fellowships in the Royal Society and the Royal Society of Edinburgh.'
