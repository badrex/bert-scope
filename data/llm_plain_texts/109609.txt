tldr: This article describes the process of unrolling and examining two mummies of Egyptian Ibis birds. The author, John Pearson, discusses the embalming techniques used by the ancient Egyptians and compares the preservation methods of the two mummies. He also notes that the stomach and intestines were not removed during the embalming process, contrary to the belief that this was the practice for human bodies.
