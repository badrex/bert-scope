tldr: The Surface Evolver is a computer program that helps study the shapes and stability of liquid surfaces. It uses the Hessian matrix to analyze equilibrium points and eigenvalues to determine the stability of these points. This tool can be applied to various problems, such as capillary surfaces, liquid metals, and cell membranes.
