tldr: This article discusses the research on spectrum photography and its potential for quantitative chemical analysis. The author presents methods for detecting and analyzing metallic elements in solutions using their characteristic spectra. The study covers various elements, including magnesium, zinc, cadmium, aluminum, indium, thallium, copper, silver, mercury, tin, lead, tellurium, arsenic, antimony, and bismuth. The author demonstrates the high sensitivity of the spectrum reaction and its applications in detecting trace amounts of elements in water and other solutions.
