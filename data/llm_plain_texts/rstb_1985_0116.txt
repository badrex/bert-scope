tldr: This article discusses the advancements and challenges in animal husbandry in Europe from 1973 to 1995. It highlights the intensification of pig and poultry industries, the expansion of dairy output through increased milk yield per cow, and the shift away from intensive beef cattle systems. The article also explores the future technological advances and socio-economic factors that will influence animal production, such as milk quotas and public concerns over animal welfare, pollution, and health aspects of animal products.
