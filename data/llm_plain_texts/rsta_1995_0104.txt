tldr: This article discusses the development of nanostructured sensors for molecular recognition, focusing on new materials and transducers. It explores the control of surface structures of chemically sensitive materials down to the molecular scale and the molecular understanding of sensor mechanisms. The article also highlights selected case studies, including electron conductors, ion conductors, mixed conductors, molecular cages, polymers, and biomolecular function units.
