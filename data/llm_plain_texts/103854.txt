tldr: A man was suspected of being poisoned after eating a herb, leading to severe symptoms. The author, Vincent Bacon, treated the patient by inducing vomiting and providing medical treatment. The cause of poisoning was identified as the monkshood plant, also known as Aconitum. The patient recovered, while another person who ate the same plant but in smaller amounts remained unwell.
