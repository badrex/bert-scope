tldr: In 1694, Edward Floyd reported an unusual phenomenon in Pembroke-shire where grass-eating worms appeared in large numbers on a hill. These worms were consumed by birds and swine, eventually disappearing after three weeks. The event was mysterious and had never been seen before or since.
