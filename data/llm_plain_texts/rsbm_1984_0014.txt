tldr: 'Andrew McCance was a prominent figure in the steel industry, known for his contributions to metallurgy and industrial leadership. He made significant advancements in understanding the properties of steel, including the prevention of cracking in armor plate ingots, the heat-treatment of armor plate, and the physical chemistry of steelmaking. McCance's work laid the foundation for modern steel production and his leadership in the industry was instrumental in shaping the steel industry in Scotland.'
