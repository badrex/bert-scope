tldr: 'Gamaliel Smethurst invented the Shwan-Pan, an arithmetical instrument inspired by Chinese methods. It consists of wires with beads that can be moved to perform calculations. The Shwan-Pan offers advantages over traditional European methods, such as being tactile, faster, and less prone to mistakes. It can be used to teach arithmetic and improve accounting accuracy. The instrument resembles the ancient Abacus.'
