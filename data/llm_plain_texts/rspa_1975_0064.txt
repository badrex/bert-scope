tldr: The Prospero satellite conducted various experiments and technological advancements in space technology. These included testing lightweight solar cell assemblies, evaluating cover slip behavior, proving the space-worthiness of hybrid electronic assemblies, and examining thermal control surface finishes. The satellite also carried a micrometeoroid experiment to detect and study these space debris. The experiments and technological developments from Prospero have been used in subsequent spacecraft projects.
