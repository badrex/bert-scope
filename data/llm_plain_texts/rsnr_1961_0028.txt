tldr: In 1961, the Canadian Fellows of the Royal Society presented a table for the Mace as a gift to the Society during the Tercentenary Celebration. The table was made in England according to the design of Professor Russell, using Canadian walnut and capped with leather from Canadian Caribou. The Canadian Fellows expressed their commitment to contribute to the Society's mission of promoting the sciences and useful arts for the benefit of humanity.
