tldr: This article examines the magnetic storm of August 22, 1916, and its effects on the Earth's magnetic field. The author discusses the complexity of magnetic changes during storms and the challenges in studying them. They analyze the magnetic disturbances at various stations and propose different types of disturbances, such as oscillations and rotations in the disturbance vector. The author also investigates the relationship between the magnetic changes and the occurrence of auroras.
