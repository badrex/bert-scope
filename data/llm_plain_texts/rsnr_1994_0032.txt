tldr: This article is a review of the book "The Private Lives of Albert Einstein" by Roger Highfield and Paul Carter. The reviewer, Sir Hermann Bondi, discusses the book's exploration of Einstein's personal life and its impact on his scientific work. The review also highlights the book's strengths and weaknesses, including its balanced and scholarly approach to the debate over Mileva's contribution to special relativity and its underplaying of Einstein's work on radiation coefficients and quantum statistics.
