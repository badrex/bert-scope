tldr: The author investigates the action of certain metals and organic bodies on photographic plates. They discover that volatile substances like linseed oil and turpentine can affect the plates, and that the action is due to the vapour given off by these substances. The author also explores the conditions under which these organic bodies act on the photographic plates and the factors affecting their activity.
