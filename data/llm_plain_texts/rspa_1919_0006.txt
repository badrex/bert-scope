tldr: In his 1918 address, Sir J. J. Thomson discusses the importance of science in post-war reconstruction and the need for greater application of scientific methods to industry. He also highlights the importance of scientific research in maintaining national defense and suggests the establishment of research departments for naval and military services. Additionally, he addresses the challenges faced by universities and the need for increased support to maintain their efficiency and research capabilities.
