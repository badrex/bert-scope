The author highlights the differences in pharmacokinetic behavior between leucocyte and fibroblast interferons and the importance of determining the minimum effective regimens for clinical trials. The article also explores the various routes of administration, including intramuscular injections, eye drops, and nasal sprays, and their respective effects and limitations.
