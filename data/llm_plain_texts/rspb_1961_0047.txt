tldr: The authors describe the process of isolating and purifying 6-aminopenicillanic acid from penicillin fermentation broth. They explain various methods, including concentrating the broth, adsorption and elution from alumina and charcoal, and ion exchange resin, to obtain crystalline 6-aminopenicillanic acid. This discovery is essential for understanding the properties and potential uses of this important antibiotic.
