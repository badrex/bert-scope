tldr: John Goldingham observed and recorded the eclipses of Jupiter's satellites using two Dollond telescopes in Madras, East Indies. He provided a detailed account of the instruments used, the circumstances observed, and the methods employed to minimize errors in the observations. Goldingham recommended comparing both the immersion and emersion times of the eclipses to obtain accurate results.
