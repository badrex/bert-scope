tldr: This article discusses the strength and weaknesses of glass, exploring the effects of composition, surface damage, and environmental factors on its mechanical properties. The author examines various glass types, such as borosilicate and soda-lime, and investigates the role of submicroscopic flaws, heat treatment, and moisture in determining glass strength. The study also highlights the importance of understanding these factors for improving glass manufacturing and applications.
