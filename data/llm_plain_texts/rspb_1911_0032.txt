tldr: This obituary highlights the life and achievements of Harry Marshall Ward, a prominent botanist and plant pathologist. Ward made significant contributions to the study of plant diseases, fungi, and their life histories. He is best known for his work on the coffee leaf disease in Ceylon and his research on tropical epiphyllous lichens. Ward's passion for research led him to make numerous discoveries in the fields of botany and mycology, earning him a reputation as a leading investigator in his time.
