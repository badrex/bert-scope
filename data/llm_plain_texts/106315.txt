tldr: This article describes the case of Miss Lingfield, who suffered from a severe muscular contraction that caused her head to be drawn down over her shoulder and her face to be turned obliquely. Miles Partington, a medical practitioner, attempted to treat her condition using electricity. He found that by applying electrical sparks to the affected muscles, Miss Lingfield experienced significant improvement in her condition, leading to a more comfortable life. This case study highlights the early use of electricity as a potential treatment for muscular disorders.
