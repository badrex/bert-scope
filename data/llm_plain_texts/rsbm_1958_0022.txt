tldr: Charles Todd was a prominent figure in public health and medical research. He made significant contributions to the understanding of diseases like malaria, diphtheria, and scorpion venom, and played a crucial role in organizing and improving serum institutes in Egypt. Todd's work on cellular individuality and iso-agglutinins in fowls is considered his principal scientific achievement.
