Adair's love for natural history, geology, and chemistry began in his early years, and he later pursued his passion for mountaineering, which influenced his scientific approach. His work on osmotic pressure for protein molecular weight determination faced challenges, but his mountaineering experiences provided him with the confidence and perspective to overcome these obstacles.
