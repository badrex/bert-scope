tldr: This study analyzes the direct correlation between luminous and electrical field-changes produced by thirty-seven lightning discharges to ground. The research uses photographic and electrical techniques to understand the nature of lightning and its effects on the atmosphere. The findings reveal the complexity of lightning discharges and their impact on the surrounding environment.
