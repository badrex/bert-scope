tldr: 'The author, Joseph Carne, describes the Relistian Tin Mine in Cornwall, where rounded pebbles were found at a depth of 75 fathoms. The mine has a lode with various minerals, including schist, chlorite, and quartz. The lode has a flucan, or cross lode, which cuts the main lode at an angle. The author also mentions the presence of copper and copper pyrites in the mine.'
