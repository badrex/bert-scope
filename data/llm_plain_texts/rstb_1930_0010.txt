tldr: This study investigates the development of the vertebral column in Urodela, focusing on the embryonic stages and the formation of the skeletal system. The author examines the relationship between arches, intervertebral and vertebral bodies, and traces the development from cartilaginous to ossified stages. The research also discusses the development of the first vertebra and its relation to the occipital region, as well as the feeding habits and rearing of Urodelan specimens.
