tldr: This article is a biography of Douglas Rayner Hartree, a prominent mathematician and physicist known for his work in numerical analysis and theoretical physics. Born in Cambridge in 1897, Hartree made significant contributions to the fields of atomic physics and computational methods. He held various academic positions and worked on numerous projects, including the development of the self-consistent field method and the application of numerical analysis to complex problems in physics. Hartree's legacy continues through his influence on the scientific careers of his family members and his dedication to helping others with their work.
