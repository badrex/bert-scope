tldr: James Short observed and recorded the details of a lunar eclipse that occurred on November 22, 1760, in Surrey-Street in the Strand, London. He documented the beginning, middle, and end times of the eclipse, as well as the degree of moon obscuration and its position in the sky. This observation contributed to the understanding of lunar and solar eclipses and their timings.
