tldr: This article is a biography of Edward Battersby Bailey, a prominent geologist and former Director of the Geological Survey of Great Britain. Bailey dedicated his life to geological thinking and exposition, with a focus on interpreting the phenomena observed through surface mapping of igneous and metamorphic rocks in the mountains, hills, and islands of western Scotland. His work contributed to the understanding of geologic mapping and the study of Earth's geological processes.
