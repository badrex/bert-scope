tldr: John Scott Haldane was a renowned physiologist and philosopher who made significant contributions to the understanding of life and its mechanisms. He emphasized the importance of studying life as a living whole and developed methods to analyze the respiratory exchange of animals. Haldane's work on carbon dioxide and carbon monoxide poisoning helped improve safety in coal mines and provided valuable insights into the nature of life and its relationship with the physical sciences.
