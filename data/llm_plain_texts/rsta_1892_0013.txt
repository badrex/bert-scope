tldr: The author investigates turacin, an animal pigment containing copper, and its relation to turacoverdin. They discuss the presence of copper in various animals and its role in the formation of pigments. Turacin is found in the feathers of certain birds, while turacoverdin is a green pigment from green feathers of Turacus and other plantain-eaters. The author also examines the chemical properties and distribution of these pigments in birds.
