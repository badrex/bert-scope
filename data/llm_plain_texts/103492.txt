tldr: A patient experienced a severe cough that led to the expulsion of a polypus from their windpipe. The polypus was examined and found to be hollow but damaged, preventing it from being blown up. The patient had been suffering for over six months and eventually succumbed to a fatal consumption, highlighting the importance of early diagnosis and treatment of such conditions.
