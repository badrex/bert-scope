tldr: 'This article presents a meteorological journal for the year 1774, kept by Samuel Farr in Bristol. It includes daily temperature and barometer readings, as well as a table of winds and weather changes for the year. The author emphasizes the importance of outdoor temperature observations and criticizes indoor thermometer readings for their lack of accuracy in representing external conditions.'
