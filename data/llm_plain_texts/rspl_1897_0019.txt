tldr: This article reports the magnetical observations conducted at Falmouth Observatory in 1896. The observations include measurements of magnetic declination, horizontal force, and vertical force. The results are part of the International scheme for magnetic observations and will be published in the Royal Cornwall Polytechnic Society's Annual Report and the 'Proceedings' of the Royal Society. The observations were made using instruments purchased from the Government Grant Fund administered by the Royal Society.
