tldr: This article investigates the impact of spurious correlation on personal equation in observations. The author conducts experiments to determine the relative steadiness of judgment and finds substantial correlation in independent judgments. The study highlights the importance of considering not only the variability of observers but also their correlation in understanding the personal equation.
