tldr: Thomas George Cowling was a prominent British astronomer and mathematician who made significant contributions to the fields of stellar structure, cosmical electrodynamics, kinetic theory, and plasma physics. Despite having a background in mathematics, Cowling's work in astronomy led to important discoveries and advancements in the understanding of the universe. He was a dedicated academic and served in various positions in professional organizations, receiving numerous honors throughout his career.
