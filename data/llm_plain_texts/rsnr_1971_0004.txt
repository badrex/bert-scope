tldr: This article discusses James Watt's corrections and additions to John Robison's account of steam and steam engines. Watt revised Robison's articles in the Encyclopaedia Britannica to provide a more accurate and complete history of his work on steam engines. The article highlights Watt's revisions, including his invention of the separate condenser, experiments on latent heat, and improvements to the fuel and water consumption of steam engines.
