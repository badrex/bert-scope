tldr: The author, John Beaumont, shares his observations and descriptions of plant-like minerals and rock formations found in Mendip-hills in Somersetshire. He discusses various types of Trochitae and Entrochi, their shapes, sizes, and structures, and how they are connected. Beaumont also describes the Radix, a mineral substance resembling a wheel, and its unique features.
