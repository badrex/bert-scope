tldr: 'In November 1958, a special joint conversazione was held by the Royal Society, Linnean Society, and Geological Society to honor the memory of Charles Darwin and Alfred Russel Wallace on the centenary of their groundbreaking paper on species variation and natural selection. The event included historical exhibits, displays of present-day research in fields related to Darwin and Wallace, and a film showcasing the insect problems Darwin encountered in Brazil.'
