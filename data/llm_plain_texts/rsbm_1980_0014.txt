tldr: Edward James Salisbury was a renowned botanist and ecologist who made significant contributions to the fields of plant geography, palaeobotany, and ecology. He was a foundation member of the British Ecological Society and an influential university teacher. His research focused on coastal vegetation, woodland vegetation, and the biology of plants in their natural surroundings. He authored several popular textbooks on botany in collaboration with F. E. Fritsch.
