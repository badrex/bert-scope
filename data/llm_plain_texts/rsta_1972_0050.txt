tldr: This article discusses the challenges and solutions for designing a removable flood barrier for the River Thames to prevent future disasters like the 1953 flooding. The authors explore various barrier designs, including floating, rising shutter, and retractable types, and analyze their feasibility, cost, and reliability. They also consider the impact on shipping and navigation, and recommend a specific design for the Woolwich Reach.
