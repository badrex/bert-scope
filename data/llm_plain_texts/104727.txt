tldr: In 1749, a mysterious shock was experienced by many people in London and its surroundings. The author, James Parsons, documented the events and shared the accounts of others who felt the shock, which was accompanied by a red light and a trembling motion. Some people reported sickness and headaches after the event. The shock was felt in various locations, but not everywhere, and it also affected animals, causing them to jump out of the water. The cause of the shock remains unknown.
