tldr: Frederick William Carter was a pioneering engineer and mathematician who contributed significantly to the development of the electrical engineering industry. He worked for the General Electric Company in America and the British Thomson-Houston Company in England, where he advised on and worked on various electrical traction projects. Despite facing challenges in teaching, Carter's expertise in engineering and mathematics made him a valuable consultant and engineer throughout his career.
