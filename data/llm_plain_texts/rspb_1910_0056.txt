Pflüger made significant contributions to the fields of muscle and nerve physiology, metabolism, and protein chemistry. Dallinger was a devoted scientist and educator, serving as a president for several scientific societies and giving numerous lectures on natural science. Both individuals left a lasting impact on their respective fields.
