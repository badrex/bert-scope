tldr: In June 1751, a powerful lightning strike damaged the church and steeple of Southmolton in Devon. The lightning split stones, shattered windows, and caused burns and damage to iron and wire components. The event also killed two horned cattle and three sheep nearby. The author, Joseph Palmer, provides a detailed account of the damage and the circumstances surrounding the incident.
