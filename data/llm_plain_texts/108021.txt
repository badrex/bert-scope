tldr: In 1836, the volcano Coseguina erupted in Central America, causing a massive explosion and widespread destruction. The eruption produced a large plume of smoke and ash that spread over a wide area, causing darkness and a suffocating smell. The eruption also triggered earthquakes and heavy rains, leading to significant damage and loss of life. This event was one of the most extensive volcanic eruptions in modern times.
