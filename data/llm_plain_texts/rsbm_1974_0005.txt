tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Francis Albert Eley Crew, a prominent geneticist and educator. It highlights his early life, education, and career, focusing on his contributions to the fields of genetics, animal breeding, and cytology. Crew was a key figure in establishing the Institute of Animal Breeding in Edinburgh and made significant contributions to the understanding of intersexuality and sex transformation in animals. His work and influence in the field of genetics had a lasting impact on the development of experimental biology in Britain.
