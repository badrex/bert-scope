tldr: The Royal Society clarifies its role in the publication of the Philosophical Transactions, explaining that they do not endorse or approve the content of the papers, but rather select and publish papers of importance and significance. They emphasize that their role is to improve knowledge and benefit mankind, and they discourage crediting unverified reports of their approval or applause.
