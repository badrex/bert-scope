tldr: 'This article discusses the life and work of Sir William Petty, a prominent figure in the founding of the Royal Society and a pioneer in economic statistics. Petty's work focused on the quantitative study and practical management of resources, both on a civic and international scale. His innovative ideas and methods in administration, such as a state medical service, land registry, and decimal system, were ahead of his time. Petty's contributions to the field of economic statistics and administration are still relevant today.'
