It explores the classification, distribution, and physiological roles of various peptides, as well as the methods for modulating peptide action, such as agonists, antagonists, and inhibitors of breakdown or synthesis. The article highlights the challenges and opportunities in developing peptide-based drugs, focusing on the need for more specific receptor antagonists and the potential for treating conditions like pain, behavioral disorders, and cardiovascular diseases.
