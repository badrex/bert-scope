tldr: This study examines the formation and early growth of the human facial bones, focusing on the maxilla, intermaxillary bones, and other bones of the face. The research describes the stages of development, ossification, and fusion of these bones in a fetus, as well as the changes that occur during growth. The findings provide insights into the complex process of facial bone formation and contribute to our understanding of human anatomy.
