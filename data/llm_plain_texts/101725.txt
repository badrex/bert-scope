tldr: This article discusses the mechanical origin of various qualities of matter and the corpuscular hypothesis. The author presents experiments and considerations about heat, cold, tastes, odors, volatility, fixity, corrosiveness, and other qualities, aiming to demonstrate that they can be explained by mechanical principles. The author also touches upon magnetism and electricity, suggesting that these qualities may have mechanical origins as well. The article provides a historical account of the development of the corpuscular hypothesis and its potential implications for understanding the nature of qualities in matter.
