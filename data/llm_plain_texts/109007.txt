tldr: This article describes the author's exploration and analysis of the remains of over thirty horse-like species found in the Cavern of Bruniquel. The author examines the dental structures of these fossils, providing detailed descriptions of the grinding surfaces of the upper and lower molars. The study contributes to the understanding of equine evolution and the history of the cavern's organic contents.
