tldr: Frederick Currey discovered amorphous starch in a new tuberaceous fungus, which he named Amnylocarpus encephaloides. This finding challenges the belief that fungi do not contain starch and provides insight into the chemical composition and classification of this new genus within the Tuberacei family.
