tldr: Dr. Jonathan Goddard was a prominent physician in the 17th century, known for his contributions to medicine and his involvement in the early days of the Royal Society. He was a skilled practitioner, a defender of the College of Physicians, and a supporter of experimental science. Goddard was also a pioneer in the field of telescope design, being one of the first to make a telescope with his own hands.
