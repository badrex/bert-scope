tldr: In 1769, a team of astronomers led by Benjamin Franklin observed the transit of Mercury in Pennsylvania. They used multiple telescopes and recorded the contacts and micrometer measures. Their observations helped determine the longitude of the state-house observatory. This collaboration showcased the power of collective scientific efforts.
