tldr: 'Sidney George Brown was a brilliant inventor who made significant contributions to various fields of applied science. He invented the world-famous drum cable relay, which improved submarine telegraphy, and developed a directional beam wireless system. Brown also designed and constructed gyro compasses for sea, land, and air navigation. His other notable inventions include the first telephone relay, the A-type phone, and the loudspeaker.'
