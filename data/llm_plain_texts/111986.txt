tldr: This article presents a comprehensive catalogue of 5,078 nebulae and clusters of stars, compiled for the year 1860.0. The catalogue includes objects discovered by various astronomers, such as Sir William Herschel, Messier, Mairan, Oriani, and others. The author, J.F.W. Herschel, also discusses the process of reducing the observations to a common epoch and the precessions for 1880, ensuring the catalogue's accuracy and usability for future observations.
