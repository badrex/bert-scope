tldr: The article discusses the development and use of the 24-inch Schmidt satellite cameras for geodetic and geophysical investigations. These cameras, designed and built in Britain, are compared to the Baker-Nunn cameras used by the Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory. The article also covers the methods of measuring precise satellite positions, the accuracy of the cameras, and their role in international satellite tracking programs.
