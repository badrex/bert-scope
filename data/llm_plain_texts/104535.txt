tldr: This article discusses the case of a man who was born with two tongues. The man, Henry Wharton, was a chaplain to Archbishop Sancroft. The story was communicated to the Royal Society by Cromwell Mortimer. Wharton's double tongue was of the same shape and size, and midwives advised removing the lower tongue. The man's life was marked by constant illness.
