tldr: James William McBain was a prominent chemist and colloid scientist known for his work on soap chemistry, ionic micelles, and association ions. He published two influential textbooks on colloid science and contributed to the understanding of the properties of soaps, detergents, and dyes. McBain's research interests and achievements were recognized with numerous awards and honors throughout his career.
