tldr: The article discusses the life and work of Joseph Kenyon, a prominent chemist known for his contributions to the resolution of alcohols and the study of optical activity. Kenyon developed the Pickard-Kenyon method for resolving alcohols, which led to significant advancements in the field. He also worked on the synthesis and resolution of sulfur and selenium compounds, expanding the understanding of their properties and potential applications.
