tldr: Mr. Short observed a solar eclipse on December 19, 1739, using a reflecting telescope with a 16-inch focus that magnified about 40 times. Due to cloud cover, the beginning and end of the eclipse were not visible, but he was able to see the sun being eclipsed by about 2 or 3 digits.
