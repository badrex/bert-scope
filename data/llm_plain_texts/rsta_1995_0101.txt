tldr: The article discusses various lithography methods being developed for the fabrication of future generations of silicon integrated circuits, comparing their capabilities and limitations. It explores electron-beam lithography, UV lithography, soft-ray projection, and X-ray lithography, highlighting the challenges in achieving high resolution, low cost, and fast exposure rates for these technologies. The article concludes that overcoming these difficulties is crucial for the continued miniaturization and advancement of microelectronics.
