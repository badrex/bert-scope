tldr: This article traces the history of the search for electromagnetic induction from 1820 to 1831, focusing on the experiments and discoveries of Oersted, Ampere, Fresnel, and others. It details the initial excitement and interest in the connection between electricity and magnetism, the development of new devices and techniques, and the eventual understanding of electromagnetic induction. The article highlights the challenges faced by scientists during this period and the importance of their work in laying the foundation for modern electrodynamics.
