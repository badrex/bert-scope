tldr: George Forbes was a versatile scientist and engineer who made significant contributions to the fields of physics, astronomy, and electrical engineering. He conducted research on the velocity of light, participated in early astronomical expeditions, and played a role in the development of electric lighting and power transmission. Forbes also invented a range-finder, a gun-sight, and made improvements in the arc-lamp and electric motors. Despite his numerous achievements, Forbes lived a modest life and was known for his sense of honor and piety.
