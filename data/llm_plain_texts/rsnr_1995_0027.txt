tldr: This article explores the connections and influences of the Romantic poet Samuel Taylor Coleridge with leading British and German scientists of the Enlightenment era. Coleridge, despite not being a scientist himself, cultivated friendships with notable figures like Georg Christoph Lichtenberg and Johann Friedrich Blumenbach, who were involved in various scientific fields. These relationships helped shape Coleridge's understanding of science and influenced his literary works.
