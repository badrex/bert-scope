tldr: The author, Edmund Ronalds, conducted experiments to determine if larger amounts of carbon were excreted by the kidneys in cases where the lungs or liver functions were impaired. He discovered that the kidneys excrete from three to five grains of sulfur and nearly six grains of phosphorus daily, not combined with oxygen.
