tldr: Linus Pauling was a renowned chemist and scientist who made significant contributions to various fields, including chemical bonding, protein structure, and molecular biology. He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1954 and the Nobel Peace Prize in 1962. Pauling's work in promoting the benefits of vitamin C and advocating for nuclear disarmament made him a well-known figure in both the scientific and general public.
