tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and work of Paul Langevin, a French physicist who made significant contributions to the fields of physics, particularly in nuclear physics and acoustics. He is best known for his work on the Langevin ultrasonic transducer, which is still used today in medical imaging and non-destructive testing. Langevin also made important contributions to the understanding of the behavior of atoms and molecules, and he was a pioneer in the study of nuclear reactions.
