tldr: This article discusses the importance of magnetic surveys conducted at sea and on land during the voyage of H.M.S. Penguin from 1890-93. It highlights the observations made at various locations, including the West Coast of Australia, and the discovery of a magnetic shoal under the sea at Port Walcott. The article also explains the methods used to study the magnetic disturbances and the findings about the cause of these disturbances.
