tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Francis John Richards, a prominent plant physiologist. Richards made significant contributions to the understanding of salt marsh ecology, particularly through his research on the process of vertical accretion. He was also among the first to introduce statistical methods in interpreting ecological data, which helped him reach clear-cut conclusions about complex processes. Richards' work and life are remembered for his versatility, learning, and kindness.
