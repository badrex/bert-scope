tldr: This article discusses the observations and findings of Commander J.L. Wharton on the currents and undercurrents of the Dardanelles and Bosphorus. The author explains the general flow of Black Sea water through the Bosphorus, Marmara, and Dardanelles to the Mediterranean, which is influenced by N.E. winds, water temperature, and differences in specific gravities between the two seas. The article also describes the counter-currents, their directions, and the impact of wind on the movement of water.
