tldr: The Kew Committee's report for the year ending October 31, 1876, details the various observations and experiments conducted at the Royal Society's Kew Observatory. The report covers magnetic work, electrometer, photoheliograph, extra observations, verifications, London office, chronometer testing, pendulum experiments, and instruction given. The committee also discusses the instruments' maintenance, improvements, and the data supplied to various institutions and researchers.
