tldr: 'Grace Chisholm Young was a trailblazing mathematician who overcame societal barriers to pursue her passion for learning. She earned a doctorate in Germany in 1895, the first doctorate awarded to a woman in any subject. Grace collaborated with her husband, W.H. Young, on numerous mathematical projects and made significant contributions to his work. Their partnership and her academic achievements helped establish Cambridge as a world center for pure mathematics.'
