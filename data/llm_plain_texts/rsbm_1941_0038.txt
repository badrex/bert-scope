tldr: John Smyth Macdonald was a prominent physiologist who specialized in the study of nerve and muscle physiology. He conducted research on the effects of temperature on nerve and muscle excitability and made significant contributions to the understanding of action currents in mammalian nerves. Macdonald also played a vital role in the administration of medical schools and universities throughout his career.
