tldr: Sir William Johnson provides insights into the customs, manners, and language of the Northern Indians of America. He discusses the decline of traditional practices and the impact of European influence on Native American societies. Johnson also describes the process of choosing tribal leaders, the use of wampum belts as a form of record-keeping, and the unique features of the Native American language.
