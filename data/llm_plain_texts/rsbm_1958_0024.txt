From his early life as a brilliant student to his contributions in various departments like the Colonial Office, National Health Insurance Commission, and Ministry of Shipping, Anderson was known for his intellect, character, and determination to serve the public. His ability to handle complex tasks and work with diverse groups made him an invaluable asset in various challenging roles throughout his life.
