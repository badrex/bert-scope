tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of William Joscelyn Arkell, a notable geologist and palaeontologist. Arkell made significant contributions to the understanding of Jurassic stratigraphy, ammonites, and tectonic phenomena. He authored a major work, "The Jurassic System in Great Britain," which remains an indispensable reference in the field. Throughout his career, Arkell conducted extensive research, published numerous papers, and held various positions at New College, Oxford.
