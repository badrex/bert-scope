tldr: The author describes the fossil tusk of an extinct proboscidian mammal found in Queensland, Australia. The tusk is indicative of an elephantine animal, somewhat smaller than existing ones in Asia and Africa. The author provides detailed evidence of the ivory nature of the tusk and includes minute characters of the dentine variety. Microscopic sections and figures accompany the paper.
