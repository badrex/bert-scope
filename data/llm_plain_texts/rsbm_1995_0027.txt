tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of George Taylor, a prominent British figure in flowering plant taxonomy from 1930 to 1970. Taylor was trained at Edinburgh University and worked at the British Museum (Natural History) before becoming the Director of the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew. His influence on flowering plant taxonomy and gardening in Britain and abroad was significant, and he authored a standard work on the classification of the genus Meconopsis.
