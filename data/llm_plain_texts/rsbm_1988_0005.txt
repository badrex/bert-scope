tldr: This biographical memoir recounts the life of Malcolm Dixon, a renowned biochemist known for his work on enzyme purification and kinetics. Dixon's contributions to the field, including the development of systematic enzyme nomenclature, helped establish enzyme studies on a firm quantitative basis. His personal life, marked by a strong family background and deep Christian beliefs, is also explored.
