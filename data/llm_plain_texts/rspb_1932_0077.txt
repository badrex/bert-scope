tldr: The study investigates the inheritance of the ability to transmit a plant virus by the leafhopper insect, Cicadulina mbila. The author found that the ability to transmit the virus is an hereditary character and that some individuals of this species were unable to transmit the virus. The research provides insights into understanding the transmission of plant viruses and the potential for controlling the spread of diseases.
