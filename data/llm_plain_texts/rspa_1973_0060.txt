tldr: The authors conducted experiments to study the thermal expansion of alkali halides at low temperatures. They determined the linear expansion coefficients and the Gruneisen parameter for sodium, rubidium, and cesium halides. The results showed that the Gruneisen parameter approaches a limiting value at low temperatures, and some alkali halides exhibit negative expansion coefficients below certain temperatures. This research provides insights into the anharmonic nature of crystal lattices and the relationship between thermal expansion, adiabatic compressibility, and heat capacity.
