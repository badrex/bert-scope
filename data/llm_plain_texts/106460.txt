tldr: This article provides a detailed record of weather and crop conditions in Lyndon, Rutland, from 1780 to 1781. It describes the temperature, rainfall, and barometric pressure throughout the year, as well as the impact of these conditions on the growth of crops and the overall health of the land. The author, Thomas Barker, also discusses the effects of weather patterns on the local economy, mentioning the prices of various crops and the general well-being of the community.
