tldr: The Royal Society expedition to Montserrat investigated the island's volcanic history and petrology, as well as earthquakes and soufriere activity that caused damage and alarm in 1934 and 1935. The study involved fieldwork, geological mapping, and comparison with nearby Martinique's Mt Pele. The research aimed to understand the geological history of Montserrat and its relationship with other Caribbean islands, as well as the origin of the island's volcanic activity.
