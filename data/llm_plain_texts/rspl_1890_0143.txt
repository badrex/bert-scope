tldr: The author discusses the successful isolation and cultivation of the malarial fungus, demonstrating its pathogenic qualities. They also report the development of a more advanced state of the fungus in a specific nutrient medium. Inoculation of various animals with the fungus resulted in a fatal disease, sometimes displaying intermittent symptoms. The presence of the characteristic organisms in the animals' blood after death supports the conclusion that the malarial fungus is pathogenic.
