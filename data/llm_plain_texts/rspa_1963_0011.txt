tldr: The authors studied the temperature variation of the effective Gruneisen parameter in caesium chloride structures. They found that the Gruneisen parameter increases generally as the temperature increases. For most modes, the Gruneisen parameter is almost independent of frequency. However, for some transverse acoustic modes, the Gruneisen parameter decreases abruptly as the temperature increases. This study provides insights into the behavior of perfect Gruneisen solids and the dynamics of caesium chloride lattices.
