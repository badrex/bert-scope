tldr: This article is an obituary for Gilbert Newton Lewis, a prominent chemist known for his work in physical chemistry, thermodynamics, and electrochemistry. He made significant contributions to the understanding of chemical substances and their free energy, as well as being a supporter of the theory of relativity. Lewis was a dedicated educator and leader, playing a crucial role in shaping the Department of Chemistry at the University of California.
