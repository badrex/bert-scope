tldr: This article describes a case of unusual limb mortification affecting a family in Wattisham, Suffolk. Despite the severity of the condition, most of the family members survived, and the mortification did not spread beyond the original separation. The cause of the disorder remains unknown, although poor-quality corn used in their bread is considered a possible factor. The article highlights the importance of further investigation into such rare medical conditions and their potential causes.
