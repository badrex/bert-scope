tldr: The author investigates the structure and arrangement of air-sacs and blood vessels in the human lung. They describe the lobulette, a collection of air-sacs connected to a bronchial tube, and observe that the air-sacs do not communicate with each other except through the bronchial tube. The author also discusses the bronchial tubes' termination and the alveoli, which play a crucial role in gas exchange.
