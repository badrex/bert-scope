tldr: This article discusses the ritualization of behavior in animals and humans, focusing on a religious ritual among the Walbiri people of Central Australia. The author describes the process of the ritual, including the use of bull-rovers, blood, and seeds, and how it is connected to the creation myths and worship. The article also highlights the role of these rituals in teaching and passing down secret knowledge to new initiates.
