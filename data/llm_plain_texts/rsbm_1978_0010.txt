tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of John Edensor Littlewood, a prominent mathematician who contributed significantly to the field of mathematical analysis. It highlights his early life, education, and collaboration with G. H. Hardy, which led to the establishment of a renowned school of analysis in England. Littlewood's work focused on complex function theory, number theory, and Diophantine approximation, among other areas.
