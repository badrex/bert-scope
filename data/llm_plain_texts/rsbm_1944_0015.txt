tldr: Frank Lee Pyman was a prominent organic chemist and pharmaceutical researcher who made significant contributions to the fields of medicinal chemistry and natural products. He worked at the Welcome Chemical Works, where he conducted research on the relationship between chemical constitution and physiological action, local anaesthetic action, and the isolation and characterization of various compounds from plant sources. Pyman's work on asymmetric nitrogen compounds and the synthesis of selenium analogues of atoxyl showcased his expertise and innovation in organic chemistry.
