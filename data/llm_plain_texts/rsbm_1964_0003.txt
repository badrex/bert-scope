tldr: Gerhard Domagk, a notable figure in medical science, discovered that a synthetic red dye could effectively treat bacterial infections in mice. This groundbreaking experiment led to the development of Prontosil, a drug that revolutionized the treatment of various bacterial infections in humans. Domagk's work paved the way for further advancements in chemotherapy and earned him the Nobel Prize for Medicine in 1939.
