tldr: This article examines the late Quaternary history and ecological development of the Cumberland Lowland in England. It investigates the geography, geology, and history of human settlement in the region, as well as the significance of the Scottish Readvance Glaciation and the chronology and extent of post-Glacial marine transgression and regression. The study presents data from various sites and discusses the ecological history of the region, including the effects of geographical processes, climatic changes, and human settlement.
