tldr: This study provides a taxonomic account of the marine algae collected during the 1965 Royal Society Expedition to the Solomon Islands. The algal flora comprises 71 species of Chlorophyta, 27 of Phaeophyta, 121 of Rhodophyta, and 14 of Cyanophyta, along with seven species of sea grasses. The authors present collection data, habitat summaries, and taxonomic notes for each species, contributing to the understanding of marine algal biogeography and ecology in the region.
