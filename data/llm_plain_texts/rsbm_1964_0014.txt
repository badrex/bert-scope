tldr: Joseph Lade Pawsey was a pioneering radio astronomer who made significant contributions to radar research and ionospheric physics. He worked on television development at E.M.I. and designed the aerial and feeder systems for the first television tests at Alexandra Palace. Pawsey later joined the Radiophysics Laboratory of C.S.I.R.O. in Sydney, where he conducted notable researches in radio astronomy and became the Assistant Chief of the Radiophysics Division.
