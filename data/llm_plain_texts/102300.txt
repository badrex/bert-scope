tldr: The author describes a unique natural resource found near Smyrna, called Soap Earth, which is used to produce soap. The earth is mixed with lime and dissolved in boiling water to create a thick brownish substance, which is then combined with oil to make soap. The process takes around 13-14 days, and the resulting soap is dried and sold. The author also provides details on the economic impact of this resource, including the number of camels used to transport the Soap Earth and the profit generated by soap houses.
