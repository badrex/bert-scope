tldr: 'The article discusses the properties and uses of smectite clay minerals, which are essential in various industries due to their unique physicochemical properties. These properties include a large chemically active surface area, high cation exchange capacity, and interlamellar surfaces with unusual hydration characteristics. Smectite clay minerals have diverse applications, such as in foundries, oil well drilling, wine production, and civil engineering, as well as in the manufacturing of industrial, chemical, and consumer products.'
