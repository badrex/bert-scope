tldr: This article explores the microstructures of natural materials like woods, palms, and bamboo that contribute to their exceptional mechanical efficiency. The authors analyze the composite and cellular structures of these materials and discuss the implications for designing mechanically efficient engineering materials. They also create model materials with similar microstructures to demonstrate their potential applications in engineering.
