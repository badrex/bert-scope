tldr: Augustus Daniel Imms was a renowned entomologist and educator who made significant contributions to the fields of insect morphology, taxonomy, and ecology. He worked in various roles, including professor of biology, forest entomologist, and chief entomologist at Rothamsted Experiment Station. Imms authored several textbooks and conducted research on various insect species, including Anopheles, Collembola, and termites. His work has influenced many students and researchers, who now hold important positions in entomology worldwide.
