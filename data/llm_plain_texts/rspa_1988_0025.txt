tldr: This article discusses the advancements in aerodynamic research and development in civil aircraft wing design over the past 20 years. The Hatfield aerodynamic team has contributed to the success of European industries through Airbus wing designs, achieving aerodynamic efficiency that results in significant fuel savings. The paper outlines the environment, tools, wing design process, and achievements of the team, as well as future development possibilities.
