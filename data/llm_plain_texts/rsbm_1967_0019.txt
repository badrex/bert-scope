tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of Laurence Rickard Wager, a prominent geologist and explorer. Wager made significant contributions to the fields of geology, petrology, and geochemistry, and was a skilled climber and mountaineer. He participated in various expeditions and research projects, including the British Arctic Air Expedition and the East Greenland Expeditions, which helped shape our understanding of Earth's geological processes.
