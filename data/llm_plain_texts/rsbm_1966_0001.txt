tldr: 'Edward Victor Appleton was a brilliant physicist and geophysicist who made significant contributions to the understanding of radio wave propagation and the discovery of the ionosphere. He worked at the Cavendish Laboratory and later held the Wheatstone Chair of Physics at King's College London. Appleton's research led to the measurement of the height of the Heaviside Layer and the discovery of the Appleton Layer beyond it.'
