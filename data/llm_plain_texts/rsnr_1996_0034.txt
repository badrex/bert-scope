tldr: This book review recounts the life and achievements of Paul Ewald, a pioneer in the field of X-ray crystallography. Ewald's work laid the foundation for numerous developments in crystallographic studies and he was a key figure in establishing the International Union of Crystallography. The review highlights Ewald's contributions to science, his dedication to international collaboration, and his influence on the lives of those who knew him.
