The authors analyze data from various locations and times to deduce the behavior of the electron density and loss coefficient. They discuss the significance of their findings and explore the possibility of different mechanisms for electron loss and production. The results provide insights into the understanding of the upper atmosphere and the behavior of electrons in the ionosphere.
