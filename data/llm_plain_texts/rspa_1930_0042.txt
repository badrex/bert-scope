tldr: This article discusses the development of X-ray spectroscopy as a method for quantitative analysis, focusing on the challenges and advancements in the field. The authors test the method on alloys of elements with varying concentrations and atomic numbers, finding an accuracy of about 1 in 200. They also address factors affecting the intensity of X-ray lines, such as applied potential, target inhomogeneity, and line comparison, and compare their results with previous work in the field.
