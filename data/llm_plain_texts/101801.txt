tldr: The author, Michael Angelo De Guattini, and his companion Dionysius of Placenza, observed various animals and a strange plant during their voyage to the Kingdom of Congo. They encountered unusual creatures like the "Poux de Pharaon," large serpents, and massive ants. They also discovered a unique fruit tree called Niceffo with only two leaves. These observations provide insights into the diverse wildlife and flora in the region.
