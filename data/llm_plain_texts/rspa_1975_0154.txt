tldr: The article discusses the potential of satellite technology in supporting international communication for education and health services in the Pacific Basin. The Peacesat project, initiated in 1971, uses a communication satellite to link institutions and individuals in nine different countries, enabling them to share resources and improve services. The project focuses on low-cost, interactive, and accessible telecommunications to support health, education, and community services.
