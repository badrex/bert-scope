tldr: The author discusses the research conducted at the Radar Research Establishment, Malvern, focusing on semi-conductor research, particularly on germanium and silicon. The article highlights the development of laboratory equipment for handling high purity materials, the study of electronic band structures, and the exploration of new types of junctions and devices based on carrier injection and exclusion. The research also delves into the influence of impurities and structural imperfections on the properties of semi-conductors.
