tldr: This study examines the Upper Domerian and Lower Toarcian deposits in Great Britain, focusing on the Spinatum, Tenuicostatum, and Falciferum zones. The research explores the environmental changes and factors that influenced the distribution of sediments and fauna during this period. The findings provide insights into the tectonic stability, sea level fluctuations, and the impact of these changes on the local ecosystems.
