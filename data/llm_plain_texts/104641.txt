tldr: In December 1749, Mr. Wm. Elstobb observed a lunar eclipse and documented the precise times of various stages of the eclipse. He noted the edge of the umbra appeared as two curves meeting at an obtuse angle and provided details about which lunar features were visible and which were not during the eclipse.
