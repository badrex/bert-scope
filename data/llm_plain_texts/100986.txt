tldr: Dr. Christopher Wren invented a tool for drawing objects in perspective. The instrument uses strings and a ruler to maintain a horizontal position, allowing the user to trace the outlines of an object. This technique was used to create accurate representations of three-dimensional objects on a two-dimensional surface.
