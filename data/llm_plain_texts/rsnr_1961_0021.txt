tldr: The Shell International Petroleum Company arranged a premiere of films at the Royal Festival Hall in 1961 to celebrate the Tercentenary of the Royal Society. The event showcased the company's commitment to the intersection of pure and applied science. The main film, "A Light in Nature," and the supporting film, "The Revealing Eye," demonstrated the historical and scientific potential of industrial companies like Shell.
