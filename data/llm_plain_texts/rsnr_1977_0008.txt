tldr: This article discusses the significant contributions of A. A. Campbell Swinton to the development of television. Swinton proposed the use of cathode ray tubes for both transmitting and receiving television signals, which laid the foundation for modern all-electronic television systems. Despite not patenting his ideas, Swinton's work influenced subsequent television engineers and inventions.
