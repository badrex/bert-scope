tldr: The Meteorological Committee of the Royal Society reports on the work done in the Meteorological Office from 1866 to 1875. They discuss ocean meteorology, improvements in log quality, and the study of surface temperatures and currents in the Atlantic Ocean. The report also highlights the challenges of securing a supply of observers and the importance of systematic supervision for accurate data collection.
