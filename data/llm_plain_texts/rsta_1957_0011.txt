tldr: This article presents the methods and results of palaeomagnetic investigations conducted in various locations across Great Britain. The authors describe their use of a magnetometer and discuss the conventions used for plotting and measuring palaeomagnetic directions. They acknowledge the support and assistance provided by various individuals and institutions throughout the research process. The study contributes to the understanding of the Earth's magnetic field and the geological history of the region.
