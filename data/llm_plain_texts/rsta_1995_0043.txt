tldr: This article reviews and synthesizes research on the exchange of trace gases between land and the atmosphere. It highlights advancements in understanding soil and plant processes associated with trace gas production and destruction, atmospheric chemistry, and new methods for studying gas exchange in the field. The article emphasizes the need for further development of models to simulate methane and nitrous oxide production in various environmental conditions and encourages the application of developed techniques in tropical regions.
