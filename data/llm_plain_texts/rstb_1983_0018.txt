tldr: The study investigates the cardiac innervation and circulatory system of Eledone cirrhosa, a type of octopus. The innervation arises from the visceral nerves and includes two main peripheral ganglia on each side. The study provides detailed information on the nerves supplying the cardiac organs and vessels, and discusses the possible physiological significance of the findings.
