tldr: The author discusses the concept of release of function in the nervous system, explaining how loss of control in higher brain functions can lead to excessive activity in lower centers. This phenomenon is observed in various nervous system diseases, such as hemiplegia and spinal cord injuries. The author also highlights the importance of understanding this concept in the evolution of function and its implications for neurological medicine.
