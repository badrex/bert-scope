tldr: This obituary honors Frederick Du Cane Godman, a prominent zoologist and naturalist who made significant contributions to the fields of ornithology, entomology, and biogeography. Godman's lifelong partnership with Osbert Salvin led to the creation of the comprehensive work 'Biologia Centrali-Americana,' which documented the flora, fauna, and archaeology of Central America. His dedication to scientific research and his passion for natural history left a lasting impact on the field.
