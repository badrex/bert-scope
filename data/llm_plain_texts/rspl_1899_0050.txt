tldr: The author conducted observations in Greece and Sicily to study the orientation of ancient Greek temples. They found that the orientations of these temples were closely related to astronomical events and local geography. The author also used their findings to determine the possible dates of foundation or dedication for some of the temples, such as the Temple of Theseus and the new Erechtheum.
