tldr: 'Samuel Phillips Bedson was a prominent pathologist and virologist who made significant contributions to the understanding of blood platelets, viruses, and immunity in virus infections. His work on psittacosis, a disease caused by infected birds, helped pave the way for future research in infectious diseases. Bedson's career spanned various institutions, including the Lister Institute and the London Hospital, where he made lasting impacts on the fields of pathology and virology.'
