tldr: In 1739, John Martyn observed an Aurora Australis near London. The phenomenon appeared as a red band with greenish-blue streams, emanating from a central point. The event was visible for several hours and had a significant impact on the appearance of the night sky. Martyn's description provides valuable information for understanding this rare celestial event.
