tldr: This article explores the advances in coordination chemistry, specifically focusing on polymer-salt complexes in the solid state. These complexes, also known as polymer electrolytes, have unique properties that make them suitable for various applications, such as rechargeable lithium batteries, smart windows, and electrochromic display devices. The article discusses the thermodynamics of complexation, the nature of salt-polymer interactions, the structures of these compounds, and their potential applications.
