tldr: The author describes the design and construction of a water storage and distribution system in a valley. The system includes a large underground reservoir, an aqueduct, and a series of walls and channels to control water flow. The purpose of this system is to collect and distribute water from the Rivolet Audot, rain, and snow for use in the surrounding area. The author also discusses plans for further expansion and improvements to the system.
