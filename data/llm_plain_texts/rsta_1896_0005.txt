tldr: This article discusses the geology and mineralogy of the ruby mines in Upper Burma, focusing on the mode of occurrence, origin, and metamorphoses of corundum and associated minerals. The author, C. Barrington Brown, and Professor John W. Judd provide a historical overview of the discovery and mining of rubies, sapphires, and other gemstones in Burma, as well as the various minerals found in the region. They also describe the geological conditions and the associated minerals in different localities, such as the eastern United States, India, and other parts of the world.
