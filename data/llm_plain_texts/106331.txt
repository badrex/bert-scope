tldr: This article presents a detailed record of the weather, barometer, and thermometer readings in Lyndon, Rutland, from 1777 to 1778. The author, Thomas Barker, documents the temperature, rainfall, and atmospheric pressure, providing valuable insights into the climate patterns of that time. The article also mentions an earthquake felt in the region and compares the weather conditions of that year to those of 1725 and 1735.
