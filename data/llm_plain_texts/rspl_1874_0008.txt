The report covers magnetic work, including the Magnetograph instruments, magnetic reductions, and the Electrometer. It also covers meteorological work, such as the recording of barometers, thermometers, anemometers, and Bain-gauges, as well as the verification of meteorological and magnetic instruments. Additionally, the report mentions sunspot observations, pendulum experiments, and the testing of Sextants.
