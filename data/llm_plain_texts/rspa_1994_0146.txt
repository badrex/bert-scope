tldr: This article studies the effective properties of a nonlinear composite dielectric material using a simple cubic array of inclusions embedded in a matrix. The authors develop both upper and lower bounds for the overall energy of the composite using the nonlinear Hashin-Shtrikman variational principles. The results indicate the potential improvement over the simple classical bounds that can be obtained using these principles.
