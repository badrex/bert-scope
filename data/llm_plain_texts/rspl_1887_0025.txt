tldr: This article discusses the characteristics and differences between tropical and extra-tropical cyclones, including their formation, behavior, and impact on weather patterns. It highlights the oval shape, rotation, cloud formations, and central pressure differences in these weather systems, as well as their propagation and interaction with surrounding pressure areas. The author also notes the specific features of tropical cyclones, such as their smaller size, steeper central depressions, and reluctance to cross land areas.
