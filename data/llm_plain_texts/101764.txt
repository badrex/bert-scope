tldr: In this letter, Monsieur Leeuwenhoek shares his observations of a vast number of tiny living creatures in water. He explains his methods of observing and estimating the number of these microorganisms using microscopes. He also discusses the revival of these creatures after the water was heated and then cooled down. This work contributed to the understanding of microscopic life and the development of microscopy.
