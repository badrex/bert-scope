tldr: Edward Hindle was a prominent biologist known for his work in entomology, virology, and parasitology. He made significant contributions to the understanding of arthropod transmission of diseases and developed a prophylactic vaccine against yellow fever. Hindle also introduced the golden hamster to the home and laboratory and played a crucial role in the formation of various societies and clubs related to biology and animal welfare.
