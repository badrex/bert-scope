tldr: Eustace Ebenezer Turner was a prominent chemist and educator who made significant contributions to the fields of organic, physical, and inorganic chemistry. He is best known for his work on organometal complexes, asymmetrically constituted arsonium salts, and the discovery of conformational isomerism in the aromatic series. Turner also played a crucial role in shaping the chemistry department at East London College and was a dedicated teacher who inspired many students.
