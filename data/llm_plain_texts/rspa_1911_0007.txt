tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and achievements of Sir Benjamin Baker, a renowned engineer who contributed significantly to civil engineering, particularly in bridge design and urban railways. He was responsible for the design and construction of the Forth Bridge and the Aswan Dam, and his work on the London Underground Railways and the Central London Tube Railway showcased his innovative ideas and solutions. His understanding of the law of fatigue and the strength of materials greatly influenced the engineering industry.
