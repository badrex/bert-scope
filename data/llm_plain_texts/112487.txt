tldr: The Royal Society has been working on promoting science and organizing meteorological observatories. They have established six or seven observatories across the British Islands to study weather patterns and provide accurate meteorological data. The society is also working on improving weather forecasting and maritime meteorology, collaborating with the Board of Trade and providing telegraphic weather warnings to ports.
