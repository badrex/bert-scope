tldr: In April 1750, an earthquake was felt in Flintshire, causing a violent shock and moving beds. The event caused terror in the community but no significant damage. The author, Mr. Pennant, reported the incident to Rich. Holford Esq., Master in Chancery. This event highlights the impact of natural disasters on communities and the importance of understanding seismology.
