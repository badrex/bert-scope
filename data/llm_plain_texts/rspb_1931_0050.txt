tldr: This obituary honors T.B. Wood, a distinguished agricultural scientist who contributed significantly to the fields of animal nutrition, plant breeding, and agricultural research. Wood's work on animal nutrition, including the development of a method for computing rations for farm animals, and his research on the nutritional values of pasture, have had a lasting impact on the agricultural industry. He also played a crucial role in various national food supply committees during World War I.
