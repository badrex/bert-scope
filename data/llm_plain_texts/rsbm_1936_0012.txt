tldr: George Thurland Prior was a distinguished mineralogist and petrographer who worked at the British Museum for over forty years. He made significant contributions to the fields of mineral chemistry, petrography, and meteorites. Prior was an expert in analyzing minerals containing rare elements and helped identify new minerals and rocks. He also developed a new classification system for meteorites based on their chemical composition. Prior served as the President of the Mineralogical Society and authored numerous publications in the field.
