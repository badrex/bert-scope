tldr: 'Theodore Morris Sugden was a renowned chemist and educator who made significant contributions to the field of physical chemistry. Born in Yorkshire, he studied at Jesus College, Cambridge, and went on to have a distinguished career in academia and research. Sugden was known for his exceptional breadth of knowledge, his flair for experimental work, and his ability to inspire and support his students. He held various positions, including Reader in Physical Chemistry and Master of Trinity Hall, Cambridge. Sugden was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society in 1963 and passed away in 1984 after a short illness.'
