tldr: In 1781, John Lloyd, Esq. F. R. S., experienced an earthquake in Hafodunos, near Denbigh. He described the event, including the time, location, and intensity of the shaking. He also mentioned other places where the earthquake was felt and provided details about the barometer reading and the lack of any weather changes. Lloyd offered to share his account with the Royal Society if they found it valuable.
