tldr: 'Henry Alexander Miers was a renowned mineralogist and crystallographer who made significant contributions to the field. He worked at the British Museum and later held the Waynflete Chair of Mineralogy at the University of Oxford. Miers was also involved in the development of crystallographic techniques and the study of interfacial angles in crystals. His work had a lasting impact on the understanding of crystallography and mineralogy.'
