tldr: The author provides a detailed description of the skull and skeleton of the Myotragus balearicus, a peculiarly modified rupicaprine antelope. The article discusses the unique features of the skull, such as its high orbital position, short and wide lachrymal, and the position of the auditory meatus. The author also compares the skull with those of other related forms, including Capra, Ovis, Nemorhcedus, and Budorcas, to determine its affinities and similarities.
