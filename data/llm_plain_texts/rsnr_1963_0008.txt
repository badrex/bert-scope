tldr: This article provides an index and overview of the laboratory records and notebooks of C. T. R. Wilson, a prominent scientist known for his work on condensation phenomena and atmospheric electricity. The annotated index aims to facilitate access to the original sources of Wilson's groundbreaking research, which significantly influenced modern physics and our understanding of new elementary particles. The records cover topics such as cloud formation, ionization, X-rays, and the development of the cloud track chamber.
