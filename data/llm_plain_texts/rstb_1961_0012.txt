tldr: The article describes a large Pleistocene fauna and flora found in terrace deposits of the River Salwarpe at Upton Warren, Worcestershire. The fossils are dated to the beginning of the Gottweig Interstadial, following the Midland Irish Sea Glaciation. The extensive fauna and flora provide valuable information for understanding the ecology and climatology of the time period.
