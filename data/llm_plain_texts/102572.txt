tldr: This article discusses the mineral waters of St. Amand near Tournay and Valenciennes, their properties, and the various experiments conducted on the water. The author, Mr. Geoffroy, describes the water's source, quality, and the effects attributed to it, such as aiding in palsy, shortness of breath, and other ailments related to nervous system and lung issues. The water is also said to have purging and de-obstructive properties, and can be consumed or used in mud baths for treatment.
