tldr: Samuel Sugden was a talented chemist and academic who made significant contributions to the fields of physical chemistry and munitions development during World War I. He later became a prominent figure in academia, holding positions at Birkbeck College, University College London, and the Royal Society. Sugden was also a dedicated researcher, establishing active schools of research and making important advancements in the study of explosives, war gases, and magnetochemistry.
