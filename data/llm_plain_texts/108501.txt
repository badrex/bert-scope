tldr: The author examines the structure and function of the rostellum in the orchid plant Listera ovata. They discovered that the rostellum plays a crucial role in the plant's pollination process by producing and secreting a viscous substance that helps retain and apply pollen to the stigma. The rostellum's unique structure and function provide insights into the reproductive biology of orchids and their diverse adaptations for pollination.
