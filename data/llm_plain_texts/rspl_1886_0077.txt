tldr: The author investigates the distribution and abundance of microorganisms in the air using a method developed by Hesse. They found that microorganisms are widely distributed in the atmosphere but their numbers vary in different places and at different times. The author also studied the influence of season, atmospheric conditions, and altitude on the abundance of microorganisms in various locations, including towns and the countryside.
