tldr: The article is an obituary for Hermann Kopp, a prominent German chemist and historian of chemistry. Kopp is best known for his influential 'History of Chemistry' and his role in founding the 'Jahresbericht fiber die Fortschritte der Chemie und verwandter Theile anderer Wissenschaften,' a renowned scientific journal. Kopp's work focused on establishing connections between the physical and chemical properties of substances, and he made significant contributions to the fields of crystallography and chemical education.
