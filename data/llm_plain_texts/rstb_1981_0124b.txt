tldr: This article reviews recent advances in understanding the role of pheromones in insect communication and emphasizes the importance of multicomponent systems. It discusses the origin, ratios, and behavioral response of insects to individual pheromone components, as well as the significance of chirality in pheromone effectiveness. The article also presents examples of pheromone identification and potential use in control measures for various insects.
