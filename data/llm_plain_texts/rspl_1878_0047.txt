tldr: The author studied the development and anatomy of parasitic isopods, specifically Cymothoa oestroides and C. parallela. They observed the stages of development, from egg to adult, and described the formation of various body parts and organs, such as the nervous system, eyes, and digestive system. The research was conducted at the Zoological Station in Naples.
