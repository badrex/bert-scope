tldr: The study examines the ultrastructure of the heart and pericardium of an aplacophoran mollusk (Neomeniomorpha) and provides evidence for ultrafiltration of blood through the heart wall into the pericardial coelom. This discovery supports coelomic ultrafiltration as a primitive characteristic of mollusks.
