tldr: This article provides a detailed method for curing castorium, also known as beaver stones, using a traditional Russian technique. The process involves boiling the stones with water and wood ashes, smoking them with birch bark, and then drying them in a kitchen or the air for a week or more. The cured castorium can be packed in a cask or other container for transportation.
