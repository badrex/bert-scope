tldr: This article presents an ab initio Hartree-Fock study of the ilmenite-structured MgSiO3, focusing on the chemical nature of the Si-O bond and the electronic properties. The authors used a periodic ab initio Hartree-Fock method to optimize the unit cell volume and calculate density of states and electron charge density maps. The study highlights the importance of using a basis set that can describe the ionicity of oxygen and magnesium and the role of d orbitals on silicon.
