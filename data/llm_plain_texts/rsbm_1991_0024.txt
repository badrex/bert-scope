tldr: 'Karel Franti\#x161;ek Weisner was a distinguished Czech chemist and electrochemist, known for his work on polarographic currents. Born in Prague, he came from a family with a strong background in engineering and entrepreneurship. Weisner's early education and passion for science led him to study at the Prague Institute of Technology, where he made significant contributions to the field of electrochemistry. His career was marked by his resilience and dedication to his work, even during the difficult times of World War II. Weisner's discoveries and interpretations of rate-controlled polarographic currents earned him recognition and respect from his peers, including his mentor, Professor Heyrovsky. Throughout his life, Weisner remained committed to his country and its history, as well as to his family and scientific pursuits.'
