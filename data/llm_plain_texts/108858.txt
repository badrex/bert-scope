tldr: This article presents experimental research on the transmission of electric signals through submarine cables. The author, Fleeming Jenkin, investigates the effects of various cable lengths and battery power on signal transmission. The study confirms previous theories and introduces modifications to improve the speed of legible transmission. The research is significant for understanding the practical applications of submarine cables and their underlying principles.
