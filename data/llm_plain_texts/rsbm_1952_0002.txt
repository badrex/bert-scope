tldr: Boris Petrovitch Babkin was a renowned Russian physiologist who made significant contributions to the fields of digestive system physiology and conditioned reflexes. His work focused on pancreatic secretion, the representation of the gastrointestinal tract in the cerebral cortex, and the double innervation of digestive glands. Babkin's research led to a better understanding of the complex interactions between the nervous and humoral systems in the regulation of digestive processes.
