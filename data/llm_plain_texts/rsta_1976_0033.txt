tldr: This article discusses the observations of five major eruptions in the solar atmosphere, specifically eruptive prominences, recorded by the X-ray spectroheliograph on Skylab. The authors describe the various forms and characteristics of these prominences, as well as the processes involved in their formation and development. The observations provide valuable insights into the complex and dynamic nature of the solar atmosphere.
