tldr: This article discusses the development and potential applications of biosensors, which are analytical devices that combine biological recognition systems with electrochemical, optical, or other transducers to detect and measure specific analytes in samples. The author highlights the growing importance of biosensors in various fields, such as healthcare, agriculture, environmental monitoring, and bioprocessing industries. The article also covers advancements in sensor architectures, transducers, and biorecognition systems, as well as the performance criteria and future prospects for biosensors.
