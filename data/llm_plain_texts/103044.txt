tldr: This article compares the rainfall patterns in Upminster, Essex, and Paris over 18 years, with a focus on the dry year of 1714. The author, W. Derham, also discusses the potential health impacts of the drought, such as contagious diseases among animals and humans, and the effects on the environment. The article includes a table of rainfall data for Upminster and a comparison with the data from the Paris Observatory.
