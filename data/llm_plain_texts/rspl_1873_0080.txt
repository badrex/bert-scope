tldr: Prof. Wyville Thomson shares his observations and findings from deep-sea dredgings and soundings in the South Atlantic during a voyage from the Cape of Good Hope to Australia. He discusses the abundance of animal life, the composition of the ocean floor, and the possible sources of the materials found at various depths. The study also highlights the importance of Foraminifera in understanding the origin of deep-sea sediments.
