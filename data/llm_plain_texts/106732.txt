tldr: This article provides a detailed botanical description of the Benjamin Tree of Sumatra, correcting previous misconceptions about its classification. The author, Jonas Dryander, explains how earlier botanists, including Linnaeus, had misidentified the tree and presents new evidence based on dried specimens collected by Mr. Marsden. The article concludes that the tree belongs to the Styrax genus according to Linnaeus' classification.
