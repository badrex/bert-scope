tldr: The Royal Society of London held its annual meeting on June 8, 1871, to elect new fellows. Among the elected members, William Henry Besant, George William Callender, and others were admitted into the Society. During the meeting, Prof. R. Owen presented a paper on the fossil mammals of Australia, specifically the genus Nototherium, an extinct large marsupial herbivore.
