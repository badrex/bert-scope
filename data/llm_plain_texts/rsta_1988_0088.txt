tldr: This article discusses the tectonic evolution of the Himalayas and Tibet, focusing on the role of granites in the process. It describes five major plutonic belts related to the Mesozoic orogenic evolution of the region and reviews their tectonic settings. The article highlights the differences between the Karakoram and Transhimalaya belts and explores the factors that may have influenced their development.
