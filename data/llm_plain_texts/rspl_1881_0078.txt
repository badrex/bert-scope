tldr: The author studied the anatomy of the Chiton's kidney and found that it is a paired gland with paired openings into the pallial groove and pericardium. The kidney has a unique structure with branched glandular caeca opening into a duct. The author also compared the arrangement of the Chiton's kidney to that of Anodon, finding a close agreement in their kidney structure.
