tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Frank Campbell MacIntosh, a renowned physiologist and researcher in the autonomic nervous system. Born in Cape Breton, Nova Scotia, MacIntosh made significant contributions to the understanding of synaptic transmission and the role of acetylcholine in the nervous system. He worked under notable mentors and conducted groundbreaking research at the National Institute for Medical Research in Hampstead and McGill University in Montreal. MacIntosh's work had a lasting impact on the field of neurophysiology and he was admired and loved by his colleagues and students.
