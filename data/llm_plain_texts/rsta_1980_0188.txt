tldr: The article presents a detailed study of the geology and volcanic history of Longonot Volcano in Central Kenya. The volcano consists of peralkaline trachyte lavas and pyroclastics, with seven episodes of activity recognized. The study emphasizes the importance of understanding time relations and original volumes of volcanic products for accurate sampling and analysis of the volcano's geochemistry.
