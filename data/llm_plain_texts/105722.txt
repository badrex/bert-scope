tldr: In 1761, an earthquake was felt across Siberia, affecting various locations along the frontier lines. The shaking was observed to be from east to west, and the event lasted around 12 minutes. Although some damage was reported, such as cracked earth and broken earthenware, no significant harm was caused. This event highlights the importance of understanding and studying earthquakes in the region.
