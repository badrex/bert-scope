tldr: Hans Pettersson was a renowned Swedish oceanographer who made significant contributions to the field of oceanography, including the study of tides, coastal water movements, and internal waves. He also conducted research on radioactivity in the ocean, light penetration, and plankton growth. Pettersson's work helped advance the understanding of oceanic processes and improve oceanographic instruments and techniques.
