tldr: In a letter to the Royal Society, William Hamilton shares his observations and experiences during his journey to Mount Etna. He describes the formation of the volcano, the surrounding landscape, and the devastation caused by the eruption in 1669. Hamilton also discusses the lava's impact on the environment and the local population's resilience despite the constant threat of eruptions.
