tldr: This article discusses the applications of mass spectrometry in the study of porphyrins and related tetrapyrroles. It covers the use of electron impact mass spectrometry, field desorption mass spectrometry, and other ionization techniques in the structure determination of various porphyrin derivatives. The article also highlights the importance of mass spectrometry in biosynthetic studies and the analysis of complex metastable fragmentation processes in chlorophyll derivatives.
