tldr: In 1757, an extraordinary hailstorm struck Virginia, causing significant damage to buildings and gardens. Lieutenant Governor Francis Fauquier documented the event, describing the size and shape of the hailstones and the impact on his own property. The storm also coincided with unusually high temperatures in the region.
