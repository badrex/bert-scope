tldr: This article describes the observation of sunspots made at the Royal Academy of Paris in August 1671. The author notes the appearance, movement, and shape of the sunspots, as well as their correlation with the Sun's apparent diameter. The observations provide insights into the sunspots' diurnal motion and their potential periodical revolution, which is estimated to be around 27 days and a half.
