tldr: The author examines the structure and affinities of Trigonocarpon, a fossil fruit found in the Coal-Measures. They discover that its structure and modifications of elementary organs are highly developed, similar to existing coniferous plants. The author suggests a relationship between Trigonocarpon and the Chinese genus Salisburia, based on their similar fruit structures.
