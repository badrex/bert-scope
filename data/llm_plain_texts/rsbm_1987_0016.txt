tldr: 'The article is a biographical memoir of Leonard Harrison Matthews, a renowned zoologist and naturalist. He was a traveling naturalist who visited various regions, including South America, the Falklands, South Georgia, the Antarctic, the Arctic, Europe, and Africa. His extensive knowledge of animals and their habits was incorporated in a long series of research papers and popular books. He was a member of the Discovery Investigations and contributed significantly to the understanding of the fauna of the Bristol Channel and the Antarctic region. He also worked on developing scientific instruments with his brother, Sir Bryan Matthews.'
