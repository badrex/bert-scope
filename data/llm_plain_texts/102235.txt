tldr: This treatise provides a detailed guide to the art of assaying and refining various types of metals, including silver, gold, copper, and lead. The author, Sir John Pettus, explains the process of determining the purity and content of metals, as well as the tools and techniques used in the process. The book is divided into five sections, covering silver, gold, copper, lead, and mineral salts, with illustrations of instruments and operations.
