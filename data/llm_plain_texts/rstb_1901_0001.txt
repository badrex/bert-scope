tldr: The author examines the fundus oculi of 182 mammal species using ophthalmoscopy and provides detailed observations on eye color, vascularization, optic axes, irides, and pupils. They also discuss the influence of domestication on eye characteristics and classify mammalian eyes into three main types based on eye color. The study contributes to the understanding of the anatomy and physiology of the mammalian eye.
