tldr: This article presents the magnetic observations made during Captain James Clark Ross's Antarctic expedition on board the ships Erebus and Terror. It discusses the challenges of conducting magnetic surveys on board iron ships and the methods used to correct the magnetic data. The author provides a detailed analysis of the magnetic corrections and the constants involved in the calculations.
