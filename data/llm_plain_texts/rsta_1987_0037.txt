tldr: This article discusses the importance of accurate and reliable plasma measurements in the JET project, a fusion experiment. It reviews the various diagnostic systems used to measure plasma parameters, such as density, temperature, and energy input and loss mechanisms. The article highlights the challenges faced in measuring high-temperature plasmas and the need for cross-calibration and careful design to improve measurement accuracy.
