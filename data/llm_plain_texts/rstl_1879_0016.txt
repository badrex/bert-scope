tldr: The author presents a detailed account of the Polyzoa species collected during the Transit of Venus Expeditions in Kerguelen Island and Rodriguez in 1874-75. The article focuses on the zoological collections, specifically the Polyzoa species, and provides a list of 26 species, including nine forms previously undescribed. The author discusses the distribution of these species, their habitat, and the unique characteristics of each species.
