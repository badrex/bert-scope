tldr: The obituary of Charles Frederick Arden-Close, a prominent surveyor and geodesist, highlights his contributions to the Survey of India and West African Surveys. Arden-Close's work in India involved topographic and geodetic surveys, while in West Africa, he helped map the boundary region between the Niger Coast Protectorate and the German colony of Kamerun. His dedication to surveying and geodesy had a significant impact on the field and his contemporaries.
