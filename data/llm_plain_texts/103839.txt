tldr: The author proposes a plan to improve the understanding of Russian history by publishing separate pieces of historical research and authentic documents related to the Empire. The Imperial Academy of Sciences aims to gather materials for a future complete history and encourage contributions from other researchers. The plan includes collecting historical manuscripts, biographies of Russian rulers, geographical information, descriptions of cities, and more. This initiative aims to bring to light previously unknown or insufficiently researched aspects of Russian history.
