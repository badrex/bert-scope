tldr: This volume contains the Obituary Notices of deceased Fellows of the Royal Society from the Year-Books for 1900 and 1901. It also includes a general index to all the Obituaries that have appeared in the "Proceedings" since their publication began in 1860. The obituary notices will be issued with the "Proceedings" in the future, forming appendices to the volumes.
