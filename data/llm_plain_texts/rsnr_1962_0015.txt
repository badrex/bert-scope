tldr: This article is an appreciation of Sir Charles Sherrington, a renowned physiologist who discovered the action of reflex units within the central nervous system and contributed to understanding functional integration within the brain. Sherrington also explored the philosophy of the mind and received the Nobel Prize for his work. The article highlights his life, achievements, and personal qualities, as well as his transition from physiology to philosophy in his later years.
