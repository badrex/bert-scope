tldr: This article describes the anatomy and physiology of an ostrich, focusing on its digestive system, organs, and muscles. The authors provide detailed observations on the ostrich's ventricles, spleen, kidneys, liver, heart, and eyes, comparing them to human anatomy and other birds. They also discuss the animal's diet and health, as well as the differences between their findings and those of the Royal Academy at Paris.
