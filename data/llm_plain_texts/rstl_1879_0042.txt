tldr: The author describes the petrological, botanical, and zoological collections made in Kerguelen's Land and Rodriguez during the Transit of Venus Expeditions. They discuss the unique limestone caves found on Rodriguez, which contain marine shells and extinct land species. The author also examines the bone-earth found in the caves, which is mixed with coralline sand and contains remains of various land snails and a large admixture of land shells.
