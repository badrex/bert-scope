tldr: The article describes the discovery and morphology of Trypanosoma simi\#xE6;, a new species of trypanosome that attacks both monkeys and goats. The trypanosome is carried by tsetse flies and causes a chronic disease in goats and a rapidly fatal disease in monkeys. The article provides detailed descriptions of the trypanosome's size, shape, nucleus, micronucleus, undulating membrane, and flagellum.
