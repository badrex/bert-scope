tldr: This biographical memoir recounts the life and career of Richard van der Riet Woolley, the eleventh Astronomer Royal. Born into a family with connections to academia and science, Woolley pursued his passion for astronomy at the University of Cape Town, where he excelled in physics and mathematics. His career led him to play significant roles in extending optical astronomy in three continents and upholding the prestigious tradition of the Astronomer Royal at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich.
