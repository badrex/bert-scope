tldr: The author investigates microseisms associated with disturbed weather in the Indian seas, particularly during the south-west monsoon and storms in the Arabian Sea and Bay of Bengal. By analyzing the periods and amplitudes of microseisms, the author explains their relationship with weather disturbances and proposes a new method for forecasting storms and the advance of the monsoon. This research provides valuable information for meteorologists and seismologists.
