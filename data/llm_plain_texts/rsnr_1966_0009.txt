tldr: In 1965, a delegation from the U.S.S.R. Academy of Sciences visited the United Kingdom to meet British scientists and discuss the working of the Royal Society/U.S.S.R. Academy of Sciences agreement on scientific exchanges. The delegation attended various scientific visits, meetings, and events, including a reception in their honor and a tour of the Atomic Energy Research Establishment. The visit aimed to strengthen international scientific collaboration between the two countries.
