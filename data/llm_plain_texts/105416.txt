tldr: The author, Keane Fitzgerald, describes the construction and working of a new metalline thermometer that measures temperature using metal bars and levers. This innovative instrument can measure temperature more accurately than previous devices and can also be used as a pyrometer to measure the expansion of metal bars. The thermometer has potential for various applications in scientific inquiries and daily life.
