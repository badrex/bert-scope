tldr: This article presents meteorological observations and climate patterns from 1724 to 1725 in Petersburgh and Lunden, focusing on the barometer, thermometer, winds, and weather. The author, Wm. Derham, provides an abstract of the observations made by Mr. Tho. Consett, highlighting the differences in climate between the two locations and the impact of cold weather on rainfall. The article also includes an appendix with additional observations from Lunden, comparing the barometer and thermometer readings to those from Petersburgh.
