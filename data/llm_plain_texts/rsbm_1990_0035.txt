tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Evgenii Mikhailovich Lifshitz, a renowned physicist who made significant contributions to theoretical physics, particularly in the areas of general relativity and the Landau-Lifshitz theory. Lifshitz's life and work are discussed, including his close relationship with fellow physicist Lev Landau and his role in the publication of the Course of Theoretical Physics. His dedication to his work and his impact on the scientific community are highlighted.
