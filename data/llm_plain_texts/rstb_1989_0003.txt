tldr: The study examines the ontogenetic changes in the skull of the Carboniferous tetrapod Greererpeton burkemorani. It reveals that allometric changes occur in the increase of area in some circumorbital bones, the width of the skull table, the length and width of the braincase, and the anteromesial shift in the stapedial foot plate. The position of the orbits does not shift posteriorly over the growth interval studied.
