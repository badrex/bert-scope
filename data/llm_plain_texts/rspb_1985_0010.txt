tldr: The author reviews the scientific and medical advancements that led to the development of in vitro fertilization (IVF) and its current status. They discuss the methods used for ovarian stimulation, oocyte maturation, and fertilization, as well as the challenges faced in embryo implantation and the potential for embryo 'helping' and multiple pregnancies. The author also highlights the research potential in various disciplines and the impact on the ethics of human reproduction and embryology.
