tldr: Dr. Robert Hooke invented a marine barometer to help predict weather changes at sea. He later replaced the mercury barometer with a sealed thermometer that included spirit of wine, making it more portable and accurate. This new instrument allowed sailors to better prepare for storms and other unfavorable weather conditions.
