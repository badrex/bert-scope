tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of Wilder Graves Penfield, a prominent figure in Canadian neurology and neurosurgery. Penfield is credited with the development of physiological neurosurgery and was a key figure in the founding of the Montreal Neurological Institute. His work on cerebral localization and epilepsy contributed to his scientific distinction and established him as one of the leading figures in neurology and neurosurgery.
