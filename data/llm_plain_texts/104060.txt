tldr: Captain Walter Hoxton reported an unusual agitation in the magnetic needle of his compass during a voyage from Maryland. The compass and other navigational tools were affected, making it difficult to steer the ship. The issue resolved on its own after an hour. This observation highlights the challenges in navigation and the importance of understanding the behavior of magnetic compasses.
