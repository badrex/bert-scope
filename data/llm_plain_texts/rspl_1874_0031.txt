The author discusses the similarities in the assemblage of animal forms found in various locations and the temperature distribution in the ocean. The 'Challenger' explored the east coast of New Zealand, the Kermadec Islands, Tongatabu, Fiji, and the New Hebrides, collecting samples and data on the marine life and geological formations.
