tldr: This study investigates the temperature variations in the human body during health, focusing on diurnal changes. The researchers observed two boys, one robust and one delicate, and found that the temperature reaches its highest point in the morning and falls significantly in the evening, with a quick rise in the early morning hours. The study provides insights into understanding temperature fluctuations in healthy individuals and the importance of accurate temperature measurements.
