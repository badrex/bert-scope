tldr: The author, Rev. E. A. Eaton, shares his observations and discoveries during the 1874 transit-of-Venus expedition to Kerguelen's Island. He discusses various species of birds, mammals, plants, and algae found on the island, as well as the unique behaviors and adaptations of these organisms. Eaton also reports on the challenges faced during the expedition and the specimens collected for further study.
