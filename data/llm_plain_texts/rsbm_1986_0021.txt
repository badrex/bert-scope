tldr: Richard Weck was a civil and structural engineer who made significant contributions to welding research and engineering during his career. He worked on electric arc furnaces, steel foundries, and the development of welded ship hulls during World War II. Weck also conducted research on residual stress in welded construction and played a crucial role in the British Welding Research Association.
