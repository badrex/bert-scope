tldr: This article honors the memory of Antoine Laurent Lavoisier, a great French scientist who revolutionized chemistry and made significant contributions to various fields, including geology, meteorology, and public administration. Lavoisier's work in the Ferme, a financial corporation, and his involvement in the Academy of Sciences demonstrate his dedication to both pure science and practical applications. His influence on modern chemistry and his contributions to various scientific disciplines make him one of the immortals in the history of science.
