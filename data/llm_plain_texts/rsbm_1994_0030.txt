tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of William Charles Evans, a renowned scientist who made significant contributions to medical biochemistry, agriculture, animal physiology, and microbial biochemistry. Born into a humble family in Wales, Evans overcame adversity to pursue a successful career in science, studying at the University College of North Wales and later working at the University of Manchester Medical School and the University of Leeds. His research focused on the biochemistry of bacteria, enzyme mechanisms, and the influence of growth media on microbial metabolism. Evans' work had a lasting impact on the scientific community and his life story serves as an inspiration for aspiring scientists.
