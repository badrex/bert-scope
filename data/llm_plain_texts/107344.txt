tldr: This article discusses the solar eclipse that was supposedly predicted by Thales, an ancient Greek philosopher. The author examines various dates and opinions on when the eclipse occurred and how it may have affected the war between Cyaxares, king of Media, and Alyattes, king of Lydia. The author also considers the importance of accurate astronomical calculations in determining the date of the eclipse and its impact on historical chronology.
