tldr: This article describes the various skills and techniques of the indigenous people in terms of their arts, crafts, and medicine. It highlights their expertise in making fine textiles, glass and china ware, and their unique methods of making aqua vitae, cooling water vessels, and grinding sugar cane. It also discusses their use of natural resources for construction, such as sea shells for lime and betel leaves for chawing. Furthermore, the article mentions their medical practices, including the use of wolfs-bane, arsenic, and sulphur, and their methods for diagnosing diseases.
