tldr: The article discusses the life and charitable intentions of Robert Boyle, a prominent scientist of the Royal Society. Upon his death, Boyle left a significant portion of his estate for the advancement of Christianity among infidels. This led to the establishment of the famous Boyle Lecture, which aimed to prove the Christian religion against notorious infidels. The article also highlights the legal complications that arose during the settlement of Boyle's residuary estate.
