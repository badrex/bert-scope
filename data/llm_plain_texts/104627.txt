tldr: This article provides a detailed account of the Roman Camp at Castor in Norfolk, England, along with a plan of the site and a description of an observed halo or mock-sun. The author examines the camp's dimensions, fortifications, and surrounding geography, offering insights into the Roman presence in the region and the possible use of the camp as a strategic military location. The article also discusses the discovery of numerous Roman medals in and around the camp, suggesting their use as currency.
