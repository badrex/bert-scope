tldr: The author shares his experience with injecting medicated liquors into the abdomen to treat ascites. He reports the case of a patient who has remained stable after the procedure despite experiencing a tertian ague. The author expresses gratitude for the recognition from the Royal Society and shares his discovery of using a single puncture for injections. This method allowed him to extract nearly fifty pints of dropsical lymph, contributing to the treatment's success.
