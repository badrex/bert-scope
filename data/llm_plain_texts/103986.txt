tldr: In a letter to Dr. Alexander Stuart, Mr. John Cope shares the discovery of an ancient chimney piece at Widgel-Hall in Hertfordshire. The date on the piece, expressed in Roman numerals and Indian figures, is the earliest known use of Indian figures in England. The carving is in good condition despite being 718 years old and never painted over.
