tldr: The author studied the physical structure of the Old Red Sandstone conglomerate in County Waterford, focusing on cleavage, joint surfaces, and faults. They discovered three distinct types of physical planes in the rock and determined the position, strike, inclination, and downthrows of various faults. The author also discussed the differences between joint and cleavage planes and their significance in understanding the rock's formation.
