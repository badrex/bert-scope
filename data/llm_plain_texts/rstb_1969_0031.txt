tldr: This article presents a preliminary analysis of the palm flora in the Solomon Islands, based on limited material and literature. It discusses the distribution of palm genera and species in the region, their relationships, and how they contribute to the understanding of the Solomon Islands' flora. The author also highlights the unique case of the introduced palm, _Pritchardia henryana_, and its implications for the island's phytogeography and biogeography.
