tldr: This obituary highlights the life and scientific achievements of Joseph John Thomson, a prominent physicist known for his work on electromagnetic theory, electric discharge, and the discovery of the electron. Thomson made significant contributions to the understanding of X-rays and cathode rays, and his research led to the development of the modern electron microscope. His work has had a lasting impact on the field of physics.
