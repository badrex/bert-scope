tldr: This article is a biographical memoir of Meirion Thomas, a notable figure in the field of education and research. Thomas spent most of his career at the University of Newcastle upon Tyne, where he set high standards for research and education. His life, marked by discipline and devotion to duty, had a significant impact on the university and those who knew him. Thomas's early life, family background, and experiences in World War I shaped his personality and career, leading him to become a respected and influential figure in the world of education.
