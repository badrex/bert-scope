tldr: The author investigates the production of pictures on the retina of the human eye and their various factors, such as the time of viewing, light intensity, and eyelid compression. They discovered that retinal pictures can have different colors, endurance, and changes depending on these factors. The author also found that low light levels can create distinct negative pictures on the retina, and that the position of the eyes and the way they view objects can affect the clarity of vision.
