tldr: 'Thomas Muir was a prominent Scottish mathematician and educator who made significant contributions to the field of mathematics and education in South Africa. He played a crucial role in shaping the Cape educational system and was a pioneer in promoting research and teaching of mathematics. Muir authored a five-volume work on determinants and left behind a valuable mathematical collection, which is now considered one of the best in the British Empire.'
