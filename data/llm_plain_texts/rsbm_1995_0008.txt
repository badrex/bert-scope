tldr: 'David Guthrie Catcheside was a prominent figure in post-war genetics, making significant contributions to plant genetics, cytology, fungal biochemical genetics, and bryology. He played a crucial role in launching the next generation of geneticists and was responsible for several new institutional developments in genetics and microbiology. Catcheside's work on Oenothera and his time at the John Innes Horticultural Institution greatly influenced his career and research.'
