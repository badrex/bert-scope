tldr: This article discusses the advancements in measuring the electric and magnetic moments of the neutron. It covers various experiments and techniques, such as the use of electric fields, nuclear magnetic resonance, and the Ramsey separated oscillatory field method, to determine the neutron's magnetic dipole moment, electric dipole moment, and charge. The article also highlights the importance of these moments in the development of the theory of fundamental particles.
