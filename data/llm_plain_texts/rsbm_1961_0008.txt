tldr: Peter Alfred Gorer was a renowned pathologist, immunologist, and geneticist who made significant contributions to the fields of serology and serological genetics of tissue transplantation. His work on antigenic variation in mice laid the foundation for understanding tissue transplantation and its implications in medicine. Gorer also conducted research on cancer and was a pioneer in the study of the relationship between the antigenic composition of tumors and their transplantability.
