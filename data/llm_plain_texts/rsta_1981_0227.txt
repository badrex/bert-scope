tldr: This article provides a historical overview of the concept of life originating from outer space and its impact on the development of biology. It discusses various theories and beliefs held by scientists and philosophers throughout history, from the 17th century to the 20th century, about the possibility of life on other planets and the influence of extraterrestrial life on Earth. The article also explores the connection between cosmochemistry and the origin of life, as well as the role of extraterrestrial "seeding" in the evolution of life on Earth.
