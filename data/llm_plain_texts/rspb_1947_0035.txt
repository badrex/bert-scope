Both scientists made significant contributions to their respective fields, with Hopkins focusing on the chemistry of living cells and Planck developing the quantum theory of energy. The address also recognizes the achievements of other distinguished scientists, including Godfrey Hardy, Cyril Hinshelwood, and Frank Burnet, who have made significant contributions to mathematics, chemical kinetics, and virology.
