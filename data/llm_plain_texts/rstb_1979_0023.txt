tldr: This article reviews historical records of giant tortoise populations in the western Indian Ocean, focusing on their decline and extinction on many islands during the 17th to 19th centuries. The authors discuss the causes of decline and extinction and consider the implications for the history of the Aldabra population. They also examine archival evidence and literature to provide a comprehensive understanding of the distribution and history of giant tortoises in the region.
