tldr: The article discusses the invention of a new method for grinding optic glasses by the astronomer Hevelius. He claims that his method allows for easy creation of optic glasses of any conic section without significant risk of failure. Hevelius plans to describe his method in his Celestial Machine and present it to the Royal Society for examination. Additionally, the article mentions Hugens' plans to try something similar and du Son's work on perfecting telescopes using parabolic figure glasses.
