tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of Alexander, Earl of Athlone, who had a versatile and industrious public life. He served in various high governmental positions, including Governor-General of Canada and South Africa. His significant contributions to medical education and hospital administration, particularly at the Middlesex Hospital and the University of London, are highlighted. Earl of Athlone played a crucial role in the development of postgraduate medical education and nursing, as well as in the governance of the University of London.
