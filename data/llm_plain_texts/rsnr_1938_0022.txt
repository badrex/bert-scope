tldr: The Royal Society took responsibility for the Bermuda Oceanographical Committee, which aimed to develop oceanographic research based on the Bermuda Biological Station. They acquired a research vessel, the Culver, to conduct hydrographic observations and study the Gulf Stream's fluctuations. The committee also strengthened the Bermuda Biological Station's resources and added more scientific staff to support the research.
