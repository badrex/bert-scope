tldr: This article discusses the use of semiconductor laser amplifiers for ultrafast optical signal processing. The authors describe various applications, such as wavelength conversion, demultiplexing, and optical phase conjugation, using four-wave mixing. They also explore the potential for all-optical signal processing and the advantages of using semiconductor laser amplifiers as functional devices in communication systems.
