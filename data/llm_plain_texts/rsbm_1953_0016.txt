tldr: Robert Newstead was a prominent British naturalist, entomologist, and archaeologist. He made significant contributions to the study of scale insects and insect pests of agriculture. Newstead also played a crucial role in the development of medical entomology and served as a consultant for insect prophylaxis during World War I. In his later years, he became an accomplished archaeologist, conducting excavations in Chester and Prestatyn, North Wales. Newstead's dedication to his work and his genuine kindness made him a respected figure in the scientific community.
