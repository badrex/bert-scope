tldr: The author investigates the function of neuromelanin in catecholamine neurons in the brain, suggesting that it may protect cells against toxic quinones produced from dopamine and noradrenaline during prostaglandin synthesis. Neuromelanin has been linked to the pathogenesis of Parkinson's disease and schizophrenia, and the author proposes further research to understand its role in these disorders and the potential protective mechanisms it provides.
