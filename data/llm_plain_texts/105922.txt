tldr: In 1770, Abbe Chappe observed the Transit of Venus from California. Despite being ill, he continued his observations for eight days. His journals and manuscripts were later deposited at the Royal Observatory. This observation contributed to the understanding of the Transit of Venus and the Royal Observatory's collection of scientific data.
