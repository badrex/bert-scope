tldr: The article is an obituary for Francois Antoine Alfred Lacroix, a renowned French mineralogist and organizer. Lacroix made significant contributions to the fields of mineralogy, petrography, and geology throughout his career, conducting extensive research and collecting valuable specimens. He was a professor of mineralogy at the Paris Museum and played a crucial role in organizing scientific meetings and managing archives and libraries. His work focused on understanding the complete history of minerals and their evolution through geological processes.
