tldr: In 1752, four Roman inscriptions were discovered on three large stones in a ploughed field near Wroxeter in Shropshire. The inscriptions provided information about the individuals they honored, including their names, military service, and family background. The stones were moved to a church in Wroxeter for display. This discovery contributed to the understanding of Roman history and culture in the region.
