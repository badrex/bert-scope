tldr: Herbert Harold Read was a prominent geologist who made significant contributions to the understanding of Scottish rocks and regional metamorphism. His work in the Scottish Highlands and with the Geological Survey of Great Britain led to important advances in interpreting the structure and succession of the Dalradian rocks, understanding the significance of regional metamorphism, and studying the reactions between gabbros and surrounding rocks. Read's dedication to his work and love for the countryside greatly influenced his career and scientific achievements.
