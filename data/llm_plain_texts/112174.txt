tldr: This article describes the observation of a sunspot over a period of time as it rotates on the Sun's surface. The author, John Phillips, provides detailed descriptions and illustrations of the sunspot's changes in shape, size, and surrounding features such as faculae and penumbra. The observations were made using a telescope and documented at various dates throughout October and November of 1865.
