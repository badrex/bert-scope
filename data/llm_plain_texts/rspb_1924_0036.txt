tldr: The article discusses the Balfour family's history and the life of John Hutton Balfour, a prominent botanist. Balfour made significant contributions to the field of botany, including founding the Botanical Society of Edinburgh and the Edinburgh Botanical Club, and serving as a professor of botany at Glasgow and Edinburgh universities. He was also the Regius Keeper and Queen's Botanist for Scotland. His work focused on plant arrangement, plant distribution, and plant qualities, and he played a crucial role in the mountain botanical survey.
