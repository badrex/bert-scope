tldr: George Claridge Druce was a renowned British botanist and naturalist who made significant contributions to the study of British flora. He compiled Floras of the counties in the Thames Valley and was a key figure in the establishment of the Botanical Society of the British Isles. Druce's extensive fieldwork and knowledge of British plants in their natural habitats were unparalleled, and he traveled widely to study plants in various locations.
