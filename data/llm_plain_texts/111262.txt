tldr: The author, William Shanks, extended the value of the base of Napier's logarithms to 205 decimal places using formulae investigated by Mr. J. R. Young. The article provides the computed values for the logarithms of 2, 3, 5, and 10, as well as the modulus of the common system of logarithms.
