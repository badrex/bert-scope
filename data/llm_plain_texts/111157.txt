tldr: This study examines the temperature records from the Highfield House Observatory in Nottingham from 1810 to 1850. The author, Edward Joseph Lowe, compares the data to previous records from nearby Lenton House and analyzes the mean temperatures for each month, year, and season. The coldest month was January 1814, while the hottest month was July 1847. The study also highlights the coldest and hottest years, as well as the temperature trends for each season.
