tldr: The authors conducted a study on the absence or diminution of free hydrochloric acid in the gastric contents of patients with malignant diseases. They found that the absence of free hydrochloric acid is more common in cancer of the stomach than in other diseases. The study also highlights the importance of considering the relationship between the amount of free hydrochloric acid and the disease for better diagnosis and understanding of the condition.
