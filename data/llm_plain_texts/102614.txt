tldr: This article presents a case study of a woman with unusual ovarian swellings and fluid accumulation. Despite various treatments, the woman eventually underwent a paracentesis, which resulted in the discharge of large quantities of fluid from her abdomen. Upon dissection, it was found that the fluid-filled bags were located in the affected ovary. This case highlights the challenges in diagnosing and treating such conditions during the time period.
