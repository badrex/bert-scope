tldr: Margaret Cutting, a young woman from Suffolk, lost her tongue due to cancer at the age of four but still managed to speak fluently and intelligibly. Despite the absence of her tongue, she could read, sing, and even distinguish tastes. This extraordinary case highlights the complexity of human speech and the resilience of the human body.
