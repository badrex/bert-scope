tldr: In a letter from Mr. Perry to Dr. Stukeley, the author describes the effects of the earthquake that occurred in the Island of Sumatra in November and December 1756. The earthquake caused damage to buildings, cracked the ground, and released sulphureous earth and water. This event was part of a series of twelve shocks felt in the region between November 3, 1756, and December 3, 1757.
