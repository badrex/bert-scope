tldr: Otto Stapf was a renowned botanist who specialized in plant taxonomy and systematics. He made significant contributions to the study of grasses and worked on various plant species from different parts of the world. Stapf also had a deep interest in plant morphology, anatomy, and the geographical distribution of plants. He was a dedicated researcher, skilled in drawing, and had a keen eye for detail, which made him an excellent mentor for many budding botanists.
