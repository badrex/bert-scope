tldr: This paper discusses the effects of magnesia in preventing the formation of uric acid and the influence of acids on urine composition. The author, William Thomas Brande, presents cases where magnesia was used to treat individuals with issues related to uric acid and other mineral deposits in the urine. He also explores the use of different acids, such as muriatic, carbonic, and citric acids, in treating these conditions.
