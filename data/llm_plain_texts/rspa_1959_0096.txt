tldr: This article presents the spin-orbit interaction matrices for the configuration f and applies the theory to neodymium. The authors tabulate the spin-orbit interaction matrix elements using group theoretical methods to help assign values to energy levels observed in neodymium salts. They also discuss various Zeeman effects and provide a simple explanation for one feature of the spectrum of neodymium.
