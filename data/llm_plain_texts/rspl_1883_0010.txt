tldr: This article investigates the formation of uric acid in the animal economy and its relationship to hippuric acid. The author examines the solubility of uric acid and its salts, the action of ammonium and sodium urates on chlorides and phosphates, and the composition of urinary excretion in various animals. The author also discusses the presence of uric acid in the spleen, liver, and other organs, as well as the mutual destructive influence of uric and hippuric acids.
