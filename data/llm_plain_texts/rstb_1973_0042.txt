tldr: This study assesses the daily energy expenditure of Yemenite and Kurdish Jews in Israel during summer and winter. The majority of male subjects were farmers, while women were mainly housewives. The energy expenditure of men in the summer was 3,050 kcal per day for both Yemenite and Kurdish Jews, while women expended 2,280 kcal per day in the summer and 2,400 kcal per day in the winter. The study also recorded climatic conditions, observed subjects' activities, and measured their energy expenditure in five villages in Israel.
