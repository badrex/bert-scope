tldr: This article discusses the impact of human activities on groundwater conditions in the Thames Flood Plain deposits of Central London. The authors examine how factors such as underground railway systems and river walls have altered the natural groundwater conditions. They also explore the potential implications of the proposed Thames Barrier on these conditions and its potential effects on drainage and basement structures.
