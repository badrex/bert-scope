tldr: This article is a biography of Henry Frederick Baker, a prominent mathematician and geometer who made significant contributions to the fields of function theory, differential equations, and geometry. Baker was a founder of a vigorous school of geometry and is known for his work on algebraic functions, birational invariants of surfaces, and matrices. He was also a dedicated teacher and mentor, inspiring many students throughout his career.
