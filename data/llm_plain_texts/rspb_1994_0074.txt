tldr: This study examines the cellular morphology and cone contacts of horizontal cells generating triphasic responses in the retina of a cyprinid fish, Rutilus rutilus. The research finds that these horizontal cells selectively contact short wavelength-sensitive cone photoreceptors, supporting the feedback cascade model of horizontal cell chromatic connectivity in the cyprinid fish retina.
