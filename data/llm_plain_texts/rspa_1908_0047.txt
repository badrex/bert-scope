tldr: This obituary celebrates the life and accomplishments of Marcellin Berthelot, a prominent French chemist known for his work in organic chemistry and education reform. Born in Paris in 1827, Berthelot made significant contributions to the field of chemistry and held various positions in the French government, including Minister of Education and Foreign Minister. He was a devoted husband and had a close friendship with fellow Frenchman, Renan. Berthelot's life and career are remembered for his dedication to science, education, and his country.
