tldr: Thomas Henry Holland was a prominent geologist and administrator who significantly contributed to the field of Indian geology. He served as the Director of the Geological Survey of India and made numerous improvements to the department, raising its status and prestige. Holland also played a crucial role in the development of geology education and the promotion of practical applications of geology in India. His contributions to the field and his administrative abilities left a lasting impact on the geological community.
