tldr: The authors studied the bonding in group IV tetrahalides using photoelectron spectroscopy. They analyzed the ionization potential data and molecular orbital structures of these compounds, providing insights into the bonding and electronic properties of the molecules. Their findings reveal the effects of halogen interactions and the role of central atom valence in the bonding of these compounds.
