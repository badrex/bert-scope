tldr: This article discusses the role of viruses in human cancers and the potential for vaccine prevention. The author highlights the connection between viruses like Epstein-Barr, Hepatitis B, and Human Papillomavirus with various types of cancer. They also describe the development of vaccines against these viruses, such as the prototype subunit preparation based on Epstein-Barr virus-determined membrane antigen, and the impact of Hepatitis B vaccines on preventing liver cancer. The author emphasizes the importance of further research and development of vaccines against virus-induced cancers.
