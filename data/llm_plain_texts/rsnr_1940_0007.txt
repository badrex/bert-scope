tldr: Thomas Fairchild, a renowned gardener and member of the Royal Society, made significant contributions to botany and plant hybridization. He was the first person to artificially create a plant hybrid, Dianthus barbatus, and conducted experiments proving the existence of sex in plants. Fairchild's legacy includes the annual Fairchild Lecture, which is still delivered today.
