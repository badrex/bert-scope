tldr: The author investigates the role of fibrinogen intoxication in cardiac diseases. They discover that a rapid admixture of fibrinogen and blood causes clotting and other changes in the blood, leading to symptoms like oedema and haemorrhage. The author suggests that fibrinogen intoxication plays a significant role in various cardiac diseases, including valvular disease of the heart and obstruction to the circulation through the lungs.
