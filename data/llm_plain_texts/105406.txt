tldr: This article presents a detailed description and analysis of the frog-fish, a unique creature found in Surinam, South America. The author, George Edwards, examines the frog-fish's physical characteristics, behavior, and possible life cycle, including its potential transformation from a frog to a fish. Edwards also compares the frog-fish to European frogs and tadpoles, discussing their differences and similarities. The article includes illustrations and references to the works of Maria Sibylla Merian and Albert Seba, who also studied the frog-fish.
