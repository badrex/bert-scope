tldr: The author describes the uses of a centrifugal wheel for various applications, including drawing away hot steam and circulating fresh air in rooms, ships, and mines. The wheel can also improve ventilation in sick rooms, prisons, and for warming, cooling, or perfuming chambers. In mines, it can help remove dangerous gases and improve the working conditions by providing fresh and wholesome air.
