tldr: In March 1971, a Japanese scientific delegation visited the UK, meeting with representatives from various universities and signing an agreement on scientific exchanges between the Japan Academy and the Royal Society. The collaboration included research fellowships and study visit awards, funded by both the Royal Society and the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science.
