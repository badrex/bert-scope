tldr: Ralph Thoresby, a Fellow of the Royal Society, writes to Dr. Hans Sloane about Roman antiquities discovered in Yorkshire. He describes the size and materials of the monuments, which include portable altars and other commemorative structures. Thoresby also mentions the discovery of a Roman station and the presence of two houses with names related to Roman history in the area.
