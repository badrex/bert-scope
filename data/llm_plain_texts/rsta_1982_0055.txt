tldr: This article discusses recent advancements in analytical potentiometry, focusing on ion-selective electrodes (ISEs). It covers solid and liquid membrane ISEs, the use of a field-effect transistor as an ISE, and the enhancement of selectivity in anion electrodes using alkylphenol. The article also explores the potential applications of these advancements in various fields, including clinical measurements.
