tldr: This review lecture discusses the prevalence and challenges of malaria, a disease caused by Plasmodial parasites, in tropical and subtropical regions. The author highlights the failure of global eradication measures due to insecticide and drug resistance, and the need for alternative methods like vaccination. The lecture explains the complex life cycle of the malaria parasite and the factors that influence resistance to the disease. It also emphasizes the importance of developing a safe and effective malaria vaccine for global health, requiring massive international support and cooperation.
