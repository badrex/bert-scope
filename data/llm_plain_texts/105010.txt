tldr: The author presents a treatise on the Carlsbad waters' ability to dissolve kidney and bladder stones. The waters have a higher degree of solubility than lime-water and have been found to dissolve stones in experiments and real-world cases. This discovery could greatly enhance the value of these waters in treating urinary disorders.
