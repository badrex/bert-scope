tldr: This article discusses the life and achievements of Sir William Reid, a 19th-century colonial governor who played a significant role in promoting technological development and the diffusion of ideas and technology throughout the British Empire. Reid, a trained Royal Engineer and a meteorologist, served as a governor in Bermuda, Barbados, and Malta, where he implemented progressive policies in agriculture, public works, and education, drawing upon his scientific and technical experience. His efforts contributed to the exchange and diffusion of information and technologies within the empire.
