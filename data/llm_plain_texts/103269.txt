tldr: This article presents barometrical altitude and rainfall observations from Zurich, Switzerland, Pisa, Italy, and Upminster, England, conducted by Dr. Joh. Ja. Scheuchzer, Dr. Michael Angelo Tilli, and W. Derham. The authors compare their findings and discuss the differences in weather patterns, barometric pressure, and rainfall between the three locations. They also examine the effects of altitude, wind, and temperature on their observations.
