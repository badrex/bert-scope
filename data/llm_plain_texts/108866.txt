tldr: This article describes magnetic observations made in British Columbia, Washington Territory, and Vancouver Island between 1858 and 1861. The observations were conducted to determine the position and direction of magnetic lines, and the results were used to create maps of the area. The author discusses the methods used, the instruments employed, and the impact of secular change on the magnetic field.
