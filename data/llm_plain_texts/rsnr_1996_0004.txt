tldr: The article discusses a collection of drawings of Aloes and other plants made by botanical artists Georg Dionysius Ehret and Jacob van Huysum in the 18th century. These drawings were commissioned to record the collection of Aloes at the Chelsea Physic Garden and provide a unique pictorial record of the plants. The article highlights the significance of these drawings in understanding the species grown in the garden and the work of the two artists during that period.
