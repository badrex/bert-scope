tldr: John Alexander Fraser Roberts was a pioneer in medical genetics and a founding father of clinical genetics. He made significant contributions to the understanding of complex traits, blood groups, and disease, and was a leader in human genetics. His work on genetic counselling, diagnosis, and prenatal diagnosis helped improve medical services and the practical application of genetic knowledge.
