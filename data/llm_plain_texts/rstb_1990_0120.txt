tldr: This study investigates the Pleistocene sea-level and neotectonic history of the eastern Solent in southern England. The research focuses on the Bembridge Raised Beach and associated deposits, providing insights into the age, composition, and sedimentology of these interglacial formations. The findings contribute to understanding the region's geological history and the impact of neotectonic activity on the landscape.
