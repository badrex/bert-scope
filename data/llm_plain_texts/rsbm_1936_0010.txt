tldr: 'Sir George Hadcock was a prominent figure in British ordnance engineering and ballistics. He worked on various projects, including gun design, shell development, and the investigation of metal overstrain. Hadcock also collaborated with Sir George Greenhill on exterior ballistics and authored several papers and tables related to gunnery. He served in various military roles and was a key figure in the British armament industry, working for firms like Sir W. G. Armstrong, Whitworth & Co. and Vickers-Armstrongs.'
