tldr: This article presents experiments conducted by Lord Rayleigh on the electromagnetic rotation of light in bisulfide of carbon. The author measured the rotation of sodium light in the medium and found the rotation to be 0.4202 minutes. The results are significant as they contribute to the understanding of polarimetry and spectroscopy.
