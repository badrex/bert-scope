tldr: This article is a tribute to Lord Adrian, a renowned scientist who made significant contributions to the field of neurophysiology. It highlights his early life, education, and the people who influenced his work. Adrian's groundbreaking research on sense organs, motor control, and the Berger rhythm laid the foundation for modern electroencephalography. He received numerous awards and held important administrative positions in various scientific institutions. His character was marked by friendship, understanding, and kindness.
