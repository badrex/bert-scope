tldr: This article investigates the absorption of solar radiation by atmospheric water vapor, finding that the total absorptive power of the atmosphere is mainly due to the water vapor it contains. The author analyzes data from various stations to understand the relationship between water vapor and absorption, concluding that the quality of solar radiation is subject to daily variations.
