tldr: Roger Pickering discovered the seeds of mushrooms and explained their reproduction process. He found that the gills of mushrooms are actually capsules containing seeds, and that the seeds resemble the farina of flowers. This discovery helped to improve mushroom cultivation techniques and expand our understanding of plant reproduction.
