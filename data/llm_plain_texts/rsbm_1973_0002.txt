tldr: Frederick Charles Bawden was a prominent plant scientist who made significant contributions to plant pathology, particularly in the study of potato viruses. His work led to the establishment of the Potato Virus Research Station in Cambridge, where he conducted groundbreaking research. Bawden's passion for plant sciences was influenced by his early life experiences and education, which shaped his career and dedication to the field.
