tldr: 'Sir Alexander Cruikshank Houston was a leading authority on water supplies and purification. His extensive research on water quality, particularly in relation to lead poisoning and acid water, led to the development of new methods for detecting and measuring sewage pollution. Houston's work in bacteriology and his collaboration with other scientists contributed to the establishment of higher and more scientific standards for drinking water purity.'
