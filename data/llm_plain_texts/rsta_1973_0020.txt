tldr: This publication presents a collection of papers summarizing the current state of knowledge about Mount Etna's structure, geological history, and eruptive behavior. The papers cover topics such as the volcano's magma, lava petrography, and gas chemistry, as well as the physical properties of its eruptive products. The 1971 eruption provided opportunities for detailed research, which may lead to longer-term investigations on Europe's most active volcano.
