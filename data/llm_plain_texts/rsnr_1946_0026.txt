tldr: The International Council of Scientific Unions (ICSU) held a meeting in 1946 at the Royal Society of London, attended by representatives from various scientific academies and unions. The council discussed the reorganization and collaboration of scientific activities, including the formation of new unions and joint commissions, and the potential cooperation with UNESCO. The meeting also addressed the impact of scientific research on society and the responsibility of individual scientists.
