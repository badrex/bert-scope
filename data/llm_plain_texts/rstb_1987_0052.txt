tldr: This article reviews the progress made in understanding the biochemistry and physiology of nitrogenase enzymes, which play a crucial role in nitrogen fixation. It discusses the structure and function of molybdenum nitrogenase and vanadium nitrogenase, as well as the role of Fe proteins and electron transfer pathways in these processes. The article also highlights the challenges and future prospects for nitrogen fixation research.
