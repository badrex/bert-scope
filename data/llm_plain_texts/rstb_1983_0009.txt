tldr: This article discusses the challenges and applications of coenzyme-dependent enzymes in biotechnology. It focuses on the immobilization and stabilization of nicotinamide nucleotide-dependent oxidoreductases, as well as the techniques for coenzyme regeneration. The article also explores the potential uses of enzyme-coenzyme systems in industry, medicine, and analysis, and speculates on future prospects for these systems in biotechnology.
