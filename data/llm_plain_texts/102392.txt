tldr: This article describes the discovery of a fossilized tongue of a flat fish called Pastinaca Marina, found in Jamaica, Maryland, and England. The author, Hans Sloane, compares the fossil to specimens in other collections and discusses the fish's anatomy, noting the intricate arrangement of bones and the unique structure of the tongue. The author also highlights the remarkable design of the fish's mouth, which is well-suited for its feeding habits.
