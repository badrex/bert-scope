tldr: The article is an obituary for Sir Herbert Eustace Maxwell, a Scottish nobleman with diverse interests and achievements. Born into an ancient Border family, Maxwell was a politician, archaeologist, and art enthusiast. He served as a Member of Parliament for Galloway for 26 years and founded the Ayrshire and Galloway Archaeological Association in 1878. His life was marked by a continuous pursuit of self-education and a love for various fields, including natural sciences, history, and art.
