tldr: Sheina Macalister Marshall was a distinguished Scottish marine biologist who made significant contributions to the study of marine production and plankton research. She conducted groundbreaking research on the feeding habits of copepods and the microplankton in the Clyde Sea Area. Her work at Millport laboratory and the Loch Striven survey helped establish the field of marine ecology and paved the way for future research in the field.
