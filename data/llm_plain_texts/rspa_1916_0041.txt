tldr: This obituary honors the life and work of John Henry Poynting, a prominent physicist and educator. Poynting made significant contributions to the understanding of the Earth's mean density through his experiments, and he also researched light pressure and the change of state in substances. In addition to his research, Poynting was an excellent teacher, examiner, and supporter of his students and colleagues.
