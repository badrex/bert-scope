tldr: Marcus Seymour Pembrey was a prominent figure in the field of physiology and medicine. He conducted extensive research on respiration, body temperature regulation, and hibernation in animals. His work contributed to understanding the respiratory exchange, energy production, and the regulation of body temperature in warm-blooded animals. Pembrey also made significant discoveries in clinical cases of Cheyne-Stokes breathing and the effects of exercise on the human body.
