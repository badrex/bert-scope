tldr: The article discusses the history and current functions of the National Physical Laboratory, which was founded in 1900 to establish and maintain standards of physical measurement and encourage the application of physics in industry. The laboratory has evolved over time, with various divisions being created to focus on specific areas of research, such as metallurgy, aerodynamics, and electronics. The National Physical Laboratory continues to play a vital role in supporting industry and advancing scientific knowledge.
