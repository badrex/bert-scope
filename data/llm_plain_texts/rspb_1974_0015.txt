tldr: This article discusses Unilever's approach to assessing the environmental impact of their chemical substances and ensuring consumer safety. The company has a multi-national presence and produces various products, including foods, detergents, and animal feeds. Unilever's environmental policy is set by the board and involves a structured approach to research, development, and testing of products to minimize potential hazards to the environment and consumers. The company also follows national and local policies and works closely with government agencies to meet emission and effluent standards.
