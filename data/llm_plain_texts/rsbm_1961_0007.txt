tldr: The article is a biographical memoir of William Edward Garner, a distinguished chemist known for his contributions to physical chemistry, heterogeneous catalysis, and war-time research on explosives. Garner's work on calorimetry, combustion, and thermochemical measurements had significant impacts on military science and the development of new explosives during both World Wars. His dedication to research and commitment to helping others made him a respected leader in the scientific community.
